It is our understanding that there is a concem regarding the
proposed depth of embedment of the fender piles for the above referenced
project It is felt that the present design embedment would create a
fixed end condition in the soil which would limit the energy absorbing
capacity of the pile
The present 70 foot pile length below top of deck was recommended
to limit the permanent lateral deflection of the pile after repeated
impacts Our calculations indicate that the pile length may be reduced to
60 feet about 20 foot embedment below mudline to lessen the fixed end
condition and still maintain relatively small lateral deflections
However it is our opinion that further reduction in the embedment length
would greatly increase the potential for significant permanent lateral
deflections
