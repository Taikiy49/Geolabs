PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the eastern portion of the Ewa by Gentry
Residential Development Area in the District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The
project site is located east of Fort Weaver Road south of Iroquois Point Road and west of
the HECO easement The project includes a total of 40 residential lots and related access
roads A Project Location Map and a Site Plan are shown on Plates 1 and 2 respectively
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the mass grading only for the project site
MASS GRADING
The mass grading work for the Area 21D Phases 9 to 17 project were observed by
Geolabs Inc from March 25 2002 to During our observation period
the mass grading work have been completed for Phase 9 only Various stockpiles of soil
are situated in Phases 10 and 11 areas The mass grading work consisted of clearing and
grubbing and placement and compaction of fills on the order of about 1 to 4 feet thick to
achieve the design mass grading elevations for the project The mass grading was
performed by RHS Lee Inc During the mass grading operations samples of the on site
and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported
