4 2 Measuring Procedure
1 Selection of pile Test Method
Selection of the appropriate pile Test Method
2 Arrangement of Test Pile Head
The length of pile above ground level should be about D diameter of pile The
pile head must be vertical
3 Drilling of hole for gage attachment
Using a drill a hole for the gage installation attachment will be drilled at 180 interval at
a distance of 1 5D from the pile head
4 Gage installation
The strain transducer and accelerometer must be installed carefully on the drilled holes
using high strength bolts
5 Input of initial value
Project information operation condition and initial values pile properties pile penetration
depth gage location etc must be in coded in pile driving analyzer and prepare for the
monitoring
6 Pile Driving Preparation
The hammer must drop exactly on the pile The purpose is to avoid eccentric loading
during pile driving
7 Gage Examination
The gage cable must be connected to the pile driving analyzer and gage test must be
performed to make sure that the gage is in order
8 If the gages are in order performing two times initial driving and record the initial values
9 Analysis
After the initial values are recorded perform several driving The obtained acceleration
and strain data are analyzed using the case method in the field After the values are
recorded it is analyzed using CAPWAP method and the data are arranged
The dynamic pile load test result sheets are attached in Appendix D
