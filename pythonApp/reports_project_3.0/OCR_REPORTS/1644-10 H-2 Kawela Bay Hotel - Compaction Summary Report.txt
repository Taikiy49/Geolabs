As requested we have performed observation and testing of the mass grading
operations at the above referenced project site from to October 1990
Our services were performed in accordance with our proposal dated March 3 1990
The grading for the development was performed in accordance with the
recommendations presented in our above referenced Geotechnical Report dated June 5
1989
The project site was graded for the development of a highrise hotel structure The
grading entailed the removal of deleterious surface materials clearing and grubbing
stabilization of the soft ground swamp area and placement of between five 5 to ten
10 feet of structural fill Intermittent site visits were made by representatives from our
office during the grading operations to see that our recommendations and specifications
pertinent to the project were adhered to
Subsequent to clearing and grubbing the low lying swamp area in the central
portion of the site was stabilized by the placement of a geotextile fabric and a minimum
of eighteen 18 inches of 3B fine gravel Import structural fills were then placed in
controlled lift heights and compacted with a sheepsfoot roller
