FINAL COMPACTION REPORT
WAIPAHU KNOLLS I
From to the present we have inspected the
earthwork and grading operations performed at the above
referenced site This report summarizes our inspection and
compaction test results
The grading operations at the above referenced lots consisted
of fills generally less than 5 feet thick on Lots to
33 fills up to 12 feet thick on Lots 24 to 28 and cuts up to 8
feet deep on the remaining lots In the buried gully area Lots
17 through 20 and 24 through 28 the loose old fill was removed
and replaced with compacted on site fill During the fill
placement operations periodic field density tests were done in
accordance with the American Society for Testing and Materials
ASTM Test Designation D 1556 Sand Cone Method

The compaction test results are summarized at the end of the
report Based on the compaction tests and visual observations
it is our opinion that the fill lots have been compacted
adequately
The soils encountered at the cut pad finished grade generally
consisted of very stiff reddish brown clayey silt Field obser
vations indicate that these soils are stiff to hard in consis
tency and can provide adequate bearing support for the proposed
single family residences
Laboratory ring swell tests were performed on samples of the
soils at the lot finished grade and generally indicated that the
soils have low swelling potential if they are maintained near the
optimum moisture content

Twelve inch deep perimeter footings below the lowest
