EARTHWORK OBSERVATION AND COMPACTION TESTING REPORT
ROYAL KUNIA SITE 4
Dear Mr Lau
This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during site grading and roadway and utility construction for the Royal
Kunia Site 4 development from May 21 1999 to in accordance with our
fee proposal dated
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the southwestern portion of the Royal Kunia
Development Area in the District of Kunia on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site
is bounded by Kunia Road on the west a drainageway on the north Kupuohi Street on the
east and Wal Mart building on the south The location of the project site is shown on the
Site Plan Plate 1 The project site includes a total of 86 residential lots and five roads The
site grading work consisted of mostly cuts less than 10 feet in thickness Fills up to about 45
feet in thickness were required near the existing drainageway at the northeast corner of the
site
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The site grading and utility installation work for the Royal Kunia Site 4 project was
observed by Geolabs Inc from May 21 1999 to The site grading work
consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills up to about 45 feet
in thickness to achieve the design finished grades for the project The site grading and utility
backfilling work were performed by Royal Contracting Company Ltd During the site grading
operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials
were obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum dry densities and
optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with
ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on
Plate 2

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and
backfill materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge
Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 3 1 through
3 16 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should
be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed
to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility installation work for the Royal
Kunia Site 4 project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report
were performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the
following references
