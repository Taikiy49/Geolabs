In accordance with our service agreement we have been providing geotechnical
engineering services during the earthwork and pavement construction for the Kalamaula
Residence Lots Unit 1 project located in the Kalamaula area on the Island of Molokai
Hawaii This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observation and testing
services performed during the earthwork operations for the period between July 1998 and
February 2000 In general our services were coordinated with the contractor The
contractor for the project was
SITE GRADING
The Kalamaula Residence Lots project site is situated east of the town of Kaunakakai
on the Island of Molokai Hawaii The site was developed for residential use by the
Department of Hawaiian Home Lands DHHL The project site involves the construction of
about 123 single family residential lots ranging in size from about 0 5 to 1 acre each with
associated infrastructure such as water lines drain lines drainage structures and roadways
The lots are turned over on an as is basis with the lessees being responsible for individual
lot grading and installation of individual wastewater systems
In general the earthwork at the site consisted of clearing and grubbing placement
and compaction of fills trench excavation and backfill and subgrade preparation and
compaction for the pavement construction Following the clearing and grubbing operations
the exposed subgrades were generally scarified brought to near the optimum moisture

In general the majority of the materials used as fills and backfills consisted of the
excavated on site materials During the site grading operations samples of the on site and
imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and tested The
maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in general
accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are
summarized on Plate 2 of this report
In general field density tests were performed on the fills and backfills with the nuclear
density gauge ASTM Test Designation D 2922 During the fill and backfill operations the
materials were generally placed in near level lifts brought to near the optimum moisture and
compacted to obtain the specified degree of compaction Periodic field density tests were

from on site survey stakes where available These locations and elevations should be
considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our professional opinion that the earthwork operations for the
Kalamaula Residence Lots Unit 1 project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period
covered by this report were performed in general conformance with the intent of the plans
and specifications and our recommendations In general areas assessed to be
unsatisfactory were re worked to achieve the specified minimum compaction
LIMITATIONS
