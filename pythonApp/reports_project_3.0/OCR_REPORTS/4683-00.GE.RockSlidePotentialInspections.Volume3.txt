APPENDIX B
Island of Oahu Key Map Area Maps and Site Plans
This Volume No 3 book is provided as a supplemental reference for the
Rockslide Potential Inspections project for the City and County of Honolulu This book
contains the various maps and site plans that show the general location of hillslopes
that were evaluated for this preliminary hillslope inventory and rating study
The base map for the Island of Oahu and data from selected Geographic
Information System GIS were obtained from publicly available GIS records The GIS
information is owned and maintained by the City and County of Honolulu Selected GIS
information was obtained from the City and County of Honolulu database in July 2001
and converted into an AutoCADD 2000 format so that the basic geographic and
cartographic information could be edited for a presentation that is appropriate for this
report As such the land division information contained in the database is believed to
be current as of about July 2001
The GIS data for the Island of Oahu that was utilized from the City and County of
Honolulu GIS database include the following information
e Island of Oahu base map outline
e 20 foot interval elevation contours where available
e Street and Highway layout with associated text
e Tax Map Key and Parcel layout and associated text
The basic GIS information was utilized to prepare general location maps and site
plans that show the approximate location of the hillslope sites that were evaluated for
this study Based on our knowledge of the potential inconsistencies present in the
publicly available GIS information it must be noted that the maps and site plans
prepared for this study may contain some inaccuracies in the geographic position of

Key Map
The purpose of the Island of Oahu Key Map is to show on a single map sheet the
overall regional spread of rock hillslope sites that are addressed by this study The map
provides a graphic depiction of the relative density of rock hillslope sites which are
located adjacent to City streets In addition the Island of Oahu Key Map shows the
boundaries of the neighborhood divisions which were utilized to prepare the Area Maps
discussed below
Area Maps
The purpose of the Area Maps is to show the relative location and site
identification Slope Identification No of the rock hillslope sites for a given

Site Plans
The purpose of the Site Plans is to graphically show the approximate location of
the rock hillslopes with respect to existing street and tax map parcel information
Information shown on the site plans is based on field reconnaissance and observation
of the existing field conditions The general information shown on the site plan is not
intended to take the place of a detailed ground survey In most cases the location of
the hillslope sites is based on field tape measurements referenced from property
boundaries shown on the Tax Map Keys
The Site Plan may also be used to identify the Tax Map Key number for parcels
that abut the rock hillslope Ownership of the parcels that abut the rock hillslope may be
identified through research of the current Tax Map Key records It should be noted that
the responsibility for mitigative measures to address the potential slope instability will
reside with the respective property owner
