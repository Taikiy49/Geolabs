Ground Penetrating Radar Survey
819 Moowaa Street
Honolulu Oahu Hawaii
A Ground Penetrating Radar GPR survey was conducted on January 31 in an
attempt to locate the boundaries of a suspected underground storage tank within the
premises of 819 Moowaa Street in Honolulu on the island of Oahu Hawaii The survey
was conducted inside the office building and comprised of an L shaped open area of
about 450 square feet
The survey was conducted on the exposed concrete floor slab with a 500 MHz
antenna assuming clayey subsurface materials The area where the tank was suspected
to exist was surveyed in a grid pattern using variable instrument settings to achieve
variable depths of penetration and resolution
Based on the results of the GPR survey there were no significant anomalies
detected within the surveyed area Therefore we believe that the tank may have been
previously removed from the site if it existed at that location Definitive indications of a
buried tank were not revealed by the GPR survey Based on the weak radar anomaly
encountered it appears as though the location where the tank was suspected to exist
may be underlain by backfill materials which may be different from the surrounding earth
subgrade materials
