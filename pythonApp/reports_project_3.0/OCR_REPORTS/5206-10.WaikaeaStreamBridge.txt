Dear Mr Ikemori
In accordance with your request and the project specifications a static load test
was conducted on a sacrificial pile for Waikaea Stream Bridge Kapaa Kealia Bike
Pedestrian Path project The project site is at the existing bridge structure crossing
Waikaea Stream Bridge in the Kapaa area on the Island of Kauai Hawaii This letter report
presents the results of our field observations and the test data collected during the static
pile load test conducted on for the above project This letter report
presents the results of our field observations and measurements taken during the static
pile load test program
The static load test was conducted on a sacrificial pile designated as Pile No TP 1
The test pile is near Abutment No 2 at the northern portion of the bridge site The static
pile load test was conducted on a 16 5 inch octagonal precast prestressed concrete pile
The pile was driven on using a Diesel hammer During
the pile driving operations of the test pile a log of the blow counts needed to advance the
pile was maintained by our engineer The pile driving record is provided on Plate 1 for
ease of reference
The sacrificial pile was loaded in general accordance with ASTM D 1143 Quick
Load Test Method A hydraulic jack system reacting against a weighted platform load
frame was used to apply load to the pile A load cell was used to confirm the loads
applied to the pile
The pile settlement was measured with two dial gauges located at two sides of
the pile The dial gauges were referred to as Front and Back of the load test pile The
measuring needle of each dial gauge was set vertically on a fixed horizontal plate on
the top of the load test pile As the load test pile moved downward the dial gauge
readings decreased The secondary method to measure the pile settlement consisted of
a 1 100 inch division ruler set on the pile head with a surveyor s level to observe the pile
settlement

In addition two 1 100 inch division rulers were set on each side of the load frame
and a remote location on a reference stick The remote location was set outside the
immediate test area and area of potential settlement The surveyor s level was used to
observe movements of the above mentioned locations
In general the load test pile was loaded to a maximum test load of 270 kips in
16 9 kip load increments Each load increment was held for at least 5 minutes After
holding the maximum test load of 270 kips for 60 minutes the test load was unloaded in
approximately four equal decrements and held for at least 5 minutes at each decrement
