In accordance with your facsimile request dated December 11th we have
reviewed and performed additional engineering analyses for MSERW No 7 at the
subject site The additional engineering analyses consisted of evaluating the global
stability and settlement of the MSERW No 7 structure The additional engineering
analyses are necessary due to the heavier select granular backfill material average
unit weight of 145 p c f used at the site for the MSERW
Based on our engineering analyses the global stability of MSERW No 7
appears to meet the minimum requirements established by Federal Highway
Administration FHWA design guidelines The global stability analyses conducted for
the cross sections at and generally indicate factors of safety
greater than 1 5 for short term and long term stabilities In addition the calculated
settlements of the MSERW structure at is estimated to be approximately 11
inches based on an average unit weight of 145 p c f for the select granular backfill
