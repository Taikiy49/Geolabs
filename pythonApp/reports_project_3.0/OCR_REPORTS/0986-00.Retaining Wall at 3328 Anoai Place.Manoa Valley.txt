RECOMMENDATIONS
Based on the limited field observations and shallow hand
probings the following recommendations are presented
1 The soils encountered in the existing dug wall footing
excavation were loose to medium density We recommend
that the footing excavation should be overexcavated an

LIMITATIONS
Our scope of work on this project is restricted to the design
recommendations of the replacement retaining wall as discussed
above We have not been involved with the original post
footing design for the main house structure nor have we been
hired to verify if the existing footings supporting the exis
ting residence are adequate It is our understanding that
this house has been constructed for over 15 years with no
noticeable signs of distress or movements It should be
understood by all parties that the new house addition will
have to be supported by the existing footing foundations as
well as the new replacement retaining wall We have assumed
that the existing footing foundation are stable and structurally
adequate to support additional loads fromthe proposed addition
or improvements
