Project Considerations
The Kauai Lagoons Resort project site is within the existing Marriott Kauai Lagoons
Resort in the Nawiliwili area of Lihue on the Island of Kauai Hawaii The Subdivision 1
and Bypass Road portion of the project generally consisted of site grading for
10 residential lots and a roadway from Hoolaulea Way to Kalanipu u development A
General Site Plan is provided on Plate 1
Our observation and testing services during the site work construction of the
project occurred between September 2008 and December 2009 Earthworks Pacific Inc
was the site work contractor for the project

Discussion and Recommendations
Earthwork at the Subdivision 1 and Bypass Road site generally consisted of cuts
of up to about 3 to 4 feet thick Some minor fills of less than about 2 to 3 feet were
required in localized areas A Site Plan is provided on Plate 2
Fill and backfill materials generally consisted of excavated on site materials In
general fill materials consisted of materials less than 3 inches in largest dimension
Samples of the on site and imported materials were obtained and transported to our
office for testing where appropriate The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture
contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557
Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were generally moisture conditioned to above
optimum moisture content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum
dry density as determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 Compaction requirement
was generally increased to a minimum of 95 percent of the maximum dry density for the
last lift of fills under pavements
Subgrades in the cut areas generally were scarified and recompacted to a
minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as determined in accordance with
ASTM D 1557
During the earthwork operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM D 6938 Nuclear Gauge Method where appropriate The
compaction test results are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 20 presented herein
These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and
backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and
interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be
considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to
be unsatisfactory were generally reworked until the specified minimum compaction was
obtained and or accepted by our field representative based on visual observation and
hand probing
Based on the observations of our field representatives it is our professional
opinion that the site work operations for the above mentioned site observed by Geolabs
in the period covered in this report were performed in general conformance with the intent
of the project plans and_ specifications and our geotechnical engineering
recommendations presented in the referenced reports and subsequent
recommendations
It should be noted the above statement of professional opinion is based on the
grades existing at the completion of the site work operations of the project in

Limitations
It should be noted that it is not possible to continuously observe all activities
occurring simultaneously on a construction site Therefore some of the earthwork may
have been performed without our observation However the level of earthwork
observation and testing services provided appears to be reasonable in reducing some of
the geotechnical risks to the project It should be recognized that there are variations in the
accuracy and statistical repeatability of the tests and methods used to monitor the
earthwork construction and materials Our services were performed in accordance with
generally accepted standards of geotechnical engineering practice no warranty is
expressed or implied Provision of construction monitoring by an engineer is not insurance
nor does it guarantee construction of any type The contractor shall retain responsibility for
the quality of the work for adhering to the plans and specifications and for repairing
defects regardless of when they are found
