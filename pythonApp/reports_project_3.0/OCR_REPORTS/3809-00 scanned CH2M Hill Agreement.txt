1 0 Introduction
CH2M HILL has been contracted by the Air Force Center for Environmental Excellence AFCEE to conduct an
Engineering Evaluation Cost Analysis EE CA at eight Installation Restoration Program IRP sites in the
vicinity of Runway 8L Hickam Air Force Base Oahu Hawaii As part of this project CH2M HILL is requesting
cost proposals for monitoring well drilling and installation at two IRP sites EA11 and LF14 shown on Figures 1
proposal
2 0 Statement of Work

operations This document will be incorporated into the site specific Health and Safety Plan HSP as an
addendum
Conduct all work in accordance with the site specific HSP A copy of the HSP is available on request
The work is expected to be done in Level D conditions However upgrade to Level C may be necessary if site
conditions warrant

Advance six 10 inch diameter boreholes according to Schedule 1 at the locations specified by CH2M HILL
approximate drilling locations are shown on Figure 1
Obtain continuous core soil samples during advancement of one 50 foot borehole
Obtain soil samples at 5 foot intervals during advancement of the other five boreholes Obtain up to 10 soil

Obtain up to 40 feet of continuous core soil samples in addition to the 50 foot borehole specified above during
borehole advancement as requested by CH2M HILL
1 Soil samples continuous core or at interval will be obtained by driving a split barrel sampler
ahead of the auger bit Soil samplers will be removed from the auger immediately after soil
sample is obtained or maximum blow counts are reached Overdrilling of soil samplers is not
permitted
2 The number of hammer blows with 30 inch drop required to advance the sampler six inches will
be counted by the driller Three sets of 6 inch drives will be attempted for each sampler unless the
maximum blow count is reached The maximum blow count is 50 hammer blows 70 hammer
blows if 140 pound hammer is used per 6 inch attempt The driller will report the results of each
sampling attempt to the CH2M HILL field representative
Install and develop one 2 inch diameter monitoring well in each borehole according to the specifications in
Figure 2 and Schedule 1 Well development will consist of surging the well with a surge block and bailing
water and fines passing the well screen In addition to the specifications in Figure 3 the following requirements
apply to all wells
1 Place filter pack with 1 inch tremie pipe
2 Surge filter pack twice for a total of 15 minutes prior placement of bentonite seal to settle the filter
pack materials
3 Mix grout in the following proportions 94 pounds Type I Portland cement not more than 4
pounds 100 percent Sodium bentonite grout powder not more than 8 gallons potable water
4 Place grout with 14 inch tremie pipe
5 Record volumes of all annular materials placed
2 4 Decontamination
Decontaminate all drill string tools that directly contact subsurface soils prior to breaking ground at each
monitoring well location by steam cleaning tools over a containment trailer
Decontaminate all soil sampler parts and soil sampler liners prior to each sample interval by scraping off any
residual soil washing in a solution of Liquinox or other suitable detergent and tap water followed by three
serial rinses with clean tap water The final rinse water will not contain visible turbidity at any time Final rinse
water will be changed at the first sign of turbidity carry over from previous washes

Unsafe Conditions CH2M HILL Form 309 or a Nonconformance Report CH2M HILL Form 316
