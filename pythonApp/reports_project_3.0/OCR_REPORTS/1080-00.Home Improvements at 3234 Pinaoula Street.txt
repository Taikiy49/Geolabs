Field Observations
At the time of our inspection none of the retaining walls
Or concrete pavements indicated any signs of distress due
to movement resulting from sliding or heaving of the soil
One hairline crack was noted in the Diamond Head Makai
corner of the concrete sidewalk which appears to be the

DISCUSSIONS AND RECOMMENDATIONS
It is our opinion that the site is suitable for the proposed
home improvement work and that the subsurface soils will pro
vide adequate support for the existing home and future improve
ments
Foundations
Spread footings should be used for the proposed bearing walls
in the basement area Spread and continuous footings should
be embedded a minimum of 12 inches below the adjacent finish
grade

Seepage Disposal
