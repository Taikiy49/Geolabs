Dear Mr. Michael: 
This amendment consists of revised micropile design recommendations for the 
proposed high-rise structure foundations for the above project. The overall length of the 
micropile foundation has been increased to 50 feet which includes 23 feet of permanent 
casing (unbonded zone) and 27 feet of bonded length. The remaining micropile 
recommendations, including the allowable capacities, installation procedure, and testing 
procedure, will remain unchanged. 
The “Cased Micropile System Recommendations” table on page 16 of our report 
entitled “Geotechnical Engineering Exploration, Halawa View Apartments, Phases 2, 3, 
and 4, Aiea, Oahu, Hawaii,” dated May 21, 2021 has been revised and is presented 
below.
