The following sheets contain revised design parameters for the proposed braced
shoring system for the new 54 RCP drain line excavation Included in the following
sheets are an Apparent Pressure Diagram 1 sheet Design Parameter Calculations 2
sheets Boring Location Map 1 sheet and a Boring Log by Ernest K Hirata
Associates Inc 2 sheets
Based on the information provided to us we understand that the invert of the
new drain line will be about 30 to 35 feet below the existing ground surface We also
understand that a bench of about 8 to 12 feet deep will be excavated to reduce the
length of sheet piles needed for driving The bench will extend approximately 30 feet
laterally from the trench on both sides to provide ample space for excavation
operations The pressure distribution on the Apparent Pressure Diagram includes
surcharge loads resulting from a 9060 Excavator or a similar machine
Based on Boring B 24 drilled by Ernest K Hirata and Associates Inc on
in the vicinity of the site indicates that the area is underlain by about 5
feet of fill consisting of stiff silty clay with gravel The fill is generally underlain by
medium stiff to stiff clayey silt with traces of weathered rock
