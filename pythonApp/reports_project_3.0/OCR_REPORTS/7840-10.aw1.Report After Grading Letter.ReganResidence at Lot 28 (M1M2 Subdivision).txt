This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observation and testing 
services performed during the site grading of the Regan Residence, Kukui’ula Lot 28 
project from November 26, 2018 through May 26, 2020 in accordance with our fee 
proposal, dated December 5, 2018.  
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS 
The project involves a planned single-family residence on Lot 28 within Kukui’ula 
Development Company’s M1/M2 Subdivision in Koloa on the Island of Kauai, Hawaii. 
This residential project consists of a two-story house with an attached garage structure, 
swimming pool, and other related site improvements. A Project Location Map and Site 
Plan are shown on Plates 1 and 2, respectively. 
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services 
performed during the site grading at the project site. 
SITE GRADING 
We observed the site grading work for the project from November 26, 2018 through 
May 26, 2020. Site grading for the project generally consisted of excavations up to about 8 
feet deep for building/retaining wall foundations and swimming pool construction, and fills 
and backfills up to about 6 feet thick to achieve the finished design grades.  
At the on-set of earthwork, the area within the limits of grading was cleared and 
grubbed of unsuitable surface materials. Greenwood Homes was the General Contractor 
for the project and JK Excavation LLC was the subcontractor who performed the site

minimum compaction were obtained or exceeded.

grading operations.
