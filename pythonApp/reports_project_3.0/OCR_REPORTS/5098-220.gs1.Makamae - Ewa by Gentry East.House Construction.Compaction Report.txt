PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located east of Fort Weaver Road in the Ewa area on the
Island of Oahu Hawaii The planned development consists of about 150 single family
residences The site development during house construction consisted of house pads
lanais garages driveways and retaining walls In addition electrical and plumbing
lines were installed A Project Location Map and Site Plan are shown on Plates 1 and 2
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for
the house grading and utility installation at the project site

HOUSE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
We observed the house grading and utility installation work for the Makamae Ewa
by Gentry East project from to The house grading work
consisted of footing excavation and footing and slabs on grade subgrade preparation for
the project The utility installation work consisted of trench backfill of plumbing and sewer
lines within the house lot Dura Construction performed the house grading and utility
installation work During the house grading operations we obtained samples of the on site
and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials and transported them to our
laboratory for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were
established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D1557 Modified Proctor
and the test results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above the optimum
moisture content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density
as determined in accordance with ASTM D1557
During the house grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density
tests were performed in general accordance with ASTM D6938 Nuclear Gauge Method
Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 19 of
this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should
be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed
as unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade
soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the house grading and utility installation work for the
above referenced project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this
report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in
the following references
1
Chapter 14 of the Revised Ordinances of Honolulu 1990 as amended
2
