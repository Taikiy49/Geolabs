PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located in the Hilton Hawaiian Village Resort Complex on the
Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is located west of the Tapa Tower swimming pool
and north of an existing retail utility building A landscaped area including a pond and
waterfall walkways and a gazebo structure occupies the new wedding chapel building
site The location of the project site is shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1 and the
new wedding chapel is shown on the Site Plan Plate 2
We understand the new wedding chapel building will consist of a two story
structure The lower floor level will be constructed mainly of masonry units and the upper
floor level constructed of a steel framed structure The chapel building will be
approximately 50 feet by 75 feet in plan dimension In addition a new gazebo structure
approximately 14 feet by 14 feet in plan dimension is planned north of the chapel building
We understand the majority of the site grading work will be in the southern portion of the
site where cuts of up to about 14 feet in height is planned
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and utility installation for the project site

grading and utility backfilling work was performed by R H S Lee Inc During the utility
backfill operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as backfill
materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum dry
densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general
accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on
Plate 3
The backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557
During the utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests were performed
in general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the
compaction tests performed are summarized on Plate 4 of this report These tests were
performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed backfills at the site Test
locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey
stakes These locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree
implied by the methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until
the specified minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field
representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion the site grading and utility installation work for the New
Wedding Chapel at Hilton Hawaiian Village project observed and tested by Geolabs in the
period covered by this report were performed in conformance with the general intent of
