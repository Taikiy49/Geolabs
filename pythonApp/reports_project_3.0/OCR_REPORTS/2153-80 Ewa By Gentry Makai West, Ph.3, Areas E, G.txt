PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the western portion of the Ewa by Gentry Residential
Development Area in the District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The Phase 3 project
site is bounded by Barbers Point Naval Air Station on the east North South Road on the
west and Geiger Road on the north The Phase 3 project is comprised of Areas A through
G Areas E and G are located at the southern portion of the Phase 3 site The location of
the project site is shown on the Site Plan Plate 1
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the mass grading operations performed at Areas E and G of the Phase 3 project
site

The mass grading work for the Phase 3 Areas E and G project was observed by
Geolabs Inc from May 16 2000 to The mass grading work consisted of
placement and compaction of fills on the order of about 2 to 3 feet thick to achieve the design
finished grades for the project The mass grading work was performed by RHS Lee Inc
Much of the fills needed at the site to raise the pre existing grades to the design
mass grading grades generally consisted of fill materials that were generated from the Kaloi
Drainage Channel excavation and existing on site soil stockpiles During the mass grading
operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill materials were
obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum
moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test
Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor test procedures and the test resuits are summarized
on Plate 2
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 test procedures
During the mass grading operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method test
procedures Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 3 1
through 3 5 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and
on constructed fills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and
interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be
considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to be
unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained and or
approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and also the evaluation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the mass grading work for the above referenced
project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were performed
in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the following references
