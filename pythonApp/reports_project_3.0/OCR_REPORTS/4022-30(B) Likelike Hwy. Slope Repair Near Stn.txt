Slope Repair Near Station No 106 00
Likelike Highway Outbound Embankment Slope
Honolulu Oahu Hawaii
Dear Dr Ding
This letter provides slope repair recommendations for the Likelike Highway
outbound embankment slope near Station No 106 00 Our recommendations are based
on a site visit made on and topographic survey information received
on
Two shallow slope erosion areas on the outbound Likelike Highway embankment
near Sta No 106 00 were observed during a site visit on Slope
erosion of up to about 3 to 4 feet deep was observed near the top of the embankment
The slope erosion areas were located within a relatively low lying area on Likelike
Highway An existing drainage inlet was observed up station and at a higher elevation
than the roadway adjacent to the slope erosion areas In addition another drainage inlet
was observed down station at a slightly lower elevation than the roadway adjacent to the
slope erosion areas The inlets were partially blocked with debris
Based on the above observations it appears that the slope erosion was caused by
surface runoff water from the recent heavy rains that flowed on Likelike Highway and onto
the slope face During the site visit it was recommended that the drainage inlets be
cleaned of the debris a berm consisting of sand bags be placed along the roadway edge
to divert surface water away from the slope and into the existing drains and that the slope
be monitored for movement The above recommendations are short term remedial
measures that should be implemented until a more permanent solution can be determined
after a topographic survey of the existing condition can be performed
The recent topographic survey of the slope erosion areas is shown on Plate 1
Based on the recent topographic survey of the eroded areas it is recommended that the
eroded slope areas be reconstructed with compacted select fill material as shown on
Plates 2 and 3 The select fill material should be well graded granular material with no
particles larger than 3 inches in largest dimension and should contain between 10 to
30 percent particles passing the No 200 sieve The fills should be moisture conditioned to
about 2 percent above the optimum moisture placed in level lifts not exceeding 10 inches
in loose thickness and compacted to at least 90 percent relative compaction Fills should
