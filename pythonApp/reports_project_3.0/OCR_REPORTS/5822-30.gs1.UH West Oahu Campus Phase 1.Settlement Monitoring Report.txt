PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The University of Hawaii West Oahu Campus covers approximately 103 5 acres
and is south and east of Farrington Highway and west of North South Road in the
District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii New roadways are planned from
North South Road across Kaloi Gulch to the new campus The project location and
general vicinity are shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
Deep fills were placed within Kaloi Gulch for the future Road B and for the
future Campus Loop Road Road D Based on the grading plans fills up to about
22 feet were placed within Kaloi Gulch
Because of deep fills within the gulch crossings and the potential for differential
settlement a settlement monitoring program was recommended for the deep fill areas
along the roadways crossing the existing gulch
SETTLEMENT MONITORING PROGRAM
Settlement monitoring points were installed subsequent to fill placement within
Kaloi Gulch for Road B and Road D The settlement monitoring points consisted of
0 5 inch diameter steel rods about 3 foot long driven into the existing ground surface
