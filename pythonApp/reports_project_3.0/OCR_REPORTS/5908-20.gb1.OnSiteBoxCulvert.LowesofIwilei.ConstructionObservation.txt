PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is in the lwilei area of Honolulu on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The
on site portion 13 010 of the project is bounded by Home Depot to the north
Pacific Street to the east and Nimitz Highway to the south and west
A new Lowe s 117k square building prototype with a garden center was
constructed As part of the project a new storm drain box culvert was constructed through
the project site The new storm drain box culvert is located on the south and southeast
sides of the site The storm drain box culvert is about 764 feet long The upstream end of
the storm drain box culvert is located at the Pacific Street side of the site and the
downstream end at Nimitz Highway side of the site

EARTHWORK CONSTRUCTION
Geolabs Inc observed the earthwork construction for the on site storm drain box
culvert at the Lowe s of Iwilei project The earthwork construction generally consisted of
excavation along the storm drain box culvert alignment placement of the stabilization layer
below the storm drain box culvert and placement and compaction of fills along the
sidewalls and above the storm drain box culvert Robinson Construction Co and Bobby K
Construction performed the on site earthwork operations for the storm drain box culvert
The stabilization layer for the storm drain box culvert consisted of placing a
minimum 24 inch thick layer of No 3 Fine gravel wrapped in a non woven filter fabric
After construction of the storm drain box culvert the open area between the excavation
sidewalls and the outside walls of the storm drain box culvert were backfilled with
No 3 Fine gravel up to a minimum of 12 inches above the groundwater level The
remainder of the excavation was backfilled with on site or imported backfill materials
compacted to a minimum of 90 percent relative compaction The backfill materials within
3 feet of the pavement finish grade were compacted to a minimum of 95 percent relative
compaction
During the storm drain box culvert earthwork operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 6938 Nuclear Gauge Method The
compaction test results are summarized in our report entitled Earthwork Observation and
Compaction Testing Report Lowe s of Iwilei On Site Honolulu Oahu Hawaii Grading
Permit No 0171 dated Test locations and elevations were
estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and
elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used
Areas assessed as unsatisfactory were generally reworked until the specified minimum
compaction was obtained
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s stabilization
layer placement and backfill placement and compaction procedures and confirmation of
storm drain box culvert subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above earthwork
excavation operations
Based on our field representatives observations and the referenced test results it
