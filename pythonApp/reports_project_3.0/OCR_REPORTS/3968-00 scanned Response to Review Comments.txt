Dear Mr Yoshizaki
In response to the review comments presented in your transmittal letter dated
July 9 we are providing the following additional information for your consideration
Old Pavement Fabric
It is our opinion that the contractors bidding on this project should be made aware
of the potential for encountering old pavement fabric on Liliha Street Old
pavement fabric encountered during the cold planing operation should be
completely removed The cold planing thickness in these areas should be
increased to provide a minimum AC overlay thickness of 3 inches After
preparation of the cold planed subgrade a leveling course consisting of 1 5 inches
of AC overlay should be placed prior to the installation of the new pavement fabric
The resurfacing operation should then be completed with the placement of the
remaining 1 5 inch AC overlay Contract documents should include additive and
deductive unit prices for removal and replacement of the pavement fabric
Street Car Track
Based on the additional information provided we understand that an old street car
track previously existed along the middle portion approximately 6 feet wide of
Liliha Street We understand that the street car track has subsequently been
removed and that the existing pavement generally consists of asphalt concrete
over macadam base and undocumented fills overlying the in situ clayey soils
Based on our pavement analyses an AC overlay of 8 5 inches would be required
for this portion of the roadway Therefore it is our opinion that the existing
pavement section is not adequate for the poor subgrade soil conditions
