Project Considerations
The project site is located at the western end of the Ewa by Gentry West
development just south of Geiger Road and west of the Coral Creek Golf Club in Ewa
on the Island of Oahu Hawaii Based on the information provided the Gentry Business
Park project generally consists of 60 industrial lots roughly 4 000 linear feet of roadway
and other related infrastructure such as drainage sewer water and other utilities We

We propose to conduct an exploration program consisting of seven borings
extending to a depth of about 20 feet below the existing ground surface using a
truck mounted drill rig Soil samples will be collected using Modified California and
standard split spoon samplers In addition bulk samples of the near surface soils will be
collected for CBR analyses Based on our experience in the project vicinity we envision
the project site may be underlain by surface fill materials clayey alluvial soils and coral
formation
Purpose and Scope
The purpose of our geotechnical engineering exploration is to obtain an overview
of the surface and subsurface condition at the project site to assist in the design of the
project To accomplish these objectives we propose the following tasks and work
efforts during this phase of the project
1 Research and review of available in house boring data and other
subsurface information in the project vicinity
2 Staking out of boring locations and coordination of underground utility line
clearance
3 Mobilization and demobilization of a truck mounted drill rig water truck
and two operators to the project site and back
4 Drilling and sampling of seven borings extending to a depth of about
20 feet below the existing ground surface In addition collection of bulk
samples for CBR analyses
5 Coordination of the field exploration and logging of the borings by our
engineer geologist
6 Laboratory testing of selected soil samples obtained during the field
exploration as an aid in classifying the materials and evaluating their
engineering properties
7 Analyses of the field and laboratory data to formulate preliminary
geotechnical recommendations pertaining to earthwork foundation
slabs on grade and pavements
8 Preparation of a formal report summarizing our work on the project and
presenting our findings and recommendations
9 Coordination of our overall work on the project by our senior engineer
