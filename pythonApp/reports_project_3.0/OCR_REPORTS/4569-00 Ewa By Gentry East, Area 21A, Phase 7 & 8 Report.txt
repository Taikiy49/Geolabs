PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the eastern portion of the Ewa by Gentry Residential
Development Area in the District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is
bounded by Iroquois Point Road on the north and is located about 700 feet east of Keaunui
Drive as shown on the attached Site Plan Plate 1 The project site encompasses an area of
approximately 1 4 acres and includes a total of 15 residential lots and one roadway
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services pertaining
to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The site grading and utility installation work for the Area 21A Phases 7 8 project were
observed by Geolabs Inc from to The site grading work
consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills on the order of
about 2 feet thick to achieve the design finished grades for the project The site grading and
utility installation work was performed by Delta Construction Corporation During the site
_grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill
materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum dry densities

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above the optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and
backfill materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests were
performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge
Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 3 1 through
3 6 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should
be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to
be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained and or
approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the referenced
tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility installation work for the above referenced
project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were performed
