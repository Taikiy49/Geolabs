PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project involves the construction of a new PETCO store and retail outlets next
to the existing Walgreens store along Kamehameha Highway between Lilipuna Road and
Mehana Street in Kaneohe on the Island of Oahu Hawaii In addition the project
includes the construction of driveways and parking stalls as shown on the Site Plan
Plate 1
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for
the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING
The site grading operations consisted of clearing and grubbing excavation for
footings placement and compaction of select granular capping fill under footings
slab on grade and pavement areas Select non expansive granular borrow materials
imported from Kapaa Quarry was used as capping fill up to about 1 5 feet below finished
grade At the onset of grading the area was cleared and grubbed prior to excavating the
wall and column footings Arita Poulson General Contracting LLC the general contractor
for the project performed the site grading work Ameron Hawaii the materials supplier
provided the select borrow structural capping fill The product data showing the gradation

During the site grading operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM D 6938 Nuclear Gauge Method Compaction test results
are summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 4 These tests were performed on the
constructed fills and backfills The test locations are identified by their relative position to
the grid lines as shown in the plan and based on their relative location to the new PETCO
building footprint Areas that were assessed as unsatisfactory were recompacted until the
specified minimum compaction was attained
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s subgrade
preparation fill placement and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on our partial field observations and the referenced test results it is our
opinion that the site grading work for the PETCO Store project in Kaneohe as observed
and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report was performed in conformance
with the general intent of the recommendations in our report entitled Geotechnical
Engineering Services New PETCO Store Kaneohe Oahu

LIMITATIONS
It should be noted that it is not possible to continuously observe all activities
occurring simultaneously on a construction site therefore some work may have occurred
without our observation However we believe the level of earthwork observation and
testing services provided is reasonable in reducing the geotechnical risks to the project It
should be recognized that there are variations in the accuracy and statistical repeatability
of the tests and methods used to monitor the earthwork construction and materials Our
services were performed in accordance with generally accepted standards of geotechnical
engineering practice no warranty is expressed or implied Provision of construction
monitoring by an engineer is not insurance nor does it guarantee construction of any type
