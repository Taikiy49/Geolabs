investigation for the proposed project The study consisted
of subsurface borings laboratory testing and engineering

and recommendations pertinent to site development are presented
PROJECT DESCRIPTION
The property is located on the northwest corner of Vineyard
and Liliha Streets in the Palama area of Honolulu The site
is approximately 290 feet long by 275 feet wide with a gentle
slope to the northeast
An 18 story condominium tower shopping center bank and park
ing facilities are presently planned The 18 story tower _
located near the center of the property is proposed to be a
concrete structure approximately 150 feet long by 50 feet wide
Relatively heavy column and or wall loading is anticipated
The shopping center area consisting of a 3 story structure
which includes parking are proposed for the northeastern half
of the property on each side of the tower The column loads

III GEOLOGY AND SOIL CONDITIONS
Geologically the site is located near the mouth of Nuuanu Valley
which is underlain by a very hard basalt flow This flow is
shown on the geologic map of Oahu and underlies much of Nuuanu
Valley extending about two thirds the way up the valley Locally
it is covered with one to six feet of stiff clay and decomposed
basalt boulders At the subject site the basalt was encountered
in all the borings from 5 to 15 feet below the existing ground
surface and extended to at least 35 feet in depth in boring No 1
The overburden consists of 0 to 7 feet man made fill and 3 to
5 feet of stiff clay together with hard silt mixed with rock
fragments The basalt is exceptionally hard and took much longer
to diamond core than it usually takes for basalt The rock is
highly fractured and the fractures often contain seams of clay
This basalt flow apparently came out over a much older flow the
top of which had undergone a long period of weathering During
this time a deep residual red soil developed over the older
basalt This soil is hard and appears to have been baked by
the overlying basalt flow It maintained its structure during

Unsuitable material must be removed prior to placing
structural fill Structural fill beneath the building or
parking areas should be compacted in 8 inch lifts to
