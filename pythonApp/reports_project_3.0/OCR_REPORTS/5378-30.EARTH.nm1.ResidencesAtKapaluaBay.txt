This report summarizes our earthwork testing services performed during the site
grading utility installation and roadway construction for the Residences at Kapalua Bay
project from through March 25 2009 in general accordance with our
fee proposal dated In general our earthwork testing services were
provided on an on call basis and were coordinated through your office
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located at the former Kapalua Bay Hotel site in the Kapalua area
on the Island of Maui Hawaii The former hotel was demolished and five new buildings
were constructed at the site Substantial cuts and fills were necessary to obtain the
required plan grades for the project The fill materials consisted of the overburden as well
as the old fill materials which were removed and then replaced as compacted fills Our
services on this project were to perform in place field density tests using the nuclear
method at the contractor s discretion as a means of verification and backup
documentation of the contractor s performance during the backfill activities
It should be noted that the project owner Ritz Carton Development contracted
Engineering Inspections Hawaii for the project s quality control and formal documentation
during the earthwork operations and general site construction The grading subcontractor
Goodfellow Brothers Inc contracted Geolabs Inc to perform in place field density tests
using the nuclear method at the contractor s discretion for internal quality control only
The requests for testing services were on an on call basis at the client s discretion and
may not be in conformance with the project s specifications for testing frequency This
report consists of a summary of the random earthwork testing services during the site
grading operations utility installation and roadway construction

SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
Geolabs performed field density testing services on the site grading utility
installation
work
and
roadway
construction
from
through
March 25 2009 The site grading work consisted of clearing and grubbing and the
placement and compaction of fills from the native ground to achieve the design finished
grades Goodfellow Brothers Inc performed the site grading and utility installation work
During the site grading operations we obtained samples of the on site and imported
materials utilized as fill and backfill materials and transported them to our laboratory for
testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in
our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test
results are summarized on Plate 2
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 6938 Nuclear Gauge Method
Results of the compaction tests are summarized on the attached Plates 3 1 through 3 66
These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and
backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and
interpolating from on site survey stakes where available These locations and elevations
should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas
assessed as unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was
obtained and or accepted by our field representative based on visual observation and
hand probing
Our services consisted of on call field testing of the contractor s fill placement
subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations
LIMITATIONS
For internal quality control purposes Goodfellow Brothers Inc contracted Geolabs
to perform in place field density tests using the nuclear method at the contractor s
discretion on the constructed fills and backfills The primary purpose of the field density
testing is to provide a means of verification and backup documentation of the
contractor s performance during the backfilling activities The requests for earthwork
testing services were on an on call basis at the client s discretion and may not be in
conformance with the project s specifications for testing frequency
