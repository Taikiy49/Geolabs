Dear Mr Andres
As requested we have collected samples of the crushed rock material from two
stockpiles on the Island of Kaho olawe The crushed rock materials are intended to be
used as aggregate subbase materials for the project Two sieve analysis tests
ASTM C 136 and D1140 were performed for the crushed rock samples collected A
summary of the test results is presented on the attached Summary of Laboratory Test
Results Plate 1
Based on the specification requirements provided we believe that the samples of
the crushed rock collected are in general conformance with the specification requirements
for the roadway wearing surface for the subject project It should be noted that the
samples tested meets the specification requirements when the tolerance requirements
provided are also taken into consideration
