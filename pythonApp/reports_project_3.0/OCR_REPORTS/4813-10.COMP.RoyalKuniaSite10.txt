This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during site grading and roadway and utility construction for the Royal
Kunia Site 10 development from March 22 2002 to in accordance with our
fee proposals dated and
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the eastern portion of the Royal Kunia
Development Area in the District of Waipahu on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project
site is located east of Kunia Road west of Anonui Street and south of the Royal Kunia
Site 12 Recreational Center The location of the project site is shown on the Project
Location Map Plate 1 The project site includes a total of 90 residential lots and
two roadways The site grading work generally consisted of cuts and fills of less than
5 feet The grading plan for the site is shown on the Site Plan Plate 2
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The site grading and utility installation work for the Royal Kunia Site 10 project was
observed by Geolabs Inc from March 22 2002 to The site grading work
consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills up to about
5 feet in thickness to achieve the design finished grades for the project The site grading
and utility backfilling work was performed by Albert C Kobayashi Inc During the site
grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and
backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum
dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general
accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results
are summarized on Plate 3

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill
and backfill materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum
of 95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear
Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on
Plates 4 1 through 4 26 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared
ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and
elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These
locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the
methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified
minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on
visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of full time and part time field observation of the contractor s
fill placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility installation work for the
Royal Kunia Site 10 project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this
report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in
the following references
