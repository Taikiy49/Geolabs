Dear Mr. Yokomizo:
In accordance with your request, we have reviewed the Mass Grading Plans
(Sheets MG-1 through MG-7) transmitted to our office on March 16, 2005.
The purpose of this review is to render an opinion regarding the conformance of the
plans reviewed to the general intent of the recommendations presented in our report. Our
review was generally limited to the geotechnical aspects of the plans. It is our opinion that
the plans reviewed generally reflect the intent of the recommendations presented in our
geotechnical engineering exploration report prepared for the above project.
It is imperative that Geolabs, Inc. be retained to provide geotechnical engineering
services during the project construction. This is to confirm the subsurface conditions
assumed in our report and to expedite suggestions for design changes that may be
required in the event that the subsurface conditions at these locations differ from those
assumed and anticipated. Our recommendations provided in the above-referenced report
are contingent upon such observations.
