REMARKS
As requested by Steve Metcalf the excavation of three test pits were observed within the
footprint of the proposed Community Center which is situated east of Building P and north
of Building E The purpose of the test pits was to evaluate the condition of the near surface
soils below the building site In particular the presence of expansive clay soils was of critical
concern
Test Pit No 1 was excavated in the northern portion of the building and encountered tan silty
coralline sand and gravel within the top 3 feet
Test Pit No 2 was excavated in the eastern portion of the building and encountered
alternating layers of coralline sand and expansive clay
Test Pit No 3 was excavated in the southern portion of the building and encountered
alternating layers of coralline sand and expansive clay
Based on the test pit data we recommend that the building foot print be overexcavated to a
depth of 3 feet below the finished slab cushion level and replaced with structural fill
