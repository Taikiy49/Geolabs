As indicated in our geotechnical engineering exploration report dated April 1994
soft compressible soil deposits were encountered at the location of MSERW No 5
Valley No 1 Because of the significant height of MSERW No 5 we believe that the
removal of soft foundation soils and replacement with rock fill would be necessary to
provide overall stability of MSERW No 5 The limits of the removal and replacement
area were based on a review of the topographic survey of the site and the results
limited borings drilled in the area Based on the observed site conditions and borings
we estimated that very stiff residual and saprolite soils may be encountered at about
and near Therefore the limits of the removal and replacement
area were recommended to start from extending to about
After the clearing and grubbing operations at MSERW No 5 relatively stiff soils
consisting of brown clayey silts were noted at the ground surface near
which was consistent with our assumptions during design However during the
excavation of unsuitable foundation soils at about we noted that the
