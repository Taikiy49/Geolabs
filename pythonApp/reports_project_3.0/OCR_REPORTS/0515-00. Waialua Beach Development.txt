for the injection well design and construction It is planned

SOIL CONDITIONS
The site is immediately underlain by 5 feet of reddish brown
silty clay which may be fill Coral formation is beneath
this clay The upper 12 feet of this coral formation is a
medium dense coral sand with coral fragments Dense coral
algal reef materials were encountered to 55 feet
WATER LEVEL
Water level was noted in the boring to about 6 feet below
the existing ground surface This level may be influenced
by tidal action
PERCOLATION TEST
The percolation test was performed by filling the 4 inch per
forated PVC casing with water and measuring the flow rate and
water level inside the casing for a period of 15 minutes
DISCUSSION AND RECOMMENDATIONS
The in situ percolation test was performed by pumping water into
the hole at a rate of 35 gallons per minute for 15 minutes
apparent rise in the water level was observed during pumping
