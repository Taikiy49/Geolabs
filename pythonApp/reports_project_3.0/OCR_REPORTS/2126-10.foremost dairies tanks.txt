Production Pile Driving Report
Foremost Dairies Tanks
Honolulu Oahu Hawaii
In accordance with our proposal dated we have observed the
production pile driving at the project site from April 11 to
Sixty three 63 foot long twelve inch square precast and prestressed concrete
piles were utilized for the foundations of the proposed sugar and milk tanks A Delmag
D19 32 single acting diesel hammer with a rated driving energy of approximately 24 000
foot pounds was used to drive the production piles
A total of thirty six 86 production piles were driven Due to the contractor s
schedule we could not observe the driving of the first five 5 piles The remaining thirty
31 piles were subsequently driven under our observation to the approximate depths of
38 to 55 feet below the design cut off elevation and were numbered from Pile No 6
through Pile No 36
The individual driving records and a summary of the driven piles observed by us
are enclosed for your use
Based on our observation we believe that the production piles were driven to
sufficient resistance and or depths to provide the design capacity of 20 tons per pile
