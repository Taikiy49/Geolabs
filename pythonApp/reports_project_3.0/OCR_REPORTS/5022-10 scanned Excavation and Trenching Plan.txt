Based on the excavation and trenching plan provided we understand that the
soils at the project site have been classified as a Type C soil with some areas being
classified as Type B soil The soil classifications provided by the contractor are based
on the soil classification provided in Appendix A of Chapter 132 Excavations
Trenching and Shoring of Title 12 Department of Labor and Industrial Relations
Subtitle 8 Division of Occupational Safety and Health Based on our borings and
observations of the excavations currently being excavated we generally concur with the
contractor that the soils at the project site may be classified as Type B and Type C soils
We understand that the excavation sidewalls at the project site will follow the
sloping requirements provided in Appendix B of Chapter 132 Excavations Trenching
and Shoring of Title 12 Department of Labor and Industrial Relations Subtitle 8
Division of Occupation Safety and Health
Therefore we take no exception with regards to the use of the soil classification
and the sloping requirements provided in Appendix A and Appendix B respectively of
Chapter 132 Excavations Trenching and Shoring of Title 12 Department of Labor
and Industrial Relations Subtitle 8 Division of Occupational Safety and Health for the
project It should be noted that a qualified geotechnical professional should observe
sidewalls of the trench excavation to confirm the soil classifications on a periodic basis
