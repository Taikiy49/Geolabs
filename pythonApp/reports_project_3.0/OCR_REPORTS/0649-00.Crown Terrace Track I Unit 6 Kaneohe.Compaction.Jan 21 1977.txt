From June 30 to periodic inspections and
density tests were made at the subject lots This report
summarizes our inspection and testing As of date all the
lots have been filled and compacted adequately up to final
grade Compaction test results for the in place fills are
enclosed in the Compaction Test Summary
During the fill placement operations periodic field density
tests were made in accordance with the American Society for
Testing and Materials ASTM Test D 1556 Sand Cone Method
During the course of fill construction various types of on
site soils were utilized These materials were tested prior
to accepting them as fill materials The maximum soil density
and optimum moisture contents were established on the soil types
in accordance with the ASTM D 1557 designation and the test re
sults are as follows

The embankment fill construction for this site was accomplished
by utilizing the road cut material and the cut materials from
the lots wetted and compacted by a sheepsfoot roller or rubber
tired equipment Prior to the fill placement the original
ground surface was undercut to remove existing vegetation and
other deleterious materials The fill was then placed in essen
tially horizontal lifts and compacted to a minimum of 90 of the
maximum density determined in accordance with the above test
method
Subsequent to the completion of lot grading utility trench
backfill operations within the building pad should be properly
backfilled and compacted to match the adjacent compacted sub
grade Such backfilling operations should be performed under
soils engineer s inspection and tested for required compaction
This office assumes no responsibility for any alterations made
to slopes or pads on the subject tract subsequent to the issuance
of this report without our knowledge and written approval
Contractors involved with utility trench retaining wall and
footing backfill should be made aware of the necessity for
compaction and testing
Should you have any questions concerning the above please do
not hesitate to call us
