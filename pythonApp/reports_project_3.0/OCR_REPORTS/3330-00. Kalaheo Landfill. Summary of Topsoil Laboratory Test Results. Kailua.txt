Summary of Topsoil Laboratory Test Results
Kalaheo Landfill
Kailua Oahu Hawaii
In accordance with your request we have performed laboratory tests on the
Ameron Pit material In general we understand that the material will be used as topsoil
for the Kalaheo Landfill project The samples were tested to evaluate its pH salinity
and nutrients In accordance with the Standard Specifications for Landscaping provided
by the Department of Parks and Recreation City and County of Honolulu various
laboratory tests for nutrient content which includes phosphorous potassium calcium
and magnesium were performed A summary of the salinity and nutrient content test
results is presented on Plate 1
Based on the laboratory test results and our observation the inch minus
Ameron Pit material has an average pH value of 6 1 and generally consists of reddish
brown silty clay with low plasticity
Based on our observation and the laboratory test results it is our opinion that the
Ameron Pit material meets the requirement specified in Section 50 Soil Preparation
of the Standard Specification for Public Works Construction City and County of
Honolulu The nutrient content test results also indicate the presence of detectable
amounts of phosphorous potassium calcium and magnesium as specified in the
Standard Specifications for Landscaping provided by the Department of Parks and
Recreation City and County of Honolulu A copy of the specification is provided on
Plates 2 1 and 2 2 for your reference
