Environmental Drilling Services for

Laie Water Reclamation Facility

In accordance with our proposal dated August 18th we are submitting this letter
report and accompanying two sets of As Built Well Details Logs of Monitoring Wells
and Laboratory Test Results to summarize the work completed in conjunction with the
installation of monitoring points at the vicinity of the Brigham Young University of Hawaii
campus in Laie on the island of Oahu Hawaii
A total of five 4 inch diameter monitoring wells and thirteen 2 inch diameter
piezometers were installed in general conformance with the specifications provided for
the Groundwater Monitoring System for the Laie Water Reclamation Facility The
monitoring points were installed at the 18 locations as directed by Hawaii Reserves
Inc and are shown on the attached Site Plan Figure 2
Soil samples were collected at approximately five foot intervals utilizing a 3 inch O D
Modified California split barrel sampler All drilling tools were steam cleaned prior to
arrival on the job site
The laboratory testing consisted of performing moisture content and unit weight
analyses for each sample collected In addition 9 Atterberg Limits and 8 Sieve
Analyses were performed to aid in the classification of the soils encountered The
results are presented on the attached Logs of Monitoring Wells and Laboratory Test
Results
