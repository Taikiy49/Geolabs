COMPACTION TESTING REPORT
DEMOLISH 43 ANTENNAS
OAHU HAWAII
Dear Mr Mitchell
This report summarizes our compaction testing services performed during the
earthwork operations for the Demolish 43 Antennas project located within RTF Lualualei
on the Island of Oahu Hawaii on September 15 and in accordance with
our fee proposal dated
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The site is located within RTF Lualualei in the Waianae area on the Island of
Oahu Hawaii The general location and vicinity of the project site are shown on the
Project Location Map Plate 1 We understand that the project consists of demolishing
several antennas at the site The antenna footings and anchor blocks will be removed
and backfilled with fill material to a minimum of 85 percent relative compaction in
accordance with ASTM D 1557 This report addresses our compaction testing services
for the earthwork operations associated with the above demolition project
EARTHWORK
The earthwork for the project consisted of backfilling excavations for demolished
anchor blocks and foundations CK Construction Corporation performed the earthwork
operations at the site During the earthwork operations samples of the soil material utilized
as backfill materials were obtained and transported to our soils laboratory for testing The
maximum dry density and optimum moisture content were established in general
accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor The test results are summarized on
Plate 2
The backfill materials were moisture conditioned to near the optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 85 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557

During the earthwork operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM D 6938 Nuclear Gauge Method Compaction test results
are summarized on Plate 3 These tests were performed on compacted surfaces of
constructed fills and backfills Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and
interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be
considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to
be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of on call field testing of the contractor s fill and backfill
placement during the earthwork operations Based on the results of the field density tests
the fills and backfills at the locations and elevations tested are in conformance with the
project plans and specifications
