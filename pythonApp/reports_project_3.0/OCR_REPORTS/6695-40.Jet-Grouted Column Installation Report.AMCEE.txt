In accordance with our fee proposal dated August 7 2013 to General Growth
Properties Inc Geolabs observed the installation of jet grouted columns in support of the
Ala Moana Center Ewa Expansion project in Honolulu on the Island of Oahu Hawaii This
letter report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observation services performed
during the installation of the jet grouted columns at the project site The project location
and general vicinity are shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
Project Considerations
The Ala Moana Center is at 1450 Ala Moana Boulevard in Honolulu on the Island of
Oahu Hawaii The shopping center is generally bounded by Kapiolani Boulevard and
Kona Street to the north Mahukona Street and Atkinson Drive to the east Ala Moana
Boulevard to the south and Piikoi Street to the west The Ewa Mall Expansion site is
generally located to the west of the Kona Iki Street alignment
Based on the information provided the Ewa Mall Expansion project generally
involves expansion of the western Ewa end of the Ala Moana Center The former
Sears department store and parking area west of Sears will be converted to four levels
namely Street Level Mall Level Upper Level and Ho okipa Level of retail
development
The subsurface conditions at the project site generally consist of surface fill
materials overlying soft and or loose lagoonal deposits extending to depths of about
20 to 37 feet below the existing pavement surface Coral ledges of variable thickness
and hardness are generally encountered below the highly compressible lagoonal
deposits across the project site
New utility lines i e water sewer and drain lines and utility line connections
adjacent to the project site are required for the development The bottom of some of these

Jet Grouted Column Installation
The jet grouted columns were installed at the project site from
through Frank Coluccio Construction Company FCCC is the subcontractor
installing the jet grouted columns at the project site while Jayar Construction Company
Jayar provided the jet grouted column locations before installation commenced
In general FCCC was required to install 36 inch diameter jet grouted columns with
a 7 day unconfined compressive strength of at least 200 pounds per square inch psi and
a 28 day unconfined compressive strength of at least 500 psi To install the jet grouted
to various depths below the existing ground surface Grout was then pumped using a high
pressure pump attached to the jetting rig The horizontal jets of cement grout slurry cut
and mixed the surrounding in situ materials with the neat cement slurry grout and a
soil cement column was formed as the drill bit was slowly rotated and withdrawn from the
drilled hole
A total of 145 jet grouted columns were installed at the project site Ninety six
jet grouted columns were installed for foundation support of about 389 lineal feet of off site
gravity sewer line running along Kona Iki Street and several below grade structures
consisting of manhole structures a valve box lift station oil sand separators OSS and
grease interceptors In addition 49 jet grouted columns were installed for the wet well in
support of the re designed sewer force main and valve vault along Kona Iki Street The
locations of the jet grouted columns are shown on the Jet Grouted Column Location
Plans Plates 2 1 through 2 8 A summary of the jet grouted columns installed for the
project is provided on Plates 3 1 through 3 4
To the best of our knowledge it is our professional opinion that the jet grouted
column installation work at the Ala Moana Center Ewa Expansion project observed by
Geolabs has been constructed in general conformance with the approved plans and
specifications our geotechnical engineering report prepared for the project and
subsequent recommendations to accommodate the encountered field conditions
