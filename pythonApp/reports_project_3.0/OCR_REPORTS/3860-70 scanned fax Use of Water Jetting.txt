This facsimile provides our response to RFI 043 regarding the use of water
jetting to compact drain backfill material for the above project The contractor s
request and our response are provided below
GBI previously requested the use of 3B over the top of
the drain pipe backfill material A for protection from crushing and or
deformation during the backfilling compaction process because of 3B fine s
self compacting characteristics It was communicated by the State to GBI that
if 3B fine is to be used in this capacity it will require the use of filter fabric to
envelope the 3B fine For this reason GBI has submitted and is waiting
approval on No 4 washed sand and crusher scalpings for the backfill material
A In the interest of protecting the pipe from being crushed or deformed
during the backfilling and compaction process GBI is requesting to water jet
the backfill A material to achieve compaction
Please advise if this method is acceptable to the State
The existing subgrade consists of relatively impermeable clay material To
reduce the potential for water ponding on top of the subgrade and softening
the subgrade soils we recommend that water jetting of the backfill material not
be allowed
