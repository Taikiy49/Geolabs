Dear Ms Hanks
We have been monitoring the ground settlement for the surcharge program at the
Home Depot Dole site in the lwilei area of Honolulu Hawaii to evaluate if the ground
has stabilized under the weight of the fill Based on the proposed design approximately
4 feet of fill material was required to raise the finish grade elevation above the design
flood level and to accommodate the truck loading dock elevation Geolabs Hawaii
recommended a surcharge program consisting of an additional 5 feet of fill material
placed at the site in order to accelerate the anticipated ground settlements due to the
compressible soil layer underlying the site Due to the limited thickness of the clayey
portions of the compressible layer and the relatively high permeability of the loose sand
we had anticipated that settlements would occur relatively rapidly Site grading
operations began around May 4
To monitor the ground settlement due to the fill materials placed ten settlement
gauges were installed at the site from May 19 to The approximate gauge
locations are shown on the Site Plan Plate 1 Gauge No 1 was installed on native
ground under select fill and surcharge fill Gauge Nos 2 through 10 were installed on the
select fill under the surcharge fill The gauges were surveyed by land surveyors from
M E Pacific Inc Readings of the gauges and the adjacent ground surface were taken
twice a week for a period of six weeks beginning Readings are currently being
taken once a week
Based on our analysis it appears that the ground has stabilized From the data
obtained during our monitoring program observed ground settlements ranged from
0 2 inches to 6 5 inches across the site In addition Gauge No 2 showed ground heave
of approximately 0 5 inches and subsequent settlement to almost zero net ground
displacement It should be noted that some ground settlement occurred during initial
placement of the select fill prior to installation of the gauges and subsequent
measurement Settlement data is presented in Appendix A
