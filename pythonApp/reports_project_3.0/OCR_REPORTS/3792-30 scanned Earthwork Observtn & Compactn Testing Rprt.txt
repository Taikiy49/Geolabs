PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The 2100 Kalakaua Avenue development is in the middle of Waikiki in the District of
Honolulu on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is at the northeast corner of the
intersection between Kalakaua Avenue and Kalaimoku Street It is proposed to develop
the site into a 110 000 square foot retail complex We understand that the proposed
commercial development will consist of a three story steel framed structure with some
associated infrastructure The commercial development will comprise of eight units
ranging in sizes from about 5 000 to 15 000 square feet The approximate location of the
project site is shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
Based on the development plans provided the finished floor elevation at the ground
floor level for the retail stores is set at Elevation 7 5 feet Mean Sea Level MSL The new
building structure is shown on the Site Plan Plate 2 In order to provide a level building
pad for the proposed construction fills up to about 3 feet thick was placed during the
mass grading portion of the project
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for
the site grading and utility installation for the project site

SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The site grading and utility installation work for the 2100 Kalakaua Avenue project
was observed by Geolabs Inc from March 19 2001 to The site was
cleared and grubbed during the mass grading The site grading work consisted of the
placement and compaction of fills up to about 2 feet thick in order to achieve the finished
grades for the project Delta Construction Corporation performed the site grading and
utility installation work
During the site grading and backfilling operations samples of the on site and
imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to
our office for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were
established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor
and the test results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the new roadway areas the fill and
backfill materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 2922 and D 3017 Nuclear Gauge
Method The results of the compaction tests performed on the fills and backfills are
summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 19 of this report These tests were generally
performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the
site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from the
available on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be considered
accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to be
unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained and or
approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility installation work for the
2100 Kalakaua Avenue project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by
this report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the
recommendations in the following references
