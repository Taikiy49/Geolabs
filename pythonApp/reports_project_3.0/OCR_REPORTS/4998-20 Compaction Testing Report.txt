PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Pavilion project is located within the Kapolei area on the
Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is located across the parking structure for the
Hotel The location of the project site is shown on the Project Location
Map Plate 1 The new one story building will be approximately 20 000 square feet in
gross area with attached restrooms a food plating area and storage area In addition
new walkways to the new pavilion will be constructed The layout of the new pavilion is
shown on the Site Plan Plate 2
ELECTRICAL TRENCH BACKFILL PLACEMENT
The electrical trench backfill testing for the Pavilion project was
performed for the electrical trench located below the eastern portion of the new building
The trench backfilling work was performed by MK Electric During the backfilling
operations a sample of the imported material utilized as backfill material was obtained and
transported to our office for testing The maximum dry density and optimum moisture
content were established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test
Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3
The backfill materials were moisture conditioned to near optimum moisture content
and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as determined in
accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557
During the electrical trench backfilling operations periodic field density tests were
performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge
Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plate 4 of this
report These tests were performed on constructed backfills at the site Test locations and
elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These
locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the
