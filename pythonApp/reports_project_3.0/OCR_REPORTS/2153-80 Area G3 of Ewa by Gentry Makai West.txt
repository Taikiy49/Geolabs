The subject site is located on the southern side of Geiger Road west of Fort
Weaver Road in Ewa on the island of Oahu Hawaii The site is bounded by proposed
residential and industrial subdivisions to the west and east and is currently bounded by
Geiger Road to the north and an old cane haul road to the south
The mass grading work for Area G3 was performed by RHS Lee Inc The mass
grading work consisted of considerable amounts of cuts especially around the lake
areas and also consisted of a lesser amount of fills The on site fill materials was
generated from Area G3 Area G2 and proposed subdivisions to the east and west The
available fill materials generally consisted of light brown brown and reddish brown silty
clay and tannish white silty coral gravel and silty coral sand
Prior to fill placement the existing vegetation was removed and the exposed
subgrades were scarified to a depth of approximately 10 inches 10 brought to near
optimum moisture content and compacted to the appropriate minimum percentage of the
maximum dry density as determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 The
fill material was then placed in level lifts and compacted to the appropriate minimum

During the fill placement operations periodic field density tests were performed in

Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests are

During the grading period various on site soils were tested prior to use in the field
The maximum dry density and optimum moisture content relationships were established
in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified
Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 2 of this report
Our services consisted of full time and part time field observation of the
contractor s fill placement and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from
on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be considered accurate
only to the degree implied by the methods used Where low test results were noted the
areas was reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
