In accordance with your request Geolabs has provided geotechnical engineering
services during the drilled shaft foundation installation for the above project This letter
report summarizes our geotechnical observations performed during the drilled shaft
installation for the traffic signal and street light structures for the project In general the
purpose of our geotechnical engineering services was to observe the drilled shaft
foundation installation for general conformance with the project plans and specifications
Geolabs Inc performed a geotechnical engineering exploration and our
recommendations were presented in the report entitled Geotechnical Engineering

PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project will involve constructing new roadways between the proposed
Kapolei Parkway to the south and the Interstate Route H 1 Highway to the north The
Phase 1B portion of the project consists of constructing the roadway in the inbound
direction between North South Road Station and 23 00 and
Station and 108 41 and the roadway in the outbound direction between
North South Road Station to 108 41 The project and general vicinity are
shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
In general the new roadway will consist of a divided four lane roadway with
two lanes in each direction The roadway will have a planted median ranging from
approximately 8 to 32 feet wide and pedestrian sidewalks on both sides of the roadway
The traveled lanes will be 12 feet wide with 8 foot wide sidewalks Standard concrete
curbs and gutters will be provided on each side of the roadway and median

As part of the project new traffic signal and street light structures will be
constructed These new structures will be supported by 18 inch to 48 inch diameter
cast in place concrete drilled shafts The shaft lengths ranged from 6 to 20 feet The traffic
signal and street light structures are shown on the Electrical Plans Plates 2 1 through
2A
SUBSURFACE CONDITIONS
The borings drilled at the project site generally encountered alluvium consisting
of very stiff to hard silty clays extending to the maximum explored depth of about
51 5 feet below the existing ground surface In several of the borings toward the
southern end of the roadway alignment the alluvium was underlain by coralline
materials consisting of medium dense to dense gravelly sands and silty sands and
medium hard to hard coral formation The coralline materials were encountered at
depths of about 11 to 18 feet below the ground level In four of the borings toward the
southern end of the alignment a surface fill layer ranging from about 1 5 to 5 feet thick
was encountered Groundwater was not encountered in the borings at the time of our
field exploration
DRILLED SHAFT INSTALLATION
Foundation International Inc performed the installation of the production shafts
ten 36 inch diameter and two 48 inch diameter production shafts were observed at the
North South Road Phase 1B site from to The approximate
drilled shaft locations are shown on the Electrical Plans Plates 2 1 through 2 11 A
Geolabs representative observed the shaft excavation placement of the steel
reinforcements and concrete placement operations during the installation of the
production drilled shafts It should be noted that installation of some of the production
drilled shafts were not observed by Geolabs
The production shafts were excavated using a track mounted drill rig equipped with
30 inch auger 30 36 and 48 inch drilling buckets 18 48 and 54 inch diameter core
barrels and and 42 inch cleanout buckets The production shafts were
excavated to shaft lengths ranging from about 6 to 20 7 feet below the existing ground
surface
After the shaft holes were drilled to their designated tip elevations the reinforcing
bar rebar cages were placed into the drilled holes Rebar cage spacers were used to
provide the clearance associated with the minimum concrete cover between the sidewalls
of the drilled hole and the sides of the rebar cage
The shaft concrete was placed after placement of the rebar cage using an elephant
truck steel tremie pipe or by end dumping using the concrete truck chute The shaft
excavations were dry during the shaft concrete placement

Based on our field observations we believe that the observed drilled shaft
installation for the traffic signal and street light structures for the North South Road
Phase 1B project have been installed in general conformance with the project plans and
specifications and within the limits of generally accepted construction practices The top of
the shafts should be measured using optical survey equipment to verify that the shaft
position is within the tolerance of the project specifications Based on our observations of
the drilled shaft installation it is our professional opinion that the drilled shafts have been
installed to sufficient depths to provide the design load capacity
