This report summarizes our testing services performed during site grading access
roadway structures and utility construction for the Waiale Wells Transmission Line
project from March 14 2011 to in accordance with our fee proposal
dated
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is situated in the Waiale area within the town of Kahului on the
Island of Maui Hawaii The project location and general vicinity is shown on the Project
Location Map Plate 1 The project involved the construction of a 0 6 Million Gallon water
tank for the new well Nos 1 and 2 a control room and access roadway In addition a
16 inch waterline was installed for a transmission line from the water tank site to the future
Maui Business Park Phase 2 near the intersection of Mokulele Highway and Hookele
Street The earthwork consisted of cut to grade up to 3 feet for the structure pads
landscaping and access road along with the excavation of 4 to 7 feet below the existing
grade for the Waterlines A and B installations The approximate project limits are shown
on the Site Plans Plates 2 1 and 2 2
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
Geolabs performed field density testing services on the site grading and utility
installation work from March 14 2011 to The site grading work
consisted of excavation for footings structure pad preparations and the backfill of two new
subsurface 16 inch transmission waterlines to the design finished grades Goodfellow
Brothers Inc performed the site grading construction of the water tank and structures
access roadway and utility installation work Standard conventional equipment including

During the site grading and utility installation operations we obtained samples of
the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials and transported
them to our laboratory for testing and analysis The maximum dry densities and optimum
moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with
ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to the optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the asphalt paved roadway areas the fill
and backfill materials within 3 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum
of 95 percent relative density Structural granular fills under structures were compacted to
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 6938 Nuclear Gauge Method
Results of the compaction tests are summarized on the attached Plates 4 1 through 4 22
These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and
backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and
interpolating from on site survey stakes and other features noted on the plans These
locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the
methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified
minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on
visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time to on call field testing of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures as well as confirmation
of subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations Based on the
observations of our field representatives and the results of the referenced tests it is our
opinion that the site grading and utility installation work for the above project observed
and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were performed in
conformance with the plans and specifications
