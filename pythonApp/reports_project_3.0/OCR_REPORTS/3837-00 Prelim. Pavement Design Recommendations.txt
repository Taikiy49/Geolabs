We understand that part of the new lane will traverse an existing underground box
culvert near the property line at Shriners Hospital adjacent to the Jodo Mission Church
The existing box culvert is about 12 feet wide 13 feet deep with a top slab thickness of
about 7 inches Based on the current design concept the finish grade of the new
pavement in this area will be at about 6 inches over the box culvert Based on the
existing site conditions and the traffic data provided we recommend that a rigid pavement
section be used for the box culvert area The new concrete pavement should be 6 inches
thick and bonded to the top slab of the box culvert to provide an overall pavement
thickness of 13 inches The rigid pavement should be thickened to 13 inches outside the
box culvert and should extend a minimum of 15 feet on both sides of the culvert
