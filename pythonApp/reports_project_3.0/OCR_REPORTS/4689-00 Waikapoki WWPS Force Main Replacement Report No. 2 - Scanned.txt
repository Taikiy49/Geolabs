PROOF TEST PROGRESS REPORT NO 2
WAIKAPOKI WWPS FORCE MAIN REPLACEMENT
Dear Mr Enright
In accordance with the project specification proof tests were conducted on
production micropiles designated as Pile 16 17
21 and 22 for the Waikapoki WWPS Force Main Replacement project This
progress report summarizes the results of the proof tests
The micropiles tested consisted of 5 5 inch OD outside diameter steel micropiles
with a wall thickness of 0 476 inches We understand that the bonded sections of the
production micropiles were extended below the bottom of the permanent casings into the
underlying cobble and boulder layer Based on information provided by your office the
unbonded length of each micropile is presented in the Pull Out Load Test Progress
Summary presented on Plate A The station number or location of each micropile is
presented in the results of the load tests on Plates 4 1 through 22 1
As required in the project specifications each micropile except Pile No 9A was
loaded in tension in general accordance with Section 209 Paragraph 3 11 of the
specifications
Compressive pile load test was performed on Pile No 9A as a
pre production micropile in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1143
Standard Load Test Method as described in Paragraph 5 1 of the test method presented
in the specifications for the project The compressive pile load test result was presented in
our previous report
Based on the project requirements the maximum test load on the micropile was
58 5 kips In general the micropile was loaded to the maximum test load of 58 5 kips in
accordance with the following load schedule 2 2 kips 11 kips 22 kips 33 kips 44 kips
52 8 kips and 58 5 kips Each load increment was held just long enough to measure the
micropile head displacement but not longer than 1 minute
The maximum load of
58 5 kips was held for 1 O minutes for each micropile except Pile Nos 8 and 11 The
maximum load of 58 5 kips was held for 60 minutes at Pile Nos 8 and 11 After holding
the micropile at the maximum test load the micropile was unloaded
