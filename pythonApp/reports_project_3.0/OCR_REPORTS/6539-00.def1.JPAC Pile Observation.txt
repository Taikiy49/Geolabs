In accordance with our fee proposal dated August 23 2011 we have observed the
production pile driving operations for the above project This letter report summarizes our
geotechnical observations during the installation of the piling foundations
The purpose of our geotechnical observations was to monitor the geotechnical
engineering aspects of the production pile foundation installation for general conformance
with the project plans and specifications
Project Description
The JPAC project site is located near Kuntz Gate on the eastern side of Hickam Air
Force Base Oahu Hawaii The JPAC facility will be a three story concrete structure
supported by a combination of shallow spread footings precast piles and drilled shafts
The project also involves the placement of up to about 4 meters 13 feet of fill and
monitoring of settlement gauges in the fill area
The scope of work discussed in this letter report includes observation of the
driven production pile foundation installation for the Construct Joint POW MIA
Accounting Command JPAC project Nan Inc is the general and piling contractor for
the project
Production Pile Driving Observations
The production pile driving program consisting of a total of 64 16½ octagonal
precast concrete piles designated as Pile Nos 1 through 64 was conducted between
January 17 and 25 2012 under the near continuous observations by our OQ Geotechnical
Specialist Pile cast lengths ranged from 88 feet to 118 feet Pile tip elevations ranged
from 31 8 to 100 5 feet Mean Sea Level Nan Inc used a PileCo Model D32 30
diesel hammer with a fuel setting of either No 3 or No 4 delivering a rated energy of
