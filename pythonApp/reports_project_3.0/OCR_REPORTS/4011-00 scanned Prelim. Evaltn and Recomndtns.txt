Preliminary Evaluation and Recommendations
Geotechnical Engineering Exploration
USS MISSOURI Halawa Site
Honolulu Oahu Hawaii
In accordance with our fee proposal dated April 15th we recently completed the
field exploration with six drilled borings for the subject project In order to expedite the
project design this letter presents our preliminary geotechnical engineering
recommendations based on the field exploration and analyses performed for the subject
project Laboratory tests for selected soil samples obtained are being performed
Detailed recommendations will be presented in our formal report upon completion of the
laboratory soil tests
Project Considerations
It is desired to repave or resurface the existing ferry driveway and Parking Lot C at
Halawa in Honolulu on the island of Oahu Hawaii The project site will be used by shuttle
buses to pick up visitors and transport them to the USS Missouri docking area The bus
pickup area will probably have a new concrete slab
Preliminary Recommendations
Based on the clayey gravel subsoil conditions encountered in our drilled borings
and the anticipated heavy bus traffic loads the following preliminary pavement design
sections are recommended

The above pavement sections assume 64 buses per day and a design life of
20 years If it is desired to resurface the existing asphaltic concrete pavement to reduce
costs it is recommended to cold plane 1 inch of the existing asphaltic concrete and
overlay with 3 inches of new asphaltic concrete However it should be noted that the
resurfaced pavement does not have the same strength characteristics of a new pavement
and will have a design life of less than 20 years
