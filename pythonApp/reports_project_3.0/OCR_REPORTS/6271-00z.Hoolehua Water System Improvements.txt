Project Considerations
The proposed Hoolehua Water System Improvements project is in the Hoolehua
area on the Island of Molokai Hawaii as shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1 It is
approximately bounded by Kalae Highway to the north Waiakala e Gulch to the east and
open land to the south and west As part of the site work package for this project a
program of probing and grouting at the foundation locations of the Tank footings was
implemented during construction due to the potential for the presence of cavities and or
voids within the basalt formations underlying the project site
Probing and Grouting Program
A foundation probing and grouting program was implemented for the Site No 1
Tank in general accordance with the project plans specifications and recommendations
presented in our geotechnical engineering exploration report dated
The purpose of the probing and grouting operations was to reduce the potential for loss of
