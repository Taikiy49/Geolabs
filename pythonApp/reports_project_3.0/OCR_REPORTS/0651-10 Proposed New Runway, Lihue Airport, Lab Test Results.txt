Gentlemen
This letter presents the laboratory test results of asphaltic
concrete trial mix design for the proposed New Runway 17 35
Lihue Airport
Three types of quarry rock samples were shipped to our laboratory
by Grove Farm Quarry Kauai Hawaii The samples consisted of
crushed basalt aggregate taken from three different stockpiles
at the Grove Farm Quarry
For our laboratory identification purpose the three different
sample sizes were 3 Fines Chips and 4 Fines AR 8000 type
bitumen was used in the design mixes
The testing was carried out in two phases In the first phase
tests were performed on the above quarry aggregates to establish
their characteristics and suitability The test results indicated
adequate performance
Muroda Associates Inc 2
In the second phase the aggregates were mixed with asphalt for
A C batch testing A summary of the A C test results are
enclosed in Plate A 2
All the necessary performance tests on the aggregates and of
the asphalt concrete mix design testings were done in accordance
with the FAA Advisory Circular No Standards for
Specifying Construction of Airport dated
A list of laboratory tests test results and test requirements
is summarized under Summary of Aggregate Test Results Plate A 1
and A C Testing Results Plate A 2 Design requirements generally
follow those specified for Bitumtnous Surface Course Item P 401
and Bituminous Base Course Item P 201
For the A C trial mix testing the aggregates gradation was based
on the midpoint S of the aggregate gradation requirement values of
Type A 1 inch maximum Bituminous Surface Course The same gra
dation was used for Type B 1 inch maximum Bituminous Base Course
Actual future working aggregate gradation may differ from the
above midpoint values This will depend on the gradation of each
bin to be used in the final mix design Based on the 3 samples we
had 38 of 3 Fines 5 of Chips and 57 of 4 Fines would meet
the gradation requirements see Curve A in Plate B 3
Based on the above aggregate gradation a bitumen content of 5 6
will meet the requirement for Item P 401 Bituminous Surface Course
and a bitumen content of 5 0 will meet the requirement for Item
Muroda Associates Inc 3
P 401 Bituminous Base Course For details refer to AsGs
Testing Results Plate A 2
The percent of bitumen content for the final design mix should
be based on the actual aggregate gradation
