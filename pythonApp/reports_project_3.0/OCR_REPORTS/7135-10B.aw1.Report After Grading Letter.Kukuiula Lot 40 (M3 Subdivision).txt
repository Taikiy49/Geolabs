PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS 
The project involves a planned single-family residence on Lot 40 within Kukui’ula 
Development Company’s Subdivision in Koloa on the Island of Kauai, Hawaii. This 
residential project consists of a two-story house and separate garage structure, 
swimming pool, and other related site improvements. A Project Location Map, Site Plan, 
and Foundation Plan are shown on Plates 1 through 3, respectively. 
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services 
performed during the site grading at the project site. 
SITE GRADING 
We observed the site grading work for the project from December 15, 2015 through 
June 20, 2017. Site grading for the project generally consisted of excavations up to about 
14 feet deep for building/retaining wall foundations and swimming pool construction, and 
fills and backfills up to about 6 feet thick to achieve the finished design grades.  
At the on-set of earthwork, the area within the limits of grading was cleared and 
grubbed of unsuitable surface materials. J.M. Pacific Construction, LLC was the General 
Contractor for the project and Ka’iwa Construction, Inc. was the subcontractor who 
performed the site grading. During the site grading operations, samples of the on-site soils
