FINDINGS AND RECOMMENDATIONS
The project site is located at 1354 Miloiki Street within the existing Kamilo Estates
Subdivision development in Kamiloiki Valley on the Island of Oahu Hawaii Based on our field
observations the subject residence generally consists of an upper level of living quarters and
a lower street level of garage parking Based on the existing topography at the project site
we believe that construction of the existing residence was accomplished by excavating into
the existing hillside to attain the design finished grades for the house construction Our
experience in the area suggests that the project site may be underlain by colluvial deposits and
weathered basalt rock formation at shallow depths
Based on our field observations it appears that the subject residence was constructed
with slab on grade foundations bearing on basalt rock formation with varying degrees of
weathering Construction plans and other documents were not available for our evaluation at
the time this letter report was prepared Based on our visual survey conducted at the site it
appears that significant distress was not observed at the subject property However the
portion of slab on grade foundations for the upper level fronting Miloiki Street appears to be
partially exposed In order to reduce the potential for undermining of the slab on grade
foundations due to unraveling of the exposed materials below the concrete slab we
recommend that the exposed face of this portion of the slab on grade foundation be grouted
In addition it is our opinion that the existing grades of the yard area alongside the house
structure on the Diamond Head side should be raised to re establish positive drainage for
the subject residence
