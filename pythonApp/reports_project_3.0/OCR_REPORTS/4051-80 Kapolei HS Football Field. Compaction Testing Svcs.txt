Dear Mr Nonaka
This report presents the compaction test results performed on the sand mix
material at the football field construction project at Kapolei High School in accordance with
our fee proposal dated
Compaction Testing Services
The project site is located at the new football field under construction within the
existing Kapolei High School in the District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii Based
on the information provided we understand that it was desired to evaluate the consistency
of the sand mix material placed across the football field by performing compaction tests at
a frequency of approximately one test per 1 000 square feet of area Based on the square
footage of the football field a total of 107 test locations were planned as shown on the
Site Plan Plate 1
Prior to the field compaction testing a sample of the sand mix material was
obtained and transported to our office for Proctor testing The maximum dry density
and optimum moisture content were established in our laboratory in general accordance
with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are
summarized on Plate 2 of this report
A total of 86 field density tests were performed using the nuclear probe method in
accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 on Some of the
test locations were omitted based on the instructions given by of
Mitsunaga Associates Inc The results of the compaction tests performed are
summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 4 of this report
Test locations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site sprinkler
heads and survey stakes These locations should be considered accurate only to the
degree implied by the methods used
