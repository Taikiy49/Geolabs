Design Subgrade Conditions
Based on our site reconnaissance the proposed roadway reconstruction
alignment is generally underlain by medium dense to dense silty sand with coralline
gravel near the existing ground surface Laboratory tests performed on the near surface
coralline materials indicated a CBR value of 133 and 203 and a swell of less than 1
percent The laboratory CBR test results are presented on Plates 3 and 4 Based on
these assumptions and test results a design CBR value of 80 was adopted in our
pavement analyses for the coralline materials subgrade condition
