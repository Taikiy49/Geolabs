PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is near the intersection of Malakole Street and Hanua Street in
the Ewa District on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The general location and vicinity of the
project site are shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1 We understand that a
septic system consisting of leaching field and septic tanks will be installed for
wastewater disposal from the existing building for the project The location of the septic
system is shown on the Site Plan Plate 2
This report addresses our compaction testing services for the excavation
backfilling during the septic system installation
EXCAVATION BACKFILLING
The compaction testing services for the C S Wholesale Grocers project
extended from to The backfilling work consisted
of the placement and compaction of the on site coralline material within the excavation
The on site coralline material was the material removed from the excavation Royal
Contracting performed the excavation backfilling work We obtained samples of the
on site coralline materials utilized as fill material and transported the samples to our
laboratory for testing The maximum dry density and optimum moisture content were
established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified
Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3

The on site coralline materials were moisture conditioned to near optimum
moisture content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry
density as determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 The backfill materials within
2 feet of the finished grade were compacted to a minimum of 95 percent relative density
During the backfilling operations field density tests were performed in general
accordance with ASTM D 6938 Nuclear Gauge Method The compaction test results are
summarized on the attached Plate 4
