Pile Driving Report
Noriko s of Hawaii
In general accordance with the scope of services outlined in our proposal dated
we have observed the driving of forty eight 48 production piles required
for the subject project
A diesel pile hammer Model Delmag D 16 32 with a maximum rated energy of
precast and prestressed concrete piles The piles were generally driven to depths of
pre fabricated steel splice can was used in each pile to splice the 50 foot long precast
concrete pile sections The pile driving commenced on June 28 and was completed on
The driven piles are numbered 1 through 48 The locations of the piles are shown
on the attached Pile Location Plan
Based on our observations we believe that the piles have been driven to sufficient
depths and or resistance to provide the design vertical compression capacity of 40 tons
per pile
Enclosed are summary of driven piles and all the pile driving records for complete
