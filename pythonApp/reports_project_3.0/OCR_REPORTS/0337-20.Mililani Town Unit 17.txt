DISCUSSION
1 All deleterious material such as logs brush roots and
rubbish were disposed of offsite prior to placing fill
2 All loose and compressible materials were removed to solid
natural ground or bedrock in areas to receive fill
3 During the filling all boulders were taken out of fill
areas and carried off site
4 Fill consisted of on site and imported materials having the
above soil classification The fill material was placed in
horizontal lifts restricted to approximately one foot in
thickness watered as required to achieve optimum moisture
conditions and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of
maximum density with a sheepsfoot roller
5 Field density tests were taken at vertical intervals of
approximately one foot Areas of fill which were found to
contain insufficient moisture and or failing tests were
reworked until passing test results were obtained
