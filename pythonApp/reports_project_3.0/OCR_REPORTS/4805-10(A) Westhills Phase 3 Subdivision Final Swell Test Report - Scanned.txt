This letter report summarizes our geotechnical engineering services for laboratory

Project Considerations
The project site is located north of the Westhills Phase I Subdivision in the Makakilo
area on the Island of Oahu Hawaii as shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1 The
project involves site grading for 143 single family units The site development also includes
the construction of a public roadway system within the residential zoned portion of the project
site and installation of sewer water supply and lighting facilities The approximate project
limits are shown on the Site Plan Plate 2
Deep fills up to approximately 65 feet were planned along the eastern and western
areas of the project site The majority of the center portion of the site was cut on the order of
about 25 feet Moderately to extremely expansive soil conditions were anticipated at the site
Where practical the highly expansive excess on site soil was placed in the deep fill areas It
is desired to conduct laboratory swell testing on the soil samples obtained in the lot areas
that were mass graded near the finished grade to evaluate the swell potential of the soils
Based on our laboratory testing and analysis the on site soils were categorized into
four general types as described below
Type 1 Low Expansion Soil with 0 to 3 9 Swell
Type 2 Moderate Expansion Soil with 4 0 to 8 9 Swell
Type 3 High Expansion Soil with 9 0 to 18 9 Swell
Type 4 Extremely Expansive Soil with over 19 Swell

In addition thirteen 13 lots Lot 82 through 85 113 through 118 and
121 were constructed on more than 20 feet of fill Settlement gauges were installed in
these lots and measured by a licensed surveyor In general the settlement induced from
compression of the fill material under its own weight is a function of the relative percentage
of compaction and time period To resist the potential differential settlement Slab Type 2
was recommended for these house foundations
Laboratory Swell Testing and Settlement Monitoring
As mentioned above the house foundations were divided into four general types
based on the swell potential laboratory testing conducted on the soil samples obtained
near the finished grade of the lots during mass grading operation
In general the soil samples were remolded and tested in 2 4 inch diameter by
one inch high rings air dried overnight and then saturated for 24 hours under a surcharge
pressure of 55 psf Details of the swell testing results and recommended foundation and
slab types are summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 5 Summary of Swell Test and
Foundation Type
A total of thirteen settlement gauges were installed in thirteen 13 lots constructed on
more than 20 feet of fill The settlement gauges were monitored through optical survey
generally every two weeks The reading was transmitted to our office for analysis In
general the settlement gauges were monitored over the duration of four to six months
Based on our analysis it is our opinion that the primary consolidation settlement occurring
in the fill layer is generally completed The house foundation recommendations are
presented on Plates 3 1 through 3 5
