SOIL COMPACTION TESTING REPORT
TIMES SUPERMARKET AT KUNIA LOT H 5 A
This report summarizes the results of our soil compaction testing performed during the
earthwork operations at the above referenced project from March 7th through June 7th in
general accordance with the intent of the project plans and specifications
BACKGROUND INFORMATION
The proposed project is located at the intersection of Kunia Road and Kupuna Loop in
the Waipahu area on the island of Oahu Hawaii It is proposed to construct a fast food
restaurant at the project site Based on the information provided we understand that
retaining walls of approximately 360 feet long are planned along the project site boundaries
for grade separation We also understand that the earthwork for the retaining wall
construction will consist of minor cuts at the site and backfill of up to 4 feet behind the
retaining walls
SOIL TESTING AND OBSERVATIONS
The earthwork operations were performed by Highway Construction Company At the
on set of earthwork the area within the contract grading limits was thoroughly cleared and
grubbed of vegetation and other unsuitable materials All materials generated from clearing
operation were disposed of off site

The backfill materials needed for the earthwork were on site material generated from
the cut operation Samples of the on site materials utilized as backfill materials were
obtained and transported to our office for laboratory testing The maximum dry densities and
optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with
American Society of Testing and Material ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified
Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 2 of this report
During the backfill operation the backfill materials were generally placed in near level
lifts brought to above the optimum moisture and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of
the maximum dry density determined in accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557
Modified Proctor Periodic field density tests were taken during the backfill operations to
confirm the compaction degree and moisture condition of the backfill material achieved
during earthwork construction
A total of 30 field density tests were performed using the nuclear probe method in
accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 The results of the compaction tests taken
during backfilling operation are summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 3 of this report These
tests were taken on the compacted backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were
estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and
elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used
Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction
was obtained It should be noted the test results only reflect the compaction and moisture
condition at the time and locations the tests were taken
Based on the results of the referenced tests and observations of our field
representatives during our site visits it is our opinion that the earthwork operations tested
and observed by Geolabs Hawaii were performed in general accordance with the intent of
the project plans and specifications
LIMITATIONS
It should be noted that full time construction monitoring was not performed for the
proposed project site The majority of earthwork at the site has occurred without our
observation In addition it should be recognized that there are variations in the accuracy and
statistical repeatability of the tests and methods used to monitor the earthwork construction
and materials Our services were performed in accordance with generally accepted
standards of geotechnical engineering practice no warranty is expressed or implied
