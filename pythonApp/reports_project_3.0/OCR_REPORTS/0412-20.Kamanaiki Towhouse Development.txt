Supplemental Recommendations
Our previous recommendations given in our
report apply with the following exceptions
1 Stabilization keys will only be required within the creep
zones These stabilization fills should be constructed
as previously recommended but the location should be
shifted to beneath the building locations This will pro
vide a more effective and economical shear key and at the
same time a stabilized mat upon which the townhouses can
be constructed
2 Slab on grade construction can be utilized upon the stabi
lization fill since this fill must be constructed of non
expansive soils
3 The stabilization fills should extend 5 feet outside the
structure perimeters These structures can than be
designed as slab on grade buildings with spread or line
foundations
4 The townhouse foundations should be designed for the pre
viously recommended bearing pressure
5 All foundations outside the stabilization key should pene
trate the adobe soil and bear upon the underlying decom
posed rock These foundations would generally extend 3 to
5 feet below the existing surface to reach the decomposed
rock material
6 The roadway pavements and sidewalk areas must be undercut
a minimum of 32 inches and backfilled with non expansive
soil to prevent detrimental movements The pavement and
base course materials can be utilized as part of the
recommended 32 inches of non expansive soil
