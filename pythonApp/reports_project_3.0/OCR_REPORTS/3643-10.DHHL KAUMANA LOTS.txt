As requested we submit our drywell abandonment report for the above project
Due to the presence of standing water in the and
U11 were abandoned and backfilled with 3 000 psi concrete In general the concrete was
placed by concrete pump truck using the tremie method A vibrating rod was used to
consolidate the concrete as the concrete was being placed The concrete was generally
placed to above the top of the drywell ring except for Drywell No 4 It should be noted
that Drywell No 4 was concreted to about 9 feet below the ground surface The remaining
drywell structure above the concreted portion was later demolished and converted into a
catch basin Controlled Low Strength Material CLSM was used to backfill from the top of
the concreted portion up to near the finish grade for 6 and U11 The
following table summarizes the drywells that were abandoned
