Presented herewith is the addendum to the above referenced report
The purpose of this addendum is to incorporate one of the requirements of
the NAVFAC Design Manual DM21 which includes the latest changes for
rigid pavement design
According to the Manual stabilized bases are required when the
design concrete pavement slab is less than 11 inches thick The granular
or cement treated base courses should be compacted to 100 percent of
maximum density as established by method
As a result of the abovementioned provision the design rigid
pavement section for the P 3 design aircraft presented in the above
referenced report should be changed to
8 0 Inch Portland Cement Concrete
6 0 Inch Cement Treated Base Course 100 Compaction
14 0 Inch Total Pavement Thickness
