This technical memorandum summarizes our field observations for the excavation,
shoring, sheeting, and dewatering plans for the Kaneohe Bay WWPS No. 3 and Paokano
Loop Sewer Improvements project located at Kaneohe Bay WWPS No. 3 between
July 19" and 23", 2021, and our recommendations.
Based on our field observations, the trench walls exposed consisted of firm, stable
soil-cement mixture (result of the jet grout column installation). At the time of our site
visits, the trench excavation appeared to be stable with no observable distress in the
trench walls and ground surface. Soldier piles with steel plates and lagging were also
installed along the trench excavation for additional trench support. During this week, the
contractor excavated to the remaining trench depth of approximately 22 feet below the
existing ground surface. The excavated materials consisted primarily of medium hard
soil-cement mixture. We believe that the current construction progress is generally in
conformance with the plans and specifications requirements.
