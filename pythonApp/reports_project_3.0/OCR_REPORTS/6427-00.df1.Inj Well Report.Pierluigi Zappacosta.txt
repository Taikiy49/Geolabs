PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Pierluigi Zappacosta Residence is located in the Kaluakoi area of the island
of Molokai Hawaii As part of the residential development it is proposed to install an
individual water system which will process brackish groundwater through a reverse
osmosis system The system effluent will be disposed of through an injection well
The well is currently under construction In accordance with State of Hawaii
Department of Health requirements it is required that a geologist maintain the injection
well logs
LOGGING PROCEDURE AND SCOPE OF WORK
The logging of the wells was based on visual and textural examination of samples
of cuttings collected at intervals of about five feet during the drilling The samples were
delivered to our office for examination and logging The procedure was generally as
follows
Cuttings samples were removed from the sample bags and spread on a
white absorbent paper paper towel Initial visual observation was
conducted to determine the origin of the material e g alluvium basalt
formation or coralline formation Closer examination was conducted to

REPORT
The well log is presented as verbal descriptions in the attached table In general
the well at the site penetrated basalts of the Volcanic Series and other late
stage lava flows of West Molokai The materials appear to be interbedded pahoehoe and
a a flows No other significant geological or hydrological features were noted in the
samples provided
LIMITATIONS
The information submitted herein is based in part upon information obtained from
visual examination of samples collected by others Variations of subsurface conditions
between and beyond the sample collection points may occur and the nature and extent of
these variations may not become evident until construction is underway
