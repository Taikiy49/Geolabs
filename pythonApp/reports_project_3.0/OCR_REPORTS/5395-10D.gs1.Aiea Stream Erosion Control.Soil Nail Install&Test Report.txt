PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The erosion control project is along Aiea Stream in Aiea on the Island of Oahu
Hawaii The project site is adjacent to the Aiea Shopping Center and at the end of Laka
Place The project involves remediation measures for the continued erosion problem
along the stream bank adjacent to the shopping center The project location and general
vicinity are shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
The remedial measure selected for the project consisted of the installation of soil
nails on the stream bank slope adjacent to the shopping center The soil nailed slope will
be covered with a sculptured shotcrete facing A total of 115 soil nails were specified for
the project Due to the existing column footing discrepancy the installation of 30 nails was

SOIL NAIL INSTALLATION AND TESTING
Soil Nail Installation
Verification and production soil nail installation was performed by CMZ of
Hawaii Inc A total of 2 pre production soil nails were installed for the project The
pre production soil nails were located between Nail Nos 11A and 12A and between Nail
Nos 32A and 33A In addition a total of 85 production soil nails were installed The soil
nails were installed using a drill assembly from a Cassagrande C 8 drill rig suspended
from a crane The drill assembly was equipped with a downhole hammer and an 8 inch
diameter drill bit The approximate locations of the pre production and production soil nails
are shown on the Soil Nail Identification Plans Plates 2 1 and 2 2
The installation of the verification soil nails Pre Production Nail Nos 1 and 2 was
performed on The 85 production soil nails 11A to 11C 12A to 12C

installed between March 25 2014 and May 5 2014
The verification soil nails were drilled to lengths of about 35 feet Pre Production
Nail No 1 and about 25 feet Pre Production Nail No 2 at an inclination about
15 degrees from the horizontal After the hole was drilled to the specified length the hole
was cleaned by blowing out the cuttings and loose material in the drill hole A 1 75 inch
diameter Grade 75 steel threaded bar was installed in the drilled hole for the verification
soil nails For the production soil nails a 1 25 inch diameter Grade 75 epoxy coated steel
threaded bar was installed Centralizers were placed along the soil nail at 2 foot from the
ends and at 5 foot intervals
A ½ inch diameter PVC grouting tube was installed along the reinforcing bar for
grouting purposes Grouting began progressively from the bottom to top through the
grouting tube until fresh grout appeared at the top of the hole The grouting tube was
removed after the completion of the grouting operation A summary of the soil nail
installation is provided in the Summary of Soil Nail Installation and Testing Plates 3 1
through 3 7 The soil nail installation records are provided in Appendix A
