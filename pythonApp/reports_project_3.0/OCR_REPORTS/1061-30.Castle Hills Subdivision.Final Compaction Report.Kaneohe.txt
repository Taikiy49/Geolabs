Surcharge Fill and Instrumentation
Soft subsoils were encountered in portions of the project refer to
our soil engineering report of These areas were
stabilized with a layer of gravel and filter fabric prior to fill
placement and later surcharged for periods of seven 7 months to several
years to accelerate the settlements and reduce long term movements
Instruments were installed to monitor the vertical and lateral movements
caused by the weight of the new fill and surcharge
The following lots received surcharge fills and were monitored with
settlement gauges and slope inclinometers until the instruments indicated
that the movements had essentially stopped or slowed down sufficiently and
could allow surcharge removal and construction of permanent improvements
