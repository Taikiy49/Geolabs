In accordance with our fee proposal dated we have performed
construction inspection services of PV piers for the proposed Tracker System on the
Island of Lanai Hawaii The findings and recommendations herein are intended for design
input only and are subject to the limitations noted at the end of this letter report
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project involves the construction of a Tracker System for Power Light Solar
Electrical Systems on the Island of Lanai Hawaii The tracker system involves the
installation of solar panels and construction of concrete pads for the equipment The
required size of the piers is 18 inches in diameter and 6 feet in depth
FINDINGS AND RECOMMENDATIONS
We understand that the contractor completed drilling operations for the piers at the
designated design locations The drilled holes were left open and are waiting to be
concreted We_ performed field inspections from through
Our inspection included evaluating the quality of the drilled holes and
measuring the hole sizes to ensure they meet design requirements

Quality of Drilled Holes
Following drilling completion the island experienced several days of heavy rainfall
therefore we believe that the drilled holes may have filled up with water Most of the holes
appeared to have weathered the storm well however some of the holes appeared to have
soft sides and bottoms and some holes had caved in We recommend re drilling the
holes and cleaning just prior to concreting
Size of Drilled Holes
