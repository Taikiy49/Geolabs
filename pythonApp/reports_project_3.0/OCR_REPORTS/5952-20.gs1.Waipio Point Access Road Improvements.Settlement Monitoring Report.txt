As requested Geolabs has provided geotechnical engineering services during the
surcharge fill and settlement monitoring program for the above project This letter report
summarizes the results of the settlement monitoring program for the project The
surcharge fill and settlement monitoring program was recommended in our report entitled
Geotechnical Engineering Exploration Waipio Point Access Road Improvements OR L
Bicycle Pedestrian Path to Waipio Soccer Park Waipahu Oahu Hawaii dated August 14
2009
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Waipio Point Access Road Improvements project is in the Waipahu area on
the Island of Oahu Hawaii Improvements are planned for the portion of Waipio Point
Access Road from OR L Bicycle Pedestrian Path to near the entrance to the Waipio
Peninsula Soccer Park a total distance of about 2 500 linear feet Improvements
include the reconstruction of Waipio Point Access Road and parking area addition of a
pedestrian bicycle path and new drainage structure The project and general vicinity
are shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
The reconstructed Waipio Point Access Road will consist of 11 foot traffic lanes
in each direction with 2 to 12 foot wide shoulders In addition a parking area up to
about 23 7 feet wide and about 1 535 linear feet long is planned adjacent to Waipio
Point Access Road A 10 foot wide shared use path for pedestrian and bike riders will
be along the roadway separated by a 4 foot wide grass strip
Because of very soft fill estuarine deposits and harbor sediments underlying the
project site and the significant amount of fill within the existing ditch area a surcharge

SURCHARGE FILL AND SETTLEMENT PROGRAM
A 5 foot high surcharge fill was placed above the ditch area and adjacent parking
area between Station and 26 00 Prior to placing the fill a total of six
settlement gauge platforms were installed beneath the surcharge fill area The settlement
gauges were identified as Settlement Gauge Nos 1 through 6 as shown on the Settlement
Gauge Location Plan Plate 2
Baseline readings for the settlement gauges were taken on August 16 and 18 2011
using optical survey equipment Subsequent to the baseline readings construction of the
surcharge fill embankment was performed On August 31 2011 the vertical pipes on the
settlement gauge platforms were extended Unfortunately survey readings were not
taken prior to extending the pipes Therefore the amount of settlement that occurred on
August 31 2011 was estimated from the August 25 2011 readings We understand that
the surcharge fill embankment was completed on Settlement gauge
readings were taken on a weekly basis The last set of readings was taken on
The settlement measured ranged from about 1 to 14 inches The
Settlement Gauge Reading Summary and Settlement Versus Time Plots are presented on
Plates 3 1 through 3 12
Based on the settlement gauge readings we believe that the settlement waiting
period has been completed Therefore the surcharge fill material may be removed and
construction within the surcharge area may proceed
