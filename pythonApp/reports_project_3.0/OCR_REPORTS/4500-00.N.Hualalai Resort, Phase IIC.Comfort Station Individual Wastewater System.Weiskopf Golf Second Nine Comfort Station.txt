Dear Ms Owen
This letter report presents the results of our percolation tests performed in
support of the design of the Individual Wastewater System for a comfort station at the
Weiskopf Golf Second Nine within the Hualalai Resort
Project Considerations
The Hualalai Resort project site is located about 8 5 miles northeast of the Kona
international Airport in the Kaupulehu area within the District of North Kona on the Island
of Hawaii As part of the Phase IIC portion of the Hualalai Resort we understand that an
individual wastewater system will be constructed at the Weiskopf Golf Second Nine
WGSN Comfort Station Based on the information provided the WGSN Comfort Station
is located adjacent to the Hole No 13 green We also understand that the State of
Hawaii Department of Health requires performance of a percolation test prior to
construction of the individual wastewater system Therefore it was desired to conduct
field percolation tests at the proposed leaching field locations to obtain data pertaining to
the preliminary design of the wastewater disposal system
Percolation Test
Two field percolation tests designated as PT 1 and PT 2 were performed at the
proposed Comfort Station site The approximate locations of the field percolation tests
are shown on the Site Plan Plate 2 The field percolation tests were performed in
general accordance with the State of Hawaii Department of Health s Administrative
Standards to evaluate the permeability of the subsurface soils for disposal of
wastewater effluent The tests were performed in 3 5 inch diameter boreholes drilled to
