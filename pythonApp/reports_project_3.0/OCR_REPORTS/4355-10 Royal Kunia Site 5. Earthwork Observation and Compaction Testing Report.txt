PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the western portion of the Royal Kunia
Development Area in the District of Kunia on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site
is bounded by Anoiki Street on the north Kupuohi Street on the east and a drainage way
on the south The location of the project site is shown on the Project Location Map
Plate 1 The project site includes a total of 180 residential lots and nine roadways The site
grading work generally consisted of cuts and fills of less than 10 feet in the majority of the
site At the southwestern portion of the site fills of up to 20 feet in thickness was placed to
fill two amphitheater shaped gullies The grading plan for the site is shown on the Site
Plans Plates 2 and 3
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The site grading and utility installation work for the Royal Kunia Site 5 project was
observed by Geolabs Inc from August 1 2000 to The site grading work
consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills up to about
20 feet in thickness to achieve the design finished grades for the project The site grading
and utility backfilling work was performed by Delta Construction Corporation During the
site grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and
backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum dry
densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill
and backfill materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum
of 95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method
Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 5 1 through 5 36 of
this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should
be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed
to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
At the partially cut and partially fill lots at the top of the two existing
amphitheater shaped gullies the shallow fill and cut portions of the lots were
over excavated about 3 feet below finish grade and re compacted to the requirements
above
Unsuitable material consisting of tires wood and concrete fragments and some
metal was encountered in the area between Lot No 130 and Road C The unsuitable
material was encountered from about 1 foot below existing grade to about 3 feet below
existing grade The unsuitable material was removed on
Our services consisted of full time and part time field observation of the contractor s
fill placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations

Royal Kunia Site 5 project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this
report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in
