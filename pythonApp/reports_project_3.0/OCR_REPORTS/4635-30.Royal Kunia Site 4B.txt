PIPE PILE FOUNDATION OBSERVATION
ROYAL KUNIA SITE 4B
Dear Mr Agbunag
This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observation services
performed during the pipe pile installation for Lot Nos 35 and 37 for the Royal Kunia
Site 4B development in accordance with our revised fee proposal dated May 3 2002
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Royal Kunia Site 4B project is located in the District of Waipahu on the Island
of Oahu Hawaii The site is located east of Kunia Road west of Kupuohi Street and
north of the Wal Mart Store The general location and vicinity of the project site are shown
on the Project Location Map Plate 1
The Royal Kunia Site 4B project involves the construction of 61 housing units
Because of the close proximity of the house pad locations to Retaining Wall A at Lot Nos
35 and 37 special foundations were recommended for these lots The special foundations
include the installation of pipe piles to transfer the axial loads of the house footing below
the influence zone of the retaining wall A total of five pipe piles each are planned for
Lot Nos 35 and 37 The approximate locations and identification numbers of the pipe
piles are shown on the Site Plans Plates 2 and 3
PIPE PILE INSTALLATION
A total of 10 pipe piles five piles for Lot No 35 and five piles for Lot No 37 were
excavated using a CME 55 truck mounted drill rig by Geolabs Inc on May 3 2002
The bottoms of the pipe piles were excavated to about Elevation 362 feet Mean Sea
Level MSL Stiff reddish brown clayey silt material was generally encountered in the
excavations The excavation for the pipe pile was filled with a 3 inch diameter standard
galvanized steel pipe and concrete with a minimum 28 day compressive strength of
2 500 pounds per square inch The pipe installation and concrete placement were
performed by Quality General on May 8 2002 The approximate locations of the pipe
piles installed are shown on the Site Plans Plates 2 and 3 The installation operations
were conducted under the near continuous observation of an engineer from our office A
summary of the installed pipe piles is presented on Plate 4
