Dear Mr Phillips
This report summarizes the site grading work performed for the Kapolei Harborside
Drainage Channel Temporary Coal Conveyor Crossing project from about August 22
2018 through
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the Kapolei Drainage Channel off Hauna Street to
the north of Malakole Street in the Campbell Industrial Park area of Kapolei on the Island
of Oahu Hawaii The project location and general vicinity are shown on the Project
Location Map Plate 1 Based on the information provided we understand that it is
proposed to install approximately 180 linear feet of 4 barrel 48 inch drain pipe identified as
DL 1 through DL 4 to convey the flood water from one side of the channel to the other
Based on the grading plan provided we understand the site work will consist of 40 000
cubic yards of excavation and about 650 cubic yards of backfill The approximate boundary
of the project is shown on the Site Plan Plate 2
Kapolei Properties LLC Page 2
W O 6047 30
EARTHWORK OPERATIONS
Royal Contracting Co Ltd performed the site grading operations for the project
The site grading work generally consisted of trench excavation drainpipe installation and
backfilling of the excavated trench In addition the project also included rerouting the
temporary bypass road drain channel grading on the Mauka and Makai sides of the coal
conveyor and grouted rubble paving installation
Following the drain line trench excavation the bottom of the excavated trench was
compacted with a Rammax trench roller to provide a firm and unyielding surface for the
installation of the drainpipes Controlled Low Strength Material CLSM was used to
backfill the excavated trenches As a result no compaction tests were taken for the trench
backfilling work
To the best of our knowledge it is our opinion that the earthwork operations during
the site grading work for the above project in the period covered by this report were
performed in general accordance with the intent of the project plans and specifications and
our geotechnical recommendations presented in the above referenced geotechnical
engineering report and subsequent recommendations
CLOSURE
We appreciate the opportunity to be of continued service to you on this project If
you have questions or need additional information please contact our office
