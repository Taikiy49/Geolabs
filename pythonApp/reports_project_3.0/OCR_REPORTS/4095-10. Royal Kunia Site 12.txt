This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during sewer line removal and backfill site grading and roadway and
utility construction for the Royal Kunia Site 12 Panhandle and Cluster development from
April5 2000 to in accordance with our fee proposal dated
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the northern portion of the Royal Kunia
Development Area in the District of Kunia on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site
is bounded by Anoiki Street on the west and north Royal Kunia Site 12 development on
the east and Golf Course on the south The location of the project site is shown on the
Site Plan Plate 1 The Panhandle project site includes a total of 51 residential lots and
one roadway The Cluster project site is located adjacent to the Pan Handle site and
includes a total of 26 residential lots and six roads The site grading work consisted of
cuts and fills of less than 5 feet in thickness
Prior to the start of site grading an existing sewer line that crosses the site was
removed and backfilled
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SEWER LINE REMOVAL AND BACKFILL
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION

Royal Kunia Site 12 Panhandle and Cluster project was observed by Geolabs Inc from
to The sewer line work consisted of the removal of about
1 500 feet of an existing 8 inch sewer line removal of existing sewer manholes and the

and placement and compaction of fills up to about 5 feet in thickness to achieve the design
finished grades for the project The sewer line removal and backfill site grading and utility
backfilling work were performed by Royal Contracting Company Ltd During the site
grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and
backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum
dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general
accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results
are summarized on Plate 2
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill
and backfill materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum
of 95 percent relative density
During the sewer line backfill site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic
field density tests were performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D
2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are
summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 16 of this report These tests were performed on the
prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations
and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes
These locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied
by the methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the
specified minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative
based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the sewer line removal and backfill site grading and
utility installation work for the Royal Kunia Site 12 Pan Handle and Cluster project
observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were performed in
conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the following references
