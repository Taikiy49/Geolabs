PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site consists of about 20 acres in Spreckelsville and extends from the
coastline to Hana Highway Approximately 16 acres are designated for residential lots
which require about 1 to 8 feet of imported fill material to achieve the plan subgrade
elevations A single road with the infrastructure including water sewer and drainage were
also constructed The remaining acres will be constructed into a private park for the
subdivision
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
Geolabs observed the site grading and utility installation work from
to The site grading work consisted of clearing
and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills on the order of about 1 to 8 feet thick
to achieve the design finished grades West Maui Land Company Inc performed the site
grading and utility installation work During the site grading operations we obtained
samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials and
transported them to our laboratory for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum
moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with
ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method
Results of the compaction tests are summarized on the attached Plates 4 1 through 4 36
These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and
backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and
interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be
considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to
be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests our opinion is that the site grading and utility installation work for the
above project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were
performed in conformance with the plans and specifications
LIMITATIONS
Geolabs assumes no responsibility for any alterations made to slopes or finished
grades on the subject project subsequent to our observation and testing period without our
knowledge and written acknowledgment
It should be noted that it is not possible to continuously observe all activities
occurring simultaneously on a construction site therefore some work may have occurred
without our observation However we believe that the level of earthwork observation and
testing services provided is reasonable in reducing the geotechnical risks to the project
It should be recognized that there are variations in the accuracy and statistical
repeatability of the tests and methods used to monitor the earthwork construction and
materials Our services were performed in accordance with generally accepted standards
of geotechnical engineering practice no warranty is expressed or implied Provision of
construction monitoring by an engineer is not insurance nor does it guarantee
construction of any type The contractor shall retain responsibility for the quality of the
work for adhering to the plans and specifications and for repairing defects regardless of
when they are found
