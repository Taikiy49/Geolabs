Kyo Ya Restaurant

As requested we have observed the production pile driving operations Phase 2
at the above referenced project from August 27 to 29 1990
Twelve inch square precast prestressed concrete piles were utilized for the
foundations A single acting diese hammer with a rated driving energy
of approximately lbs was used to drive the piles In general the piles were
driven to depths of about 35 to 125 feet below the existing grade
During the second phase of pile driving six out of the 53 total piles were spliced
The respective locations of the piles are shown on the attached Pile Location Plan
Based on our observations and pile load test results we believe that the piles were
driven to sufficient depths to provide the design allowable bearing capacity of 40 tons
