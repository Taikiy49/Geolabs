SITE DESCRIPTION
The project site is located at the end of Nantalti Street
tn the Enchanted Lake area of Kajlua
The site is a cul de sac located at the bottom of a small
valley The cul de sac area is generally flat while the
side slopes of the valley slope upward to the east and west
at about 3 hortzontal to 1 vertical rattos
The area is paved with A C and an existing CRM wall is
located on the western and southern stdes of the cul de sac
The wall ts about 8 to 9 feet high with some cracking visible
A 30 inch drain line running in a north south alignment is
located under the roadway An open catch basin is located
at the southern end of the road and drains into the 30 inch
drain line
Existing houses are located on the north while the rest of
the surrounding area is vacant and covered with grass and
trees
PROJECT CONSIDERATION
The present plan is to remove the existing CRM wall and
construct new CRM walls on the eastern and western sides
of the cul de sac
The present pavement grade may be raised up to about 1 foot

DISCUSSION AND RECOMMENDATIONS
The subsurface exploration indtcates that the site is under
latn by dense sotl which should provide adequate bearing
support for the proposed walls

cross under or near the proposed walls To reduce wall
settlements these utility trenches should be backfilled

Well graded granular matertal such as base course rock
should be used as backfill material behind the walls
Weepholes or a subsurface drainage system should be used to

drilling 3 borings to depths of 3 to 18 5 feet below the

is presented on the Boring Logs Plates A 1 through A 3
In the borings samples were obtained by driving either a

sampler with a 140 pound hammer falling 30 inches
