Project Considerations
Two drywells were constructed as part of a parking lot expansion project at Kona
International Airport During the construction of the drywells lithological information was
not recorded Therefore two borings were drilled adjacent to the existing drywells
Wells 10A and 10B The borings were drilled to depths of about 25 feet below the
existing ground surface using HQ wireline coring tools
Purpose and Scope
The purpose of our geotechnical engineering drilling services was to drill one boring
at each of the two drywells to provide lithologic information for the drywell documentation
In order to accomplish these objectives we have provided the following tasks and work
efforts
1
Obtaining the necessary excavation permits from the State of Hawaii and
various utility companies prior to drill crew mobilization
2
Coordination of the utility toning and clearance of the proposed boring
locations by our geologist
3
Mobilization and demobilization of truck mounted drilling equipment and
operators to the project site and back
4
Drilling and sampling of two borings to depths of 25 feet below the existing
ground surface for a total of 50 lineal feet of exploration

REPORT
Our field exploration consisted of the drilling and sampling of two borings
designated as Boring Nos 1 and 2 Boring Nol 1 was drilled adjacent to Well 10A and
Boring No 2 was drilled in the vicinity of Well 10B The well logs are presented on the
attached Plates A 1 and A 2 Photographs of the recovered rock cores are presented on
Plates B 1 and B 2
In general Kona International Airport sits on the 1801 1803 lava flow of Hualalai
Volcano This pahoehoe flow came from a vent upslope of the airport and spread and
ponded when it reached the coast The wells at the site penetrated this flow and appear to
have encountered older Hualalai a a flows No other significant geological or hydrological
features were noted in the samples provided
LIMITATIONS

collection points may occur and the nature and extent of these variations may not become
evident until construction is underway
