Production Pile Driving Report
Kekaulike Revitalization Project
Ewa Block
Honolulu Oahu Hawaii
In accordance with our revised fee proposal dated we have
observed the production pile driving operations at the above referenced project site from
September 28 to
Sixteen and one half inch octagonal precast prestressed concrete piles were utilized for
the foundations A single acting diesel hammer with a maximum rated energy
of 69 900 foot pounds was used io drive the piles Due to the proximity of some of the
production piles to the existing structures lower pile hammer settings were used for these piles
in an attempt to reduce the potential adverse effects on the buildings from vibration generated
during the pile driving operation Results of our vibration monitoring services performed during
the production pile driving operation are submitted in a separate report to the contractor
A total of 276 production piles were driven for the above project The locations of the
driven production piles are shown on the attached Pile Location Plan Plate 1 This included an
additional pile No 83A driven at the proposed location of pile No 83 which was driven at a
different location as shown on the attached Pile Location Plan Plate 1
The Pile Driving Summary is presented chronologically in Appendix A and by pile number
in Appendix B Individual pile driving records have been forwarded to your office and the
contractor during the course of pile driving
Based on our observations and pile load test results it is our opinion that the piles were
driven to sufficient depths and or resistance to provide the design allowable bearing capacity of
fifty five 55 tons per pile
