REMARKS As required by the Hawaii State Department of Transportation and the
project specifications Section 511 we are providing geotechnical
engineering observation services during the drilled shaft installation
operations for the Mokulele Highway Widening phase
Bridge project number In light of the situation for the need to
begin construction of the drilled shaft pier caps for Abutments 1 and 2
prior to the completion of the overall production drilled shafts am providing
a draft report on the findings and observations of the drilled shafts installed
in Abutments 1 and 2 along with the CSL test results for submittal to the
State s Engineer for their review and acceptance A final report will follow at
the end of the drilled shaft installation
Based on my observations as the field representative for Geolabs Inc as
well as the CSL and integrity coring test results it is our professional opinion
that the production drilled shafts for Abutments 1 and 2 supporting the
Waiakoa Uka Bridge were installed in general conformance with the intent of
the project plans and specifications In addition it is our professional opinion
that the drilled shafts have been installed to sufficient depths to provide the
allowable design load capacities
