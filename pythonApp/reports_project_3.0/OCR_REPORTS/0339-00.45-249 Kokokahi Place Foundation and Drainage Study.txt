Dear Sir
This report presents the result of a single lot slope stability
foundation and drainage study for the above mentioned site
The study consisted of a hydrological and geological field
reconnaissance the excavating and sampling of 3 test pits
and laboratory analysis of the samples Engineering recommenda
tions for building foundation design and construction are presented
he SITE LOCATION
The proposed building site is located on the northwest hillside
at Kaneohe The property is covered
with a moderately heavy but open growth of Plum Berry Monkey
Pod Coconut Banyan and Mango trees Some scrub is present
A small stream flows through the northern end of the property

FIELD EXPLORATION
Field investigation of slopes soil types fill trash and rain
runoff areas was done utilizing topographic map prepared by the
R M Towill Corporation Three test pits were dug logged
sampled ring and blow counts of a 5 pound hammer dropped
30 inches at 1 foot intervals were taken Two 20 pound bulk
samples were taken from test pits 1 and 2
Additionally observations were made on surrounding properties
footings retaining walls runoff problems and general site
stability

Water
No groundwater was observed on the house or garage site at depths
of 34 feet during the investigation Groundwater near the sur
face should be expected on the lower portion of the building
site at stream level
Evidence of slope wash on site was present in the form of rills
and small piles of earth materials and debris at the base of the
slope Inspection of existing footings in nearby lots showed
that the footings could be easily undermined by slope wash Slope
wash on this property should be directed away from the footings
