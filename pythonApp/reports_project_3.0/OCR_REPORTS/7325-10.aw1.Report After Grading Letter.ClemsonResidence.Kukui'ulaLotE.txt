PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS 
The project involves a planned single-family residence on Lot E within the 
Kahalawai (M1/M2) Subdivision at the Kukui’ula Development in Koloa on the Island of 
Kauai, Hawaii. This residential project consists of a single-story house and a separate 
combined two-story guest house and garage structure, swimming pool, and other 
related site improvements. A Project Location Map, Site Plan, and Foundation Plan are 
shown on Plates 1 through 3, respectively. 
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services 
performed during the site grading at the project site. 
SITE GRADING 
We observed the site grading work for the project from January 22, 2019 through 
May 29, 2020. Site grading for the project generally consisted of excavations and fills up to 
about 10 feet thick for building/retaining wall foundations and swimming pool construction 
to achieve the finished design grades.  
At the on-set of earthwork, the area within the limits of grading was cleared and 
grubbed of unsuitable surface materials. Greenwood Homes, Inc. was the general

minimum compaction was obtained or exceeded.
