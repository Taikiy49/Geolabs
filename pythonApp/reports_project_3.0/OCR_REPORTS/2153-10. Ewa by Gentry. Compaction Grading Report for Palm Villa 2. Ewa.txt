Compaction Grading Report
Palm Villa 2 Recreation Center
Ewa by Gentry
From May 1989 to June 1991 we observed the earthwork and grading operations
performed for the Palm Villa 2 project site The work for the subject Recreation Center was
performed during the Palm Villa 2 Phase EE operations conducted in April and May 1991 This
report summarizes our observations and compaction tests performed for the above referenced
site
The project site is located within the areas designated as F 4 and F 5 in the mass grading
plan for the Ewa Gentry Development in Ewa off Fort Weaver Road
The grading at the above referenced site consisted of fills up to five 5 feet The material
on site generally consisted of dark brown silty clay mixed with coral gravel and coralline silty
sand and gravel
Prior to fill placement the existing vegetation was removed and the existing ground
scarified and recompacted The fill was then placed in horizontal lifts and compacted to a
minimum of 90 percent of its maximum dry density
