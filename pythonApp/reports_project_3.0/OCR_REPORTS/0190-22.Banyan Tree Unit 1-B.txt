DISCUSSION
All deleterious material such as lumber logs brush roots
and rubbish were disposed of offsite prior to placing fill
All loose and compressible materials were removed to solid
natural ground
Fill consisted of imported materials having the above soil
classifications It was placed in horizontal lifts
restricted to six inches in thickness watered as required
to achieve optimum moisture conditions and compacted to a
minimum of 90 percent of maximum density with a steel wheel
roller
Field density tests were taken at vertical intervals of
approximately 1 foot Areas of fill which were found to
contain insufficient moisture and or failing tests were
reworked until passing test results were obtained
The upper portion of the fill was utilized as base course _
