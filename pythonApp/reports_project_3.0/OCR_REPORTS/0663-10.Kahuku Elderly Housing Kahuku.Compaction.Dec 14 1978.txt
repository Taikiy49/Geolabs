Gentlemen
From October to November 1978 periodic grading inspection
and compaction tests were performed on the subject site
This report summarizes our inspection and compaction tests
on the filled lots backfill in the sand mining pits and
proofrolled cut lots As of to date all building pads
had been filled and compacted adequately up to final grade
The clean dune sand located on the southeastern section of
the site approximately from Lots 15 through 32 was removed
and stockpiled off the project site During the course of
sand mining the above referenced area was over excavated
down to a depth of about 10 feet below its finish grade
The over excavated area was then backfilled with on site
Silty sand Periodic compaction tests were taken in the
Subsequent compacted fill lifts up to its finish grade
The fill was placed in essentially horizontal lifts and
compacted to a minimum of 90 of its maximum density esta
blished by ASTM Test Method D 1557
Recommendations
Because of the downhill creep effect of soils on a slope
building should generally be placed a minimum of 5 feet
from the top of slopes
Kanuku Foundation Inc
W
Bae
Slab on grade should have deep perimeter footing below the
surface to be protected from undermining by surface wind
and water erosion
Retaining walls on slope should be avoided unless designed
by an engineer and inspected by a soils engineer to be sure
that adequate bearing has been obtained
Slope planting should be initiated as soon as practical
to minimize wind and water erosion on the new filled slope
Additional inspection and approval will be required at areas
of disturbance during the building construction Utility
trench backfill operations within the building pads should
be properly backfilled and compacted to match the adjacent
compacted subgrade
Subcontractors involved with utility trench and footing
backfill should be made aware of the above requirements
This office assumes no responsibility for any alterations
made to slopes or building pads on the subject tract subsequent
to the issuance of this report without our knowledge and written
approval
Should you have any questions concerning the above please
do not hesitate to contact us
