Dear Ms Shibata
In accordance with your request we have reviewed the set of Plans for the
above referenced project received on Our review included the Civil
and Structural Plans Sheets X S 1 and S 2
The purpose of this review is to render an opinion regarding the conformance of
the plans reviewed to the general intent of the recommendations presented in the
above referenced report Our review was generally limited to the geotechnical aspects of
the plans
Based on our review it is our opinion that the subject plans reviewed generally
reflect the intent of the recommendations presented in our geotechnical engineering
report prepared for the subject project
It is imperative that Geolabs Inc be retained to provide geotechnical
engineering services during the project construction This is to confirm that the actual
exposed subsurface conditions encountered during construction are similar to those
assumed in our report and to expedite suggestions for design changes that may be
required in the event that the subsurface conditions at these locations differ from those
assumed and anticipated Our recommendations provided in the above referenced
report are contingent upon such observations
