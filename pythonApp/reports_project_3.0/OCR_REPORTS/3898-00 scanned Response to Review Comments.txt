Dear Mr Oyama
In response to the engineering review comments by on our geotechnical
engineering report submitted for the subject project we are providing the additional information as
shown on the attached Engineering Review Comments sheets Some of the review comments
are separately addressed in this letter as follows
Item No 1
Since the subsurface soil profiles in the TAMC area will likely consist of mudrock at
relatively shallow depths we believe that the accumulation of water at the downsiream
side of the alignment resulting from the transmission of water through the granular backfill
should not create significant problems that might affect the performance of the sewerline in
the future
Item No 3
Based on the previous and current borings drilled within the GTE Baseyard site it appears
that the existing AC pavements are generally on the order of about 2 to 4 inches thick For
the PCC pavements we believe that a slab thickness of 8 inches may be used for cost
estimation purposes
