DISCUSSION AND RECOMMENDATIONS
Based on the field exploration and laboratory test results
it is our opinion that the proposed reservoir and roadway
sites are suitable for the planned construction
Foundations
Spread or continuous footing foundations are recommended
for the reservoir structure
An allowable bearing value of 3500 p s f can be used for
footings resting on the stiff natural ground
Little settlement less than half an inch is anticipated
for the 0 2 million gallon reservoir
The footings should bear a minimum of 12 inches below the
adjacent finish grade The ground surface around the
footings should not be excavated or allowed to erode
The bottom of all footing excavations should be compacted
to 95 of its maximum dry density established by ASTM Test
Method D 1557
If a footing is located next to a weility trench at should
extend down to the bottom of the utility trench
Slab on grade should bear entirely on the stiff natural soil
or entirely on compacted structural fill

CONSTRUCTION MONITORING
It is recommended that the soils engineer be present

drilling 3 borings to a depth of 16 5 feet below the exist
