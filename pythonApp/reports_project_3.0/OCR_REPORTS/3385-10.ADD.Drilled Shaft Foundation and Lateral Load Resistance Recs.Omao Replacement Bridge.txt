above referenced report Other recommendations contained in the above referenced
report remain applicable to the project

Lateral Load Resistance
Based on the new scour depths for the two abutments and an imposed lateral load
of 18 kips per drilled shaft we have evaluated the lateral load resistance of the shafts
Based on our analyses the horizontal deflection based on a fixed connection at the top of
the drilled shaft is about 0 2 inches The maximum moment induced in the drilled shaft
occurs near the top of the shaft with a value of about 350 kip feet The moment
diagrams showing the distribution of deflection induced moment and shear along the
length of the drilled shaft are presented on the attached Plates 1 and 2
