Request for Right of Entry Permit
Geotechnical Engineering Exploration for
Proposed Kauai Intermediate School
In accordance with your requesi this letter describes the activities associated with
our performance of the geotechnical engineering exploration for the proposed Kauai
Intermediate School We are requesting a Right of Entry Permit to the property to
perform the field exploration work
Our field exploration will consist of the drilling and sampling of about 20 test
borings at selected locations across the proposed school site The borings will be about
6 inches in diameter and extend to depths of about 20 to 30 feet below ground level The
borings will be backfilled upon completion of the work
It is anticipated that the site is vegetated with tall grass and abandoned sugar cane
growth As a result trails cleared by a backhoe will provide access for vehicles to the
proposed boring locations as generally shown on the attached plan of the site No
earthmoving is anticipated in association with the trail clearing and drilling work General
access to the site is anticipated from an existing dirt haul road located to the southwest of
the proposed site
The work is anticipated to take approximately 2 to 3 weeks in duration some time
between and The exact dates are uncertain at this
time and are subject to some changes depending on the availability of equipment and
weather conditions
