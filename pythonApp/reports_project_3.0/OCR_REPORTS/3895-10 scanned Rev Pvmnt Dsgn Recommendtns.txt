In the determination of the thickness of the stabilized subbase course we
have assumed an Equivalency Factor of 1 5 for the Crushed Aggregate
Base Course Item P 209
Upon further evaluation of the quality of the aggregates in Pago Pago it is our
opinion that Aggregate Base Course Item P 208 would be more readily available
locally in Pago Pago than Crushed Aggregate Base Course Item P 209 Therefore
we recommend that Item P 208 in lieu of Item P 209 be used in the subbase course in
the above stabilized pavement sections Based on an Equivalency Factor of 1 25 for
the Aggregate Base Course Item P 208 the stabilized subbase layer will need to be
increased from 11 to 13 inches for the runway taxiway blast pad areas and from 8 to
9 inches for the shoulder areas Therefore we recommend that the following revised
stabilized pavement sections be used for the subject project
