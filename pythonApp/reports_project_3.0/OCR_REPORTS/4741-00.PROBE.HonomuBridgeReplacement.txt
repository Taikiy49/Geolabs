Report on Probing and Grouting Services

Dear Ms Kishimori
This report summarizes our probing and grouting services performed for the above
project on August 28 2001 Our work was performed in general accordance with our fee
proposal of
A total of eight probeholes were drilled below the proposed bottom of footings for
the proposed new bridge at the Honumu Stream in Hilo on the Island of Hawaii The new
bridge will be approximately 108 feet long supported by concrete piers Four probeholes
were drilled at each abutment Since the footing subgrade had not been excavated at the
time of our probing operation probing was performed at each probehole location starting
from the existing road surface elevation to a total depth of approximately 34 feet deep
below the existing ground surface Approximately 18 feet of fill materials consisting
primarily of basalt boulders and gravel will be excavated to attain the required bottom of
abutment footing elevation or top of subgrade elevation Probing of the materials 16 feet
below the footing elevation is required by the specifications
Probing was performed on August 28 2001 using Isemoto Contracting s Ingersoll
Rand air track drill with 37 inch diameter percussion hammer Immediately after the
probing work the probeholes were backfilled with Jas W Glover s Mix No 2239
sand cement grout applied at low pressure
No significant cavities were directly encountered during the probing work with the
exception of approximately 2 foot layers of soft materials at Probehole Nos B 1 B 2 at
approximately 4 to 6 feet below the planned bottom of footing respectively No signs of
cavities or voids were observed during the probing of boreholes However the amount of
grout materials used to fill the probeholes exceeded the theoretical volume of grout The
excessive grout intake on the probeholes maybe attributed to voids or cavities or crevices
within the immediate vicinity of the probeholes or due to the proximity of the probeholes to
the streambed A total of 6 0 cubic yards of sand cement grout was used to backfill the
probeholes Backfilling of the probeholes was also performed on August 28 2001
