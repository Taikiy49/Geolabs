Hillside Cut Slope Recommendation
Hanapepe Drain Project
At your request we have reviewed the cut slope design for the hillside area above
the proposed box culvert alongside Kaumuali Highway for the cited project
A site visit was also made on by an engineer from our office to
obtain an overview of the exposed soil rock conditions along the proposed cut area for
the formulation of the design cut slope recommendation
Based on our field observation moderately weathered rock could be observed
outcropping along the toe area of the proposed hillside cut area Along the upper top
portion of the hillside area the exposed surface soil condition transitions from that of soil
to weathered rock and boulders as the hillside runs from the side of Lele Road to the
other side next to Hanapepe Road We were not able to obtain an accurate delineation
of the upper soil profile overlying the weathered rock and boulder zone
It is Our opinion that a cut slope ratio of one horizontal to one vertical 1H 1V may
be used for the lower slope area where weathered rock is encountered and a cut slope
ratio of two horizontal to one vertical 2H 1H is recommended for upper slope area where
soil is encountered However a more detailed subsoils profile could not be determined
due to the limited amount of information obtained based on our recent field observation
Therefore we believe that additional field exploration involving boreholes drilling and
detailed geologic reconnaissance is needed to further mapped out and to delineate the
upper soil and the underlying weathered rock profiles
