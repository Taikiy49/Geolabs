Dear Director
We wish to inform the department of our intent to perform trail clearing for the
purpose of conducting subsurface geotechnical soils exploration at the subject site The
site is located at the base of the eastern flank of Kaluanui Ridge near the mouth of
Kamilonui Valley in the Hawaii Kai area on the island of Oahu Hawaii The site is located
along the western side of Hawaii Kai Drive just north to the Lalea at Hawaii Kai
development
We wish to emphasize that the trail clearing is to provide access for our personnel
and excavation equipment to the site and is not part of any project grubbing or grading
work Our work will consist of excavating and backfilling test pits at eight selected
locations to obtain subsurface data pertaining to buried soil conditions
Our soils exploration is anticipated to commence sometime during the months of
April and May for a period of approximately 3 days We estimate up to about 800 lineal
feet of trail may be cleared on the approximately 2 1 acre parcel The trail will be made to
allow access for a track mounted excavator machine only Vehicular traffic is not
anticipated Therefore the trail will be limited to crude construction for excavator entry
only
