Pier Load Test Report
Chance Helical Pier at Lot No 42
Royal Kunia 13B Phase 2
Waipahu Oahu Hawaii
This report presents the results of the pier load test performed on September 11
at Lot No 42 of the Royal Kunia 13B Phase 2 development in the Waipahu area on the
island of Oahu Hawaii The pier tested was an 8 inch diameter Chance helical anchor
with a 5 foot long rod and two 7 foot long extensions The test pier was located at the
bottom right hand corner of the proposed building location as shown on Plate 1 Lot 42
Special Foundation Plan undated prepared by Inc
The test anchor was loaded in general accordance with the quick load test method
for individual piles of ASTM Test Method D 1143 The test pier was loaded to
200 percent of the design load of 20 kips in increments of 10 to 20 percent 2 to 4 kips
up to a total test load of 40 kips Each load increment was held for a minimum duration of
2 5 minutes with the full test load held for a duration of 15 minutes Subsequently the full
test load was unloaded in 3 decrements with each load decrement held for about
5 minutes Lateral pier movement on order of about 1 to 2 inches was observed during
the pier load testing operations The test results of the pier load test are summarized as
follows
