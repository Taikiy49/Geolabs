GEOTECHNICAL ENGINEERING OBSERVATION AND TESTING DURING HOUSE
CONSTRUCTION REPORT
ROYAL KUNIA SITE 4
Dear Mr Lau
This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during house construction for the Royal Kunia Site 4 development from
to August 2 2000 in accordance with our revised fee proposal dated
August 26 1999
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the southwestern portion of the Royal Kunia
Development Area in the District of Kunia on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site
is bounded by Kunia Road on the west a drainageway on the north Kupuohi Street on the
east and Wal Mart building on the south The location of the project site is shown on the
Site Plan Plate 1 The project site includes a total of 86 residential lots and five roads
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the house grading and utility installation within the house lot areas for the project
site
HOUSE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The house grading and utility installation work for the Royal Kunia Site 4 project was
observed by Geolabs Inc from to August 2 2000 The house grading
work consisted of footing excavation and footing and slab on grade subgrade preparation
for the project The utility installation work consisted of trench backfill of plumbing and sewer
lines within the house lots The house grading and utility backfilling work was performed by
Reef Development of Hawaii Inc During the house grading operations samples of the
on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and
transported to our office for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture
contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test
Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 2

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557
During the house grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear
Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 3 1
through 3 3 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces
and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were
estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and
elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used
Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum
compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on visual
observation and hand probing
Field density tests were also performed during construction of a Keystone Modular
Block Retaining Wall behind Lot Nos 22 through 29 Results of the compaction tests
performed are summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 3
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s backfill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade
soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the house grading and utility installation work for the
above referenced project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this
report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the
following references

be recognized that there are variations in the accuracy and statistical repeatability of the tests
and methods used to monitor the earthwork construction and materials Our services were
