Dear Mr Kong
In accordance with our Blanket Purchase Agreement Soil Rock Testing with the
laboratory tests requested in Call No 9 Based on the request we have completed the
following laboratory tests and are transmitting the test results to you for your use Preliminary
test results have been previously transmitted to your office
The laboratory tests performed included the determination of natural moisture content
modified swell tests Atterberg Limits consolidation unconsolidated undrained triaxial
compression California Bearing Ratio CBR and Modified Proctor compaction tests The
results of the tests performed are summarized on the Summary of Laboratory Test Results
Table 1
Seventeen moisture content ASTM D 2216 determinations were performed on
selected soil samples as an aid in the classification and evaluation of soil properties The
results of these tests are presented on the Summary of Laboratory Test Results Table 1
Two modified swell tests one inch rings were performed on relatively undisturbed
samples to evaluate the swelling potential of the soils under a surcharge pressure of about
55 pounds per square foot psf One test was performed on a sample at the natural
moisture content and one sample was air dried before inundation with water Results of the
swell tests are presented on the Summary of Laboratory Test Results Table 1
Two Atterberg Limits tests ASTM D 4318 were performed on selected samples
of the soils to evaluate the liquid and plastic limits and to aid in soil classification Results of
the test are summarized on the Summary of Laboratory Test Results Table 1 Graphic
presentation of the test results is provided on Plate A 1

Three consolidation tests ASTM D 2435 were performed on selected soil samples
to evaluate the compressibility characteristics of the soils The tests were performed on the
relatively undisturbed on site soils It should be noted that the consolidation performed on
the natural on site soils located at B 12 consolidated further upon inundation with water see
Plate A 2 The other two consolidation tests performed swelled upon inundation with water
see Plates A 3 and A 4 The test results and consolidation curves are presented on Plates
A 2 through A 4
One Unconsolidated Undrained Triaxial Compression test ASTM D 2850 was
performed to evaluate the undrained shear strength of the clayey soils The test was
performed on a remolded soil sample prepared from the bulk sample located at B 16 The
applied confining pressure requested was approximately 550 pounds per square foot psf
The test results and stress strain curve are presented on Plate A 5
Two Modified Proctor tests ASTM D 1557 were performed on the two bulk samples
located at B 16 and B 19 to evaluate the relationship between the moisture content and the
dry density of the soils Results of the tests are presented on Plates A 6 and A 7
One California Bearing Ratio CBR test ASTM D 1883 was performed on the bulk
sample located at B 19 to evaluate the strength characteristics for pavement subgrade
support Results of the CBR test are presented on Plate A 8
We appreciate the opportunity to provide our laboratory testing services to you on this
project If you have questions or need additional information please contact our office
