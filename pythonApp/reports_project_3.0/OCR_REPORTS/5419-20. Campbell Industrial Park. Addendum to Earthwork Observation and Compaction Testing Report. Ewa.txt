from April 13, 2011 to April 19, 2011 in accordance with our fee proposal dated
November 13, 2008.
SITE GRADING
Geolabs, Inc. observed the site grading for the driveway and sidewalk areas for the
Air Pollution Control System Improvement project from April 13, 2011 to April 19, 2011. The
site grading consisted of clearing and grubbing, and placement and compaction of fills to
achieve the design finished grades for the project. Parsons Construction Group — Pacific
Division performed the site grading work.
The fill materials were moisture-conditioned to above optimum moisture content, and
compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as determined in
accordance with ASTM D 1557. In the pavement areas, the fill materials within 2 feet of the
finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of 95 percent relative compaction.
During the site grading operations, periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM D 6938 (Nuclear Gauge Method). Results of the compaction
tests performed are summarized on the attached Plate 1. These tests were performed on
the prepared ground surfaces and on consiructed fills at the site. Test locations and
