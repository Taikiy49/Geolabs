Dear Mr Jacobus
In accordance with your request we have provided compaction testing services at
the HRI Anemoku Street project in the Laie area on the Island of Oahu Hawaii
Field density tests of the waterline trench backfill were performed at 50 foot
intervals along the road shoulder and at utility road crossings to confirm compliance with
the compaction requirements as stipulated in the project plans and specifications
Samples of the material utilized as fill materials were obtained and transported to our
laboratory for testing The maximum dry density and optimum moisture content were
established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor
and the test results are summarized on Plate 2 A total of 21 field density tests were
performed using the nuclear probe method in accordance with ASTM D 6938 The results
of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 3 1 and 3 2 These tests
were taken on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed engineered fills at the
site The results are also summarized below
