In accordance with our fee proposal dated October 18th we have performed
additional laboratory testing and evaluation for the above referenced project Our
previous fieid exploration at the project site encountered near surface soils ranging from
slightly to highly expansive when subjected to moisture fluctuations In order to reduce
the cost of over excavation of the potentially expansive soils and replacement with non
expansive materials at Area B we have recommended that additional near surface soil
samples be obtained for swell tests in order to delineate potential areas that may be
underlain by the highly expansive clayey soils
Our supplemental field exploration consisted of obtaining 12 soil samples from
approximately 6 to 12 inches below the existing ground surface at the locations shown
on the Site Plan Plate 1 Results of the laboratory tests are presented on Plates 2
and 3
Based on the laboratory test results and engineering analyses it is our opinion
Area B of our soils report may be modified as follows The 24 inch non expansive
