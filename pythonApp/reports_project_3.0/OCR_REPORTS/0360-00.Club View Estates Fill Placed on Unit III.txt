DISCUSSION
All deleterious material such as Lumber logs brush roots
and rubbish were disposed of offsite prior to placing fill
All loose and compressible materials were removed to firm
natural ground or bedrock in areas to receive fill
Surfaces to receive fill were scarified and bladed to a
depth of one foot watered and mixed as required to achieve
optimum moisture conditions and compacted to at least 85
of maximum density with a Pactor type roller
Fill consisting of onsite materials having the above soil
classifications were placed in six inch thick horizontal
lifts watered as required to achieve optimum moisture
conditions and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of
maximum density with a Pactor type roller
Field density tests were taken at vertical intervals of
approximately two feet Areas of fill which were found to
contain insufficient moisture and or failing tests were
reworked until passing test results were obtained
Fill placed on surfaces having a slope gradient steeper than
5 1 were keyed and benched into firm material in accordance
with recommendations of the above referenced preliminary
report

Compaction on the face of fill slopes was achieved by track
rolling with a D 8 dozer also by filling full and cutting
back
The transition zone of fill over cut slopes was stripped
of all topsoil prior to placing fill
Transition lots were overcut and refilled with compacted
fill to provide a uniform pad bearing surface and to reduce
differential movement
This office assumes no responsibility for any alterations
made to the slopes or pads on the subject tract subsequent
to the issuance of this report without our knowledge and
written approval All ramps made through slopes and pads
and other areas of disturbance which require the placement
of compacted fill to restore them to the original condition
will not be certified unless such backfilling operations
are performed under our inspection and supervision and
certified compacted slopes shall negate our certification
of slope face compaction unless material is removed in _
accordance with our instructions
