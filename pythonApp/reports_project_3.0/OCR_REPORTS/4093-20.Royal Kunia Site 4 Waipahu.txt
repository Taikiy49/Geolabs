This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations performed during
the construction of special foundations at Lot 20 21 and 22 of the Royat
Kunia Site 4 Subdivision The special foundations at Lot and 6 were constructed
in September of 1999 The special foundations at Lot 20 21 and 22 were
constructed in January of 2000
The subject site is located in the Royal Kunia Site 4 Subdivision in the District of
Waipahu on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The subject site is bounded by Kupuohi Street to the
east Kunia Road to the west Wal Mart building to the south and a drainageway to the north
Due to differential and total settlement concerns arising from fills up to about 40 feet in
thickness we have recommended that five lots Lot 20 21 and 22 be constructed
with structural mat foundations Due to the retaining walls being adjacent to or in
close proximity to the proposed house locations we have recommended that three lots
Lot and 6 be constructed with pipe pile foundations for footings within the influence
zone of the retaining wall
The drilled shaft construction for Lot and 6 was performed by Foundations
International Inc The concrete was supplied by Island Ready Mix Concrete Inc Eleven
yards of concrete with a slump of 7 2 inches and a design compressive strength of
3 000 pounds per square inch psi was utilized within the pipe piles and within the annulus
created by the pipe piles and drilled shafts The observations made during the drilled shaft
construction are summarized as follows
