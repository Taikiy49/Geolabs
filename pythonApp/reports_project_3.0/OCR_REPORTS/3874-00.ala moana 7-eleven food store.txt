Geotechnical Engineering Evaluation
Proposed Ala Moana 7 Eleven Food Store
Honolulu Oahu Hawaii
In accordance with your request this letter presents our findings of the
geotechnical engineering evaluation for the proposed Ala Moana 7 Eleven Food Store
Geolabs Hawaii previously conducted a geotechnical exploration at the subject site for a
proposed new Chevron station Our evaluation of the current project is based on the
subsurface information previously obtained and recommendations presented in our
geotechnical engineering report dated
Project Considerations
The project site is located at the northeastern corner of Ala Moana Boulevard and
Queen Street The site is presently occupied by a Chevron service station The project
site is generally level with exisiting ground surface elevations ranging from approximately
4 5 to 5 5 feet Mean Sea Level MSL It is proposed to construct a new 7 Eleven
store at the northeastern corner of the site The new building will be a one story structure
with concrete slab on grade
Evaluation Findings
Based on the subsurface information previously obatined and presented in our
report dated the project site is generally underlain by dense granular
fills followed by loose to medium dense sands and gravels extending to depths of about

Following review of the transmitted project plans dated and
specifications dated September 1997 it is our opinion that the previous building
foundation recommendations presented in our report are still applicable for the current
project
In general the project plans and specifications appear to reflect the
recommendations presented in our report with the exception of the following items
1 In Plan Sheet No C 3 Detail 1 for concrete slab the 24 non expansive
material under the slab should be omitted since it is not in conformance
with our recommendations and details shown in the foundation plans
2 In Plan Sheet No S 2 our recommendation A 2 inch layer of moist fine
sand be placed above the impermeable membrane prior to placing the slab
concrete The sand should be moistened slightly prior to placing concrete
is not included
3 In page 02200 2 of the specification Item G Cushion fill shall be of
minimum four inch compacted thickness and compacted to 95 maximum
density should read as Cushion fill shall be of minimum four inch
compacted thickness
4 In page 02513 2 of the specification Item C Compact base course 90
and 6 thick minimum should read as Compact base course 95 and 6
thick minimum
