This letter presents a brief description and the results of the
field friction tests conducted in accordance with your request at the
above location
Introduction
The purpose of the tests was to examine the frictional resistances
of a selected rubber slipper on the existing pedestrial traffic surfaces
at the three locations selected by you A sketch of the test set up is
shown on Plate 1

Test Locations
The first location identified as Point A was outside the store
door on a slightly inclined ramp surface dropping vertically about 1 5
inches over a horizontal distance of 15 5 inches For this inclined
location frictional tests were run in both the up slope and down slope
directions The second location identified as Point B was in front of
Celebrity Formals near a column and on a level walkway The third
location identified as Point C was inside the doorway of the vacant
store and was tested downhill on the anti skid pads of the ramp surface
