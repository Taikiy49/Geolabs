APPENDIX A
Preliminary Slope Evaluation and Rating Forms
This Volume No 2 book is provided as a supplemental reference for the
Rockslide Potential Inspections project for the City and County of Honolulu This book
contains the site specific Preliminary Slope Evaluation and Rating Forms that document
the existing hillslope site conditions which were observed and form the basis of the
preliminary slope hazard rating
The Preliminary Slope Evaluation and Rating Form was created to assist in the
systematic recording of general site information that supports the preliminary slope
hazard rating The Preliminary Slope Rating A B or C is based on the information
collected at the site with respect to answering the primary question of the estimated
potential for rockfall on the roadway and the secondary question regarding the
historical rockfall activity at the site
Class A refers to an estimated high potential for rockfall on the roadway Class
B refers to an estimated moderate potential for rockfall on the roadway and Class C
refers to an estimated low potential for rockfall on the roadway
A more detailed description regarding the performance of the preliminary slope
rating and the criteria used in the slope rating is contained in Section 2 2 and
Section of Volume No 1 of this report

Limitations
It should be noted that the general information contained on the Preliminary
Slope Evaluation and Rating Form was recorded at the time of our site visit and
therefore may not contain information pertaining to changes in the site conditions that
may have occurred following our site visit The information is based on visual
observations and some generalizations regarding the observed site conditions The
preliminary slope assessment should not take the place of a detailed slope assessment
or other survey
