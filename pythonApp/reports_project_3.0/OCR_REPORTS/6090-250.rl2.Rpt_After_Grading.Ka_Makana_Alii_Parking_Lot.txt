PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Ka Makana Ali i development approximately 67 acres is located in the East
Kapolei area in the District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is
bounded approximately by Kapolei Parkway to the north Roosevelt Avenue to the south
the proposed Phase II North South Road Extension to the east and a drainage channel
and Kapolei Middle School to the west
Based on the information provided we understand that it is desired to expand the
existing parking lot on the east side of the property next to the theater parking lot Fill
materials on the order of about 30 000 cubic yards will be placed to raise the existing
grades by about 5 to 7 feet to achieve the design finished grades

EARTHWORK OPERATIONS
Royal Contracting Co Ltd performed the site grading work which consisted of
subgrade preparation placement and compaction of fills and backfills pavement
construction and utility trench excavation and backfill
Following the clearing and grubbing operations the exposed subgrades were
scarified to a depth of about 12 inches moisture conditioned to above the optimum
moisture and recompacted to a minimum of 90 percent relative compaction The
compaction requirements for finished subgrades subjected to vehicular traffic such as the
pavement subgrade was increased to a minimum of 95 percent relative compaction
Relative compaction refers to the in place dry density of soil expressed as a percentage of
the maximum dry density of the same soil established in accordance with ASTM D1557
Optimum moisture is the water content percentage by dry weight corresponding to the
maximum dry density
Fill and backfill materials required for the project were placed in level lifts not
exceeding 8 inches in loose thickness moisture conditioned to above the optimum
moisture content and compacted to at least 90 percent relative compaction The
compaction requirements for finished subgrades subjected to vehicular traffic such as the
pavement subgrade was increased to a minimum of 95 percent relative compaction
During the site grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials
utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing
The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our
laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D1557 Modified Proctor and the test
results are summarized on Plate 2
Field density tests were performed during the fill and backfill operations on an
as needed basis to confirm our field observations as variations in construction procedures
and soil conditions were noted A total of 178 field density tests were performed using the
nuclear probe method in accordance with ASTM D6938 Results of the compaction tests
performed are summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 13 of this report These tests were
performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at
the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from
on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be considered accurate only
to the degree implied by the methods used
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the earthwork operations during the site grading
pavement and utility line construction for the above project observed and tested by
Geolabs in the period covered by this report were performed in general accordance with
the intent of the project plans and specifications and our geotechnical recommendations
presented in the above referenced geotechnical engineering report and
subsequent recommendations
Kapolei Hawaii Property Company LLC
