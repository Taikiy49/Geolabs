Dear Mr Schroeder
It has come to our attention that the currently anticipated limits of the
undermining beneath the building will be much less than first anticipated In addition
due to utility obstructions installment of piers at the previously recommended locations
may not be possible without temporary removal of the utilities Finally due to cost
constraints Safeway Inc has determined that the Atlas piers recommended for the
electrical transformer pad will not be installed
Based on the above constraints we understand that it is desired to install only
two Atlas piers at the following locations
1 Eastern side of undermining location and
2 Southern side of undermining location between the stub out of the water
line through the wall and the stub out of the drainline below the footing
Based on the high shrinkage potential of the soil and the anticipated high vertical
cuts adjacent to the footings the actual unsupported footing span may be greater than
just between the Atlas pier and the lateral extent of the excavation In addition
cantilevering of the unsupported corner of the building may cause additional stresses
and movement of the building to occur Therefore we believe that a 2 Atlas pier
underpinning system will entail significant risks
For the same reason as stated in the above paragraph we still believe that an
Atlas pier should be installed under the transformer pad Although the potential for
damage to the transformer is low we believe that since this whole project began with a
PCB spill from a transformer underpinning of this transformer is prudent

Recommendations
We recommend that at a minimum three Atlas piers be installed at the following
locations
One Atlas pier at the corner of the Safeway building
One Atlas pier at the corner of the transformer concrete pad and the
Safeway building
e One Atlas pier west of the drainline within the western concrete pad
To accommodate the above pier locations the existing water line near the corner
of the building could be rerouted temporarily While we are waiting for the fabrication
and air freight of the Atlas plate connectors excavation through the concrete slab could
be conducted to allow for the installation of the Atlas pier
If the excavation limits and depths increase we should be notified as additional
Atlas piers may be necessary Attached is a sketch showing the anticipated lateral
extents of the excavation Note that if there is some uncertainty of the extents it may
be prudent to install more Atlas piers prior to excavation since accessibility to the pier
location should be easier and since there will be no remobilization cost by the
contractor
