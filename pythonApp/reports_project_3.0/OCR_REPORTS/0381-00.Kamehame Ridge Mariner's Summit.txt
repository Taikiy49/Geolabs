All cuts should be inspected by the project geologist or
soils engineer during and upon completion of grading to
determine whether the as graded condition 1s in accordance
with the above recommendations
All structural fills should be placed under the supervision
of the project soils engineer High fills if to be con
structed of tuff materials should be investigated indi
vidually for stability
Adequate provisions for erosion control should be included
during and after grading
Sliver fills as apparently proposed in Alternate 3
where requested by Compla Corporation are not recommended
Since they will be inherently unstable Stabilization of
fills on slopes is generally achieved by adequately keying
Sand benching the proposed fills into firm underlying natural
materials However since the underlying materials are
basically hard basalt bedrock in numerous areas adequate
keying and benching of the fills will be probably econom
ically unfeasible Therefore it is recommended that the
presently proposed sliver fill areas be left in a natural
state and building pads be designed by daylighting the
ridge where appropriate
