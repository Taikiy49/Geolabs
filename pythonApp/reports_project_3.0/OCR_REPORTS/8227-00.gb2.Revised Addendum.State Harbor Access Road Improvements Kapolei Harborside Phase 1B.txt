Based on the traffic information provided, the Equivalent Single Axle Loads
(ESALs) for Lauwiliwili Street based on a 40-year design life that was used in our
pavement analyses is about 81,681,535. Due to the high volume of traffic, the pavement
section should consist of an asphalt concrete base and base course below the surface
course.
Based on the design traffic parameters and the estimated ESALs, we recommend
utilizing the following flexible pavement section for the design.
Lauwiliwili Street
3.0-Inch Asphaltic Concrete
4.0-Inch Asphalt Concrete Base (92 Percent Max Theoretical Specific Gravity)
8.0-Inch Aggregate Base Course (95 Percent Relative Compaction)
15.0-Inch Total New Pavement Thickness
