Lateral Earth Pressure
Hanapepe Drain Project
At your request we have reviewed our lateral earth pressure recommendations for the
design of box culverts and headwall structures at the above referenced project
Based on the subsoils information obtained from our field exploration it appears that the
three proposed box culverts and headwall structures will probably be resting against the in situ
sandy deposits Therefore it is our opinion that a lateral earth pressure of 50 p c f equivalent
fluid pressure may be used for level backfill against walls restrained at the top at rest condition
This value is based on the assumption that 3B Fine gravel will be used as the required backfill
materials and also that the site soils will be sandy materials However additional loads due to
hydrostatic pressure and vehicular traffic should also be considered where applicable
