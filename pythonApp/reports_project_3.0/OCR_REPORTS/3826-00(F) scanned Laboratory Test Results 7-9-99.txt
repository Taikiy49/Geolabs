Dear Mr Leong
In accordance with our Blanket Purchase Agreement Soil Rock Testing with the
laboratory tests requested in Call No 8 Based on the request we have completed the
following laboratory tests and are transmitting the test results to you for your use Preliminary
test results have been previously transmitted to your office
The laboratory tests performed included the determination of natural moisture content
Atterberg Limits consolidation unconsolidated undrained triaxial compression California
Bearing Ratio CBR and Modified Proctor compaction tests The results of the tests
performed are summarized on the Summary of Laboratory Test Results Table 1
Five moisture content ASTM D 2216 determinations were performed on selected soil
samples as an aid in the classification and evaluation of soil properties The results of these
tests are presented on the Summary of Laboratory Test Results
One Atterberg Limits test ASTM D 4318 was performed on a selected sample of the
soils to evaluate the liquid and plastic limits and to aid in soil classification Results of the test
are summarized on the Summary of Laboratory Test Results Table 1 Graphic presentation
of the test results is provided on Plate A 1
Two consolidation tests ASTM D 2435 were performed on selected soil samples to
evaluate the compressibility characteristics of the soils The tests were performed on the
relatively undisturbed on site soils The test results and consolidation curves are presented
on Plates A 2 and A 3

Two Unconsolidated Undrained Triaxial Compression tests ASTM D 2850 were
performed to evaluate the undrained shear strength of the clayey soils The tests were
performed on relatively undisturbed soil samples The effective overburden pressures were
used as confining pressures as requested by your office The test results and stress strain
curves are presented on Plates A 4 and A 5
One Modified Proctor test ASTM D 1557 was performed on the bulk sample P 1 to
evaluate the relationship between the moisture content and the dry density of the soils
Results of the test are presented on Plate A 6
One California Bearing Ratio CBR test ASTM D 1883 was performed on the same
bulk sample to evaluate the strength characteristics for pavement subgrade support Results
of the CBR test are presented on Plate A 7
We appreciate the opportunity to provide our laboratory testing services to you on this
project If you have questions or need additional information please contact our office
