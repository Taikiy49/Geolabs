SOIL REQUIREMENTS
1 SOFT AND YIELDING AREAS ENCOUNTERED DURING CLEARING AND GRUBBING BELOW ANY AREAS
DESIGNATED TO RECEIVE FILL SHOULD BE OVER EXCAVATED TO EXPOSE FIRM MATERIAL AND THE
RESULTING EXCAVATION SHALL BE BACKFILLED WITH WELL COMPACTED ENGINEERED FILL
2 WHERE SOFT AND WET SOILS ARE ENCOUNTERED THEY SHALL BE REMOVED TO EXPOSE THE
UNDERLYING FIRM SOILS PRIOR TO PLACEMENT OF FILL MATERIALS
3 OVER EXCAVATED SUBGRADES OR AREAS DESIGNATED TO RECEIVE FILLS SHALL BE SCARIFIED 10 A
DEPTH OF ABOUT 8 INCHES MOISTURE CONDITIONED TO AT LEAST 2 PERCENT ABOVE OPTIMUM
MOISTURE AND COMPACTED TO A MINIMUM OF 90 PERCENT MAXIMUM DRY DENSITY IN ACCORDANCE
WITH ASTM D 1557 91
4 THE ON SITE SOILS GENERATED FROM THE CUT AREAS MAY BE USED AS FILL MATERIAL UP TO THE
BOTTOM OF THE NON EXPANSIVE CAPPING LAYER THESE ON SITE SOILS SHALL NOT BE USED AS
SELECT GRANULAR FILL MATERIAL RECOMMENDED BELOW SLABS ON GRADES
9 ALL FILLS AND BACKFILL SHALL BE MOISTURE CONDITIONED TO AT LEAST 2 PERCENT ABOVE OPTIMUM
MOISTURE PLACED IN LEVEL LIFTS NOT EXCEEDING 8 INCHES IN LOOSE THICKNESS AND GOMPACTED
TO A MINIMUM OF 90 PERCENT MAXIMUM DRY DENSITY
6 THE 12 INCH NON EXPANSIVE CAPPING LAYER SHALL CONSIST OF NON EXPANSIVE SELECT
GRANULAR MATERIAL SUCH AS CRUSHED CORAL MUDROCK BASALT OR CINDER SAND THE MATERIAL
SHALL BE WELL GRADED FROM COARSE TO FINE WITH NO PARTICLES LARGER THAN 3 INCHES IN
LARGEST DIMENSION AND SHALL CONTAIN BETWEEN 10 AND 30 PERCENT PARTICLES PASSING THE
NO 200 SIEVE THE MATERIAL SHALL HAVE A LABORATORY CBR VALUE OF 20 OR MORE AND
SHALL HAVE A MAXIMUM SWELL OF 1 PERCENT OR LESS ALL NON EXPANSIVE SELECT GRANULAR
FILL SHALL BE TESTED BY THE CONTRACTOR AT NO ADDITIONAL COST TO THE STATE AND APPROVED
BY THE PROJECT SOILS ENGINEER PRIOR TO DELIVERY TO THE PROJECT SITE FOR ITS INTENDED USE
SELECTED GRANULAR FILL MATERIALS SHALL BE MOISTURE CONDITIONED TO ABOVE THE OPTIMUM
MOISTURE PLACED IN LEVEL LIFTS NOT EXCEEDING 8 INCHES IN LOOSE THICKNESS AND COMPACTED
TO A MINIMUM OF 95 PERCENT MAXIMUM DRY DENSITY
W O 1997 PLATE 1
7 ALL FILLS TO BE PLACED ON SLOPES STEEPER THAN 5H 1V SHALL BE KEYED AND BENCHED INTO THE
EXISTING SLOPE
8 THE FILLING OPERATIONS SHALL BEGIN AT THE LOWEST POINT AND CONTINUE UP IN LEVEL
HORIZONTAL COMPACTED LAYERS
9 FILL SLOPES SHALL BE CONSTRUCTED BY OVERFILLING AND CUTTING BACK TO THE DESIGN SLOPE
RATIO
10 WHERE SHRINKAGE CRACKS ARE NOTED AFTER COMPACTION OF THE SUBGRADE THE SOILS SHALL
BE RE SCARIFIED AND PREPARED AS RECOMMENDED OR BE THOROUGHLY MOISTENED TO CLOSE
ALL CRACKS
11 SATURATION AND SUBSEQUENT YIELDING OF THE EXPOSED SUBGRADE DUE TO INCLEMENT WEATHER
AND POOR DRAINAGE SHALL BE OVER EXCAVATED AND REPLACED WITH COMPACTED ENGINEERED FILL
AT NO ADDITIONAL COST TO THE STATE
12 FOR FOOTINGS BEARING ON THE UNDISTURBED IN SITU STIFF SOILS THE BOTTOM OF THE FOOTING
EXCAVATIONS SHALL BE COMPACTED TO PROVIDE A RELATIVELY FIRM AND SMOOTH BEARING SURFACE
PRIOR TO THE PLACEMENT OF REINFORCING STEEL OR CONCRETE IF SOFT AND OR LOOSE
MATERIALS ARE ENCOUNTERED AT THE BOTTOM OF FOOTING EXCAVATIONS THEY SHALL BE OVER
EXCAVATED TO EXPOSE THE UNDERLYING FIRM MATERIALS THE OVER EXCAVATION SHALL BE
BACKFILLED WITH SELECT GRANULAR MATERIAL COMPACTED TO A MINIMUM OF 95 PERCENT RELATIVE
COMPACTION OR THE FOOTING BOTTOM BE EXTENDED DOWN TO THE UNDERLYING COMPETENT MATERIAL
FOR FOOTINGS BEARING ON NEW ENGINEERED FILLS THE BOTTOM OF THE FOOTING EXCAVATIONS
SHALL BE RECOMPACTED TO A MINIMUM OF 95 PERCENT RELATIVE COMPACTION PRIOR TO THE
PLACEMENT OF REINFORCING STEEL OR CONCRETE
13 PAVEMENT SUBGRADES SHALL BE SCARIFIED TO A DEPTH OF ABOUT 8 INCHES MOISTURED CONDITIONED
TO AT LEAST 2 PERCENT ABOVE THE OPTIMUM MOISTURE AND COMPACTED TO A MINIMUM OF 95
PERCENT RELATIVE COMPACTION COMPACTION REQUIREMENT FOR PEDESTRIAN WALKWAYS SUBGRADES
SHALL BE A MINIMUM OF 90 PERCENT RELATIVE COMPACTION
W O 1997 PLATE 2
14 THE AREAS ADJACENT TO THE SLABS SHALL BE BACKFILLED TIGHTLY AGAINST THE SLABS WTH NON
EXPANSIVE IMPERVIOUS SOILS AND GRADED TO DIVERT WATER AWAY FROM THE SLABS TO REDUCE THE
POTENTIAL FOR WATER PONDING AROUND THE SLABS AND FOUNDATIONS
W O 1997 PLATE 3
