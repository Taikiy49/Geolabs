Compaction Testing Report
Villages of Kapolei
Mass Grading at Village 7
Kapolei Oahu Hawaii
This report summarizes the results of our compaction testing services performed
during the earthwork and grading operations at the above referenced project site from
through
Site Grading
The project site is located in the Kapolei area on the island of Oahu Hawaii A
general layout of the project site is presented on the Site Plan Plate 1 The grading
work was performed by Oahu Construction Company Ltd
Prior to fill placement at the project site the existing ground was cleared and
grubbed of vegetation construction debris and uncompacted fill materials The
subgrade was then scarified to a depth of about 6 to 8 inches moisture conditioned to
about 2 percent above the optimum moisture content and compacted to not less than
90 percent relative compaction
During the mass grading operations selected samples of the on site stockpiles
utilized as fill materials were obtained and returned to our laboratory for testing The
maximum dry density and optimum moisture content were determined in our laboratory
in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Results of our laboratory
tests are presented on Plate 2

In place density tests of the fills were performed periodically in general
accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear Method The test results
are presented on the attached Summary of Field Density Test Results Plates 3 1
through 3 8
Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from
on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be considered accurate
only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory
were reworked and approved by our field representative
Based on the field density tests performed by our field technician at the site the
field tests taken at the random locations tested met the requirements of the project
plans and specifications
