services performed during site grading and roadway and utility construction for the Royal

PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the eastern portion of the Royal Kunia
Development Area in the District of Kunia on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site
is bounded by Anonui Street on the east and is south of the Royal Kunia Site 10
development area The location of the project site is shown on the Project Location Map
Plate 1 The project site includes a total of 59 residential lots and three roadways The
site grading work generally consisted of cuts and fills of less than 7 feet The grading plan
for the site is shown on the Site Plan Plate 2
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The site grading and utility installation work for the Royal Kunia Site 9 project was
observed by Geolabs Inc from March 20 2001 to The site grading work
consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills up to about
7 feet in thickness to achieve the design finished grades for ihe project The site grading
and utility backfilling work was performed by Royal Contracting Company Ltd During the
site grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and
