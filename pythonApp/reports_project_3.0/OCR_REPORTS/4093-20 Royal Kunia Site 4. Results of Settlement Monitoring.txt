Dear Mr Miyazaki
The purpose of this letter is to summarize the results of the settlement monitoring
program and provide our recommendations regarding the house construction at Lot Nos 7
and 22 for this project
Based on the settlement readings it appears that virtually no settlement has occurred
since the installation of the settlement monuments It is likely that some settlement occurred
during the fill placement period and prior to the installation of the settlement monuments The
settlement readings also suggest that the soil has little to no tendency to collapse under water
infiltration Note that the filling process was completed in the latter part of July 1999 and
installation of the settlement monuments proceeded in the latter part of September 1999
Since the settlement readings indicated virtually no settlement for 3 months and during
the rainy season we believe that construction of the homes at Lot 20 21 and 22
may proceed at this time If desired the settlement monuments may be removed at this time
Note however if the settlement monument is located below the house structure slabs or
pavements it should be left in place
We do recommend that and our office be notified in advance of the
construction of the special structural mat foundations in order that site visits may be made
We request that we be allowed to make a site visit after the ground is properly prepared and
prior to rebar placement Proper construction of the foundations and slabs are critical for
these lots
