Earthwork Observation and Compaction Testing Report

Geiger Road Utility Crossings
Dear Mr Young
This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations performed during
the utility trench backfilling operations for Geiger Road Utility Crossings as part of the Ewa by
Gentry Makai West project from May 1998 through August 1998
The subject site is located on the west end of Geiger Road in Ewa on the Island of
Oahu Hawaii The site is bounded by the Coral Creek Golf Course Clubhouse to the south
The electrical trench backfilling work was performed by Paul s Electric Service The
sewer and water trench backfilling work was performed by Royal Contracting Company Ltd
The fill materials were generally generated from the trench excavations The available fill
clay with coral sand and gravel and import base course material
The backfill material was brought to near optimum moisture content and compacted
to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as determined in accordance with
ASTM Test Method D 1557 The base course and the upper 2 feet of backfill material was
compacted to a minimum of 95 percent of the maximum dry density
During the grading period various on site soils were tested prior to use in the field The
maximum dry density and optimum moisture content relationships were established in our
laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and
the test results are summarized on Plate 2 of this report
During the fill placement operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with the American Society of Testing and Materials ASTM Test
Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests are
summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 4 of this report
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil conditions exposed during the
above grading operations
