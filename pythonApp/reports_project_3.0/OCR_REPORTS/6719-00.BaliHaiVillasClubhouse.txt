This report presents the results of a soils investigation at the site of the proposed Bali Hai
Villas Clubhouse located at 4970 Pepelani Loop in
A summary of the findings is as follows
1 Four 4 test borings were drilled to depths of 21 5 to 26 5 feet below the existing
grade and eight 8 dynamic cone penetration dcp test were performed to depths of
10 to 13 feet below the existing grade The locations of the explorations are shown
on the Plot Plan Plate 2
2 No groundwater was encountered in the test borings
3 The consistency of the subsurface soils varied from one exploration to another
Some of the subsurface soils were found to be soft to very soft As a result of our
recent findings in this soils investigation the following changes should be made to the
original soils report entitled Bali Hai Villas Buildings Clubhouse dated
The changes should apply only to the clubhouse and swimming pool
A Soil Bearing Capacity
B At the swimming pool Plate 1 shows a recommended zone of over excavation
in the pool This over excavation zone means the subgrade soils should be
overexcavated 36 inches below bottom of concrete and then the subgrade soil
should be proofrolled with a compactor weighing not less than 10 000 pounds
Following compaction of the subgrade soil the resulting depression should
then be backfilled with the on site material that was excavated The backfill
should be placed in 6 thick compacted lifts The backfill material should be
compacted to a minimum of 90 of the maximum dry density note that the
pool designer recommends 90 compaction of the subgrade soil
For the remainder of the pool the subgrade soil should be proofrolled with a
compacior weighing not less than 10 000 pounds The compaction shall
continue until a firm unyielding surface has been achieved as determined by
the project geotechnical engineer In the event that soft areas are encountered
during the compaction process that can not be properly compacted the soft
