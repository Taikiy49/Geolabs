Additional Soil Engineering Evaluation
Proposed 1 0 M G Reservoir
Tuluku 500 Water System
Kaneohe Oahu Hawaii
This report presents the results of our additional soil engineering
evaluation performed for the proposed reservoir
A soil exploration program including borings was originally planned
at the site However due to wet and muddy conditions along the existing
access road and right of entry difficulties a drill rig could not be
mobilized to the site during the design phase of the project
Subsequent to discussions with the Board of Water Supply it was
decided to conduct a site reconnaissance with hand augered probings at the
tank site to provide preliminary soil data for use in the site grading and
foundation design of the project Borings would then be drilled when
access waS available at the commencement of the site grading construction
to verify the preliminary recommendations Provisions would be made in
the contract documents to allow the necessary revisions to the plans
Following site clearing by the contractor we recently drilled two
borings at the reservoir site at the approximate locations shown on the
attached Site Plan
The borings were drilled to depths of 26 0 and 41 0 feet below the
existing ground surface
The soil conditions encountered in the borings were similar to those
indicated by our previous probings Therefore the recommendations
presented in our previous Preliminary Soil Engineering Evaluation report
of are applicable
