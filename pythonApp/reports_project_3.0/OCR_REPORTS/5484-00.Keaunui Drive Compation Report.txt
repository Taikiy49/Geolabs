This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during site grading and roadway and utility construction for the
Keaunui Drive Extension Station No 16 44 to Station No 21 28 project from

PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project involves site grading and utility installation for the Keaunui Drive
Extension from Station No 16 44 to Station No 21 28 The site is east of Fort Weaver
Road south of Iroquois Point Road and north of the Hawaii Prince Golf Course in the
District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The roadway extension covers a distance
of about 484 linear feet and includes the intersection with Road B A Project Location
Map and a Site Plan are shown on Plates 1 and 2 1 and 2 2 respectively
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
Geolabs observed the site grading and utility installation work from
to March 13 2006 The site grading work consisted of clearing and grubbing and
placement and compaction of fills on the order of about 3 feet thick to achieve the design
finished grades Delta Construction Corporation performed the site grading and utility
installation work During the site grading operations samples of the on site and imported
materials utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office
for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established
in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test
results are summarized on Plate 3

Hawaii California

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method
Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 19 of
this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations
should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas
assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was
obtained and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and
hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests our opinion is that the site grading and utility installation work for the
above project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were
performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the following
