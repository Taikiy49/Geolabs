REMARKS In accordance with your request we have reviewed the rock anchor
installation qualifications and working drawings and data submittals provided
by CMZ of Hawaii Inc for the Seismic Retrofit of Various Bridges Vicinity of
Pepeekeo Unit Il project The project is also known as Kolekole Bridge
The purpose of this review is to render an opinion regarding the
conformance of the submittals to the design concepts and contract
requirements Based on our review of the contractor s qualifications and
working drawings and data submittals we offer the following comments
1 It is not clear what the roles of Messrs Gilbert Caron Rock Anchor
Superintendent and Edward Minkel Managing Director were on the
projects which the Contractor has submitted as project experience
The role of the rock anchor superintendent for the proposed project
should be provided for each project listed in the rock anchor
qualifications
2 The names and telephone numbers of a reference person for each
project listed as project experience in the submittal should be
provided for reference
3 The post grouting procedures including hydraulic pressures to
fracture the grout bulb and pumping pressures to provide permeation
of the grout into the ground should be described and provided in the
submittal
4 A shop drawing showing details of the manufacturer s rock anchor
package should be provided The shop drawing should include the
thickness of the epoxy coating 8 mil thickness should be adequate
and corrugated sheathing proposed The detail provided in the
