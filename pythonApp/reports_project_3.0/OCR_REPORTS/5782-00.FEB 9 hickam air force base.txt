One test boring is required at each proposed GV speaker tower location for a
total of three test borings at Hickam Air Force Base The test borings are required to be
drilled with a minimum 2 inch diameter auger extending down to about 20 feet below
the existing ground surface Standard Penetration tests are to be performed at 2 foot
intervals in accordance with ASTM D 1586
FIELD EXPLORATION
The field exploration program consisted of drilling and sampling one boring each
at the three sites for a total of three test borings Arrowhead Contracting Inc located
the test borings in the field We drilled the test borings using a truck mounted drill rig
equipped with continuous flight solid stem augers
Our geologist classified the materials encountered in the test borings by visual
and textural examination in the field and monitored the drilling operations on a
near continuous basis Soils were classified in general conformance with the Unified
Soil Classification System as shown on Plate 3 Graphic representations of the
materials encountered are presented on the Logs of Borings Plates 4 through 6
Samples were obtained from the drilled borings in general accordance with
ASTM D 1586 Penetration Test and Split Barrel Sampling of Soils by driving a 2 inch
OD standard penetration sampler with a 140 pound hammer falling 30 inches The blow
counts needed to drive the sampler the second and third 6 inches of an 18 inch drive
are shown as the Penetration Resistance on the Logs of Borings at the appropriate
sample depths
TEST BORINGS
The test borings are designated as Boring through HAFB 10 and
are shown on the Site Plans Plates 2 1 through 2 3 The test borings extended to
depths of about 19 5 to 21 5 feet below the existing ground surface The subsurface
conditions encountered in the borings drilled at each site are presented in the following
subsections It should be noted that the groundwater levels encountered in the borings
are expected to vary with tidal fluctuation seasonal precipitation storm surge
conditions and other factors
We drilled Boring No HAFB 08 to a maximum depth of about 19 5 feet below the
existing ground surface We encountered a layer of fill which consisted of medium
dense silty sand to a depth of about 3 feet below the existing ground surface We
encountered a 2 foot thick layer of soft to medium hard siltstone below the fill overlying
alluvium that consisted of medium stiff to stiff silt extending to a depth of about 7 feet
below the existing ground surface Below the alluvium we encountered weathered
sandstone medium dense silty sand to a depth of 11 feet below the existing ground

LABORATORY TESTING
We tested selected soil samples from the field exploration in our laboratory with
an emphasis on the classification of the soils The following laboratory tests were
performed on selected soil samples obtained from the field exploration
Moisture Content ASTM D 2216 determinations were performed on selected
soil samples as an aid in the classification and evaluation of soil properties The test
results are presented on the Logs of Borings at the appropriate sample depths
One Atterberg Limits test ASTM D 4318 was performed on selected soil samples
to evaluate the liquid and plastic limits and to aid in soil classification The test results are
summarized on the Logs of Borings at the appropriate sample depths Graphic
presentation of the test results is provided on Plate 7
