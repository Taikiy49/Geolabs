Dear Mr Chee
We understand that the slabs on grade for the new warehouse building will be
subjected to vehicular traffic during construction of the warehouse In the Slabs On
Grade section of the above referenced report we recommended a minimum 4 inch thick
layer of open graded gravel ASTM C 33 No 67 gradation below the slabs based on
interior slabs that will not be subjected to vehicular traffic Because the slabs will be
subjected to vehicular traffic we recommend that a minimum 6 inch thick layer of select
borrow subbase be placed below the slabs in lieu of the open graded gravel to provide
uniform support The select borrow subbase should consist of crushed basaltic
aggregate compacted to a minimum of 95 percent relative compaction
In general we also recommend that the concrete slab be designed for the
intended loads For design of structural slabs a modulus of subgrade reaction of
300 pounds per square inch per inch of deflection pci may be used for the compacted
select borrow subbase minimum 95 percent relative compaction In addition provisions
should be made for proper load transfer across the slab joints subjected to vehicular
traffic The slab subgrades should be prepared in accordance with the recommendations
presented in the Site Grading section of the above referenced report
