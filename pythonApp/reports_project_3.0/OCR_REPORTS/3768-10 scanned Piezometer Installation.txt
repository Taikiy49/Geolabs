In accordance with your request we installed four piezometers and monitored
water levels at the subject site This letter report summarizes the data collected from
each monitoring well
Four Geokon piezometers were installed in the existing monitoring wells at the
Hawaii Metals Recycling facility in Campbell Industrial Park to monitor the water level
while one Geokon low pressure piezometer was used to monitor barometric pressure
for barometric pressure correction The monitoring was conducted for a period of
approximately 3 days under the direction of Mr Stuart Cotton from Cotton Consulting
Inc Following the monitoring period data stored in the datalogers were retrieved and
processed The results of the water monitoring are as shown on Plates 1 thru 4
