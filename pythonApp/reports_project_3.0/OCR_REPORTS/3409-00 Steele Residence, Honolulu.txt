Sheet S 6 Detail 1
Since the garage slab will be subjected to vehicular traffic it is our opinion
that compacted base course material in lieu of self compacting basaltic
termite barrier BTB should be used under the concrete slab to reduce the
potential for future slab cracking under repeated dynamic loadings
Ifthe use of BTB material is unavoidable for termite protection purposes we
recommend that the garage driveway slabs be designed as structural slabs
to reduce the potential for slab cracking due to the densification of the BTB
material under repeated dynamic loading from the vehicular traffic For
design of structural slabs a modulus of subgrade reaction of 75 to 100
pounds per square inch per inch of deflection p c i may be used for the
BTB material The BTB material should be tamped with a vibratory plate
compactor prior to the placement of reinforcing steel and concrete
