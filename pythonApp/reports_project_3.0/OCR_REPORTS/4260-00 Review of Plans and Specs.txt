In accordance with your transmittal letter dated August 12 we have reviewed the
set of Plans and Specifications for the above referenced project transmitted to our office
Our review included the Civil Plans Sheets C 1 through C 26 and Structural Plans
Specifications for the subject project
The purpose of this review is to render an opinion regarding the conformance of
the plans and specifications reviewed to the general intent of the recommendations
presented in our report Our review was generally limited to the geotechnical aspects of
the plans and specifications
It is our opinion that the plans and specifications reviewed generally reflect the
intent of the recommendations presented in our geotechnical engineering exploration
report prepared for the subject project The following comments are provided for your
consideration
1 Sheet C 15 Typical Section Reservoir Perimeter Road
For the perimeter service road around the reservoir structure we
anticipate that the pavement subgrades will likely expose the basalt rock
formation as encountered in our field exploration Therefore we believe
that the 6 inches select borrow subbase course depicted in the pavermiens
section would not be necessary
