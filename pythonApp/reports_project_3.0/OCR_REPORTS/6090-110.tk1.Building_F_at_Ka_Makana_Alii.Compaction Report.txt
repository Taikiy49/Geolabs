PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Ka Makana Ali i development approximately 67 acres is in the East Kapolei
area in the District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is bounded
approximately by Kapolei Parkway to the north Roosevelt Avenue to the south the
proposed Phase II North South Road Extension to the east and a drainage channel and
Kapolei Middle School to the west
Based on the information provided the project will involve the development of the
property into a commercial retail center providing approximately square feet of
retail and or commercial space We understand the project will be constructed in
two separate phases The Phase 1 portion of the development will encompass about
50 acres over the northern and central portions of the property The Phase 2 portion of the
development will encompass the remaining 17 acres of the development situated over the

EARTHWORK OPERATION
The mass grading work and building pad construction for Building F at Ka Makana
Ali i were performed by Royal Contracting Company under a separate project Royal
Contracting Company also performed the footing and plumbing excavations during the
building construction described in this report
The footing bottoms for Building F were generally compacted to a minimum of 90
percent of the maximum dry density determined in accordance with ASTM D1557
Modified Proctor Free draining granular materials consisting of No 3B Fine gravel was
used for the plumbing trench backfill up to about 12 inches above the pipes to provide
adequate support around the pipes and to reduce the compaction effort on the initial
trench backfill The trench backfill was placed in 8 inch level loose lifts and mechanically
compacted to no less than 90 percent relative compaction
During the earthwork operations samples of the on site and imported materials
utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing
The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our
laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D1557 Modified Proctor and the test
results are summarized on Plate 2 of this report
Field density tests were performed during the fill and backfill operations on an
as need basis to confirm our field observations as variations in construction procedures
and soil conditions were noted A total of 36 field density tests were performed using the
nuclear probe method in accordance with ASTM D6938 Results of the compaction tests
performed are summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 3 of this report These tests were
performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills Test

in the accuracy and statistical repeatability of the tests and methods used to monitor
