Project Considerations
A retail facility for Home Depot is planned in the Pearlridge area in the vicinity of
the intersection of Moanalua Road and Kaonohi Street on the island of Oahu Hawaii
We understand that the retail center facility will consist of a retail building with a planned
area of approximately 130 500 square feet a garden center of approximately
15 000 square feet a parking area with 726 spaces and other infrastructures A total of
17 boreholes are planned to be drilled to depths varying from 10 to 25 feet below the
existing ground surface
Drilling will be performed using continuous flight augers from the surface until
refusal to augering is attained after which drilling shall be performed using the
rotary wash method of drilling Soil samples will be collected at the ground surface at
two feet below the ground surface and at each identifiable change in soil conditions but
not further apart than five feet in each of the borings Rock core samples shall be

Based on a review of the general geology and our experience in the area we

The recommended exploration program is as follows
A Building Area

B Parking Area

C Pylon Sign Location

ground surface
D Retaining Walls
Four boreholes each will be drilled to a depth of approximately 25 feet below the
existing ground surface at the retaining wall locations
In addition to the geotechnical exploration services we can provide construction
monitoring and testing services to observe site improvements including building pad
preparation footing excavation and preparation placement and compaction of fill and
backfill materials and sampling and testing of Portland cement and asphalt concrete
mixtures
Purpose and Scope
The purpose of the field exploration program is to obtain an overview of the
surface and subsurface conditions at the site to develop a soil rock data set for the
formulation of geotechnical engineering recommendations pertinent to the design
of the building foundation and pavement sections for the proposed retail facility
development The purpose of our construction observation services is to observe
