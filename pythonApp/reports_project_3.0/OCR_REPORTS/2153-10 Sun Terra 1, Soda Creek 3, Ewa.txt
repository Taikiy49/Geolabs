Compaction Report Mass Grading
Palm Court Increment 3
From April 1990 to May 1991 we observed the earthwork and grading operations
performed for the above referenced project This report summarizes our observations
and compaction tests performed at the above referenced site
The above project site is located within the western portion of the Ewa Gentry
Development in Ewa In general the fill area is designated as western half of F 2 of the
mass grading plans for the development
The grading operations at the above referenced site consisted of fills up to five 5
feet The material on site generally consisted of dark brown silty clay mixed with coral
gravel material and coralline silty sand and gravel
Prior to fill placement the existing vegetation was removed and the existing ground
scarified and recompacted The fill was then placed in horizontal lifts and compacted to
a minimum of 90 percent of its maximum dry density
