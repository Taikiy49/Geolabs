Project Considerations
The project site consists of a 500 acre site mauka of Queen Kaahumanu
Highway 3 miles south of Kona Airport on the Island of Hawaii The proposed
development involves the construction of a golf course 500 dwelling units and other
related structures Details for the proposed development were not available during the
preparation of this fee estimate
Two monitoring wells will be installed to depths of 40 and 70 feet below the
existing ground surface Drilling will be performed using rotary wash method of drilling
After drilling 2 inch diameter PVC pipes for monitoring wells will be installed in each
borehole consisting of 10 feet of casing and 30 to 60 feet of casing Surface completion
concrete pad Each well will be developed by surging and pumping We anticipate that
the site is underlain by basalt rock formation
It is our understanding that the site is not contaminated and the monitoring wells
are for long term water quality monitoring
Purpose and Scope

services In order to accomplish this we envision a program generally consisting of the
following tasks and work efforts
1 Mobilization and demobilization of a truck mounted drill rig and two
operators from Honolulu to the project site and back
2 Drilling of two boreholes to depths of approximately 40 and 70 feet below the
existing ground surface We have assumed that trail clearing will be
