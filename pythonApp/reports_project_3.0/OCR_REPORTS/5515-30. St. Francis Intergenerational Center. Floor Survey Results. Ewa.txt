Dear Mr Matsuoka
This letter provides the results of our floor level survey at the site Due to reports
of sticking doors and separations at the non bearing interior walls we conducted a floor
level survey on May 21 2014 This survey revealed that one end of the building may
have lifted about 2 inches We conducted a second floor level survey on
to see if the movements had subsided sufficienily to allow repairs of
the door and non bearing walls to proceed The second set of readings indicated that
the movements had slowed but may be continuing In the meantime we have consulted
with the Wafflemat suppliers to see if they could shed some light on possible causes of
the movements We have not been able to pinpoint probable causes yet
We recommend that permanent repairs be postponed until the floor level survey
readings indicate that the movements have stopped or significantly slowed We plan to
take another floor level survey in late March or April 2015
We would like to obtain a copy of the construction report for the Wafflemat slab
including the post tensioning records
