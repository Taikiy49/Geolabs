This letter report presents the results of our pavement coring services conducted
around the existing Moanalua Shopping Center in the Pearl Harbor area on the Island of
Oahu Hawaii The findings presented herein are intended to provide input in support of
the pavement design and evaluation for the new Moanalua Center project The findings
presented herein are subject to the limitations noted at the end of this report
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located around the existing Moanalua Shopping Center in the
vicinity of Spence Street on the makai side of Bougainville Boulevard in the Pearl Harbor
area on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The general location and vicinity of the project site are
shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
Based on the information provided we understand that it is desired to evaluate the
thickness of the existing pavement section and the type of subgrade material at selected
locations along Hennley Street Turner Avenue and the main entrance road off of
Valkenburgh Street
PURPOSE AND SCOPE
The purpose of our field exploration was to obtain an overview of the existing
pavement sections and subgrade conditions in the vicinity of the existing Moanalua
Shopping Center The subsurface information obtained is intended to provide input in
support of the pavement design and evaluation for the new Moanalua Center project The
work was performed in general accordance with our fee proposal dated

LIMITATIONS
The existing pavement sections and subgrade conditions submitted in this report
are based upon information obtained from field borings Variations of subsurface
conditions between and beyond the exploration points may occur and the nature and
extent of these variations may not become evident until construction is underway
The locations of the field borings indicated in this report are approximate having
been estimated by taping from the features shown on the Boring Location Plan provided
by Belt Collins Hawaii Ltd on The physical locations of the borings
should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used
This report has been prepared for the exclusive use of MSC LLC and other project
consultants for specific application to the Moanalua Center project in accordance with
generally accepted geotechnical engineering principles and practices No warranty is
expressed or implied
This data report has been prepared solely for the purpose of assisting the engineer
in the pavement planning and design of the proposed project Therefore this report may
not contain sufficient data or the proper information to serve as the basis for preparation
of construction cost estimates
This field exploration report was not intended to investigate the potential for
hazardous materials existing at the site The equipment techniques and personnel used
to make a geo environmental exploration differ substantially from those applied in
geotechnical engineering
