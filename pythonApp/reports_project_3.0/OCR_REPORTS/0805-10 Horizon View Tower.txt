Gentlemen
From to the production
pile driving for the above referenced project was observed
by a representative of our office
The piles consisted of 16 inch octagonal precast
prestressed concrete piles driven with a Mekiernan Terry
DE 40 diesel hammer Eighteen inch diameter holes were
drilled with an auger prior to pile driving The Dye
drilled holes extended to a depth of 21 feet to 40 feet
through the upper mudrock fill and the underlying sandy
formation The piles were generally driven to depths of
31 to 53 feet below the existing ground surface
One splice was used at Column Line G 5 The splice only
penetrated down to about eight feet below the ground sur
face and can be recovered
The locations of the piles are shown on the attached
Plate A summary of the pile driving records is attached
Based on the pile driving data and pile load test we feel
that the piles were driven to a sufficient depth to develop
the design pile load capacity
