Project Considerations
The project site is located on the northwestern corner of the town of Kilauea on the
northwestern side of Kilauea Lighthouse Road on the northern coast of the island of
Kauai Hawaii It is proposed to develop the project site as a 25 lot residential subdivision
with a duplex type housing unit to be built on each lot
We understand that the duration of the mass grading work will be for a period of
approximately 4 to 6 months and the house consiruction period will be approximately 12
months
Purpose and Scope
The soil engineering services that we anticipate for this project are as follows
1 Provide a field engineer on the project site during the mass grading and house
construction to observe and test fill placement and compaction on a full time
basis
2 Observe the exposed soil subgrade at footing excavations during the house
construction period
