Dear Ms. Hayashi: 
This letter report summarizes our geotechnical observation services performed 
during the installation and testing of the pre-production sacrificial soil nail for the 
Kamehameha Schools Kapalama Erosion Control Site 11 in the Vicinity of Kaha’opulani 
and Nae’ole Buildings project located in Honolulu on the Island of Oahu, Hawaii. 
In general, this letter report presents our observations and the results of the pre-
production soil nail installation and testing performed for the project. 
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS 
The slope stabilization project is located on the south side of Kaha’opulani and 
Nae’ole Buildings at Kamehameha Schools Kapalama campus on the Island of Oahu, 
Hawaii.  Erosion and distress were observed near the top of the slope. This project 
includes the installation of a soil-nailed slope in this area. 
PRE-PRODUCTION ANCHOR INSTALLATION AND TESTING 
The sacrificial soil nail installation and verification test were performed by Hi-Tech 
Rockfall Construction, Inc. The approximate location of the pre-production soil nail is 
shown on the Soil Nail Location Plan, Plate 1. Installation and testing of the pre-production 
soil nail were conducted from May 19 to 22, 2023.

Soil Nail Installation 
A Geolabs representative observed the drilling and grouting operations during 
construction of the pre-production sacrificial soil nail. The soil nail was drilled using an 
excavator equipped with a 6-inch diameter downhole hammer drill attachment. 
The sacrificial soil nail was drilled to a depth of about 25 feet from the slope face 
with an inclination of about 15 degrees.  After the hole was drilled to the specified depth, 
the Grade 75 #10 rebar was inserted into the drilled hole.  Centralizers were placed along 
the rebar at 5-foot intervals. 
The sacrificial anchor was grouted using a 1-inch diameter PVC tremie tube that 
was fully inserted into the drilled hole. Grouting of the anchor was terminated when fresh 
grout appeared at the top of the hole. The grouting tube was removed after the completion 
of the grouting operation.  
The volume of grout placed in the drilled hole was about 4.8 cubic feet (ft3), 
approximately 1.1 times the theoretical volume of the drilled hole. Log of the sacrificial soil 
nail recording, our observations, and other pertinent information by our representative are 
provided in the Soil Nail Installation Record, Plates 2.1 and 2.2.  
Soil Nail Testing 
The pre-production soil nail was tested on May 22, 2023.  The testing apparatus 
included a 50-ton hydraulic jack (UNK RACD – S/N HTR406), and two 1-inch stroke dial 
gauges. The test was conducted by pre-loading the testing apparatus up to about 0.8 kips 
to engage the reaction timbers and ground. The verification soil nail was then loaded up to 
about 200 percent of the design load (15 kips). The soil nail was tested to 30 kips with a 
total displacement of about 0.210 inches.  Details of the anchor testing are presented on 
Plates 3.1 through 3.4. 
Based on the observations of our field representative, we believe that the pre-
production soil nail observed by Geolabs has been installed in general conformance with 
the project plans and specifications and within the limits of generally accepted construction 
practices. Based on our observations of the verification soil nail test performed by the 
contractor, it is our professional opinion that the anchor has been installed to provide 
sufficient load capacity with tolerable movements. Therefore, we recommend using the 
production anchor lengths provided in the contract documents.
