SUBJECT Slope Erosion Lot No 206 Kapolei Knolls

PRESENT AT SITE
A site visit was made on January 15 to observe the reported erosion debris from
the cut slope within Lot No 206 and bordering Lot No 124
Lot No 206 is a large vacant lot located at the end of Wahana Place and
bordering the H 1 Highway The cut slope bordering Lot No 124 has a total height of
about 10 to 20 feet with a bench at about mid height There is also a chain link fence
along the lower edge of the slope bench The soil conditions exposed consisted of about
1 to 2 feet of stiff reddish brown residual soil over weathered basalt rock formation A
small amount of debris consisting primarily of gravel and cobbles was observed along
the slope bench and behind the chain link fence
The purpose of the slope bench and chain link fence was to collect material that
may loosen from the slope face Our observations indicate that the bench and fence are
performing as planned The rock formation exposed in the cut slope is competent and
does not exhibit signs of mass instability It is our opinion that the slope has performed
fairly well considering the very heavy rainstorms that have occurred recently
