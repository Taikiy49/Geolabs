PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Ewa by Gentry Area 40 site is on the western side of Fort Weaver Road
south of Geiger Road in the Ewa District on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site
is bounded by Area 12 to the north Fort Weaver Road to the east future Keaunui Drive
to the south and Kapolei Parkway to the west The Area 40 site is divided into three
phases with Phase 1 at the eastern portion of the site adjacent to Fort Weaver Road A
Project Location Map and Site Plans are shown on Plates 1 and 2 1 and 2 2
respectively
The project involves mass grading for a residential development consisting of
house lots and related access roads The mass grading work involved fills up to about
10 feet thick
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for
the mass grading operations at the project site

MASS GRADING
We observed the mass grading work for the Area 40 Phase 1 project from
August 10 2006 to The mass grading work consisted of clearing and
grubbing and placement and compaction of fills on the order of up to about 10 feet thick to
achieve the design finished grades Delta Construction Corporation performed the mass
grading work During the mass grading operations samples of the on site and imported
materials utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office
for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established
in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test
results are summarized on Plates 3 1 and 3 2
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above the optimum
moisture content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density
as determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the mass grading operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the
compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 16 of this report
These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and
backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and
interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be
considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to
be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the mass grading work for the above referenced
project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were
performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the following
references
