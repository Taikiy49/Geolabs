DISCUSSION
The entire area is underlain by basalt which outcrops as low ridges
interspaced with thin soil cover The soil is generally only a few inches
thick with a maximum depth of 2 5 feet in Boring No The upper one
foot of the basalt is weathered Below this layer the rock is mod
_erately hard with small pukas The poring logs indicate layers of
soft clinker within the basalt
Foundations for the planned story building may be conventional spread
footings and should be founded in the moderately hard basalt All
soils and soft rock should be removed from the building site and
stock piled for landscaping purposes a
