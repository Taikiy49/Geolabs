Project Considerations
It is desired to conduct an environmental assessment at the Waimea Chevron Service
Station in Kamuela located on the island of Hawaii A total of 10 borings are planned to be
drilled to depths of about 6 to 25 feet below the existing ground surface using 6 inch hollow
stem augers Samples will be collected from each borehole at 5 foot intervals for a total of 38
samples using the standard split spoon samplers After the drilling and sampling the boreholes
will be backfilled with cement bentonite grout
Based on the information you provided we anticipate that the site is underlain by silty
clay overlying volcanic alluvium with cobbles at approximately 20 feet below the existing ground
surface
