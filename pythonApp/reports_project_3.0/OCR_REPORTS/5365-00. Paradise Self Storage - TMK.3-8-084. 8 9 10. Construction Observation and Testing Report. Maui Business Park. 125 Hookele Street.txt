SITE GRADING
The earthwork operations for the project were observed by Geolabs Inc from
November 2004 to on an intermittent basis The work consisted of
subgrade preparation and placement and compaction of fill materials to achieve the
design finished grades for the project The earthwork operations were performed by Maui
Service During the earthwork operations samples of the on site and imported materials
utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing
