SITE GRADING
The subject site is located in the Ewa by Gentry development in the District of
Ewa on the island of Oahu Hawaii A general layout of the project site is provided on
the Site Plan Plate 1 The mass grading operations were performed by Oahu
Construction Company and the roadway and utility constructions were performed by
Kiewit Pacific Company The site grading consisted of clearing and grubbing
placement and compaction of engineered fills and utility trench excavation and backfill
Following the clearing and grubbing operations the exposed subgrades were
scarified brought to near the optimum moisture content and compacted to a minimum

of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as determined in accordance with American
Society of Testing and Material ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor
During the site grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials
utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for
testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established
in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified
Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 2 of this report
During the fill and backfill operations the fill and backfill materials were generally
placed in near level lifts brought to above the optimum moisture and compacted to a
minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density determined in accordance with
ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor The upper 2 feet of fills below the
finish grade and road subgrade generally consist of non expansive coralline materials
Road subgrade materials were compacted to a minimum of 95 percent relative
compaction Periodic field density tests were taken during the fill and backfill
operations to confirm our field observations as variations in construction procedures
and soil conditions were noted
A total of 373 field density tests including retests of failed areas were performed
using the nuclear probe method in accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922
The results of the compaction tests taken during the Area 5 mass grading operations
are summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 10 of this report Compaction test results for
the Area 5 roadway and utility construction are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 12
These tests were taken on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed
engineered fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated
by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations
should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas
assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction
was obtained
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the earthwork operations during the mass grading
and roadway and utility construction observed and tested by Geolabs Hawaii in the
period covered by this report were performed in general accordance with the intent of
the project plans and specifications and our geotechnical engineering recommendations
presented in the above referenced report dated and the addendum

LIMITATIONS
It should be noted that it is not possible to continuously observe all activities
occurring simultaneously ona construction site Therefore some of the earthwork were
performed without our observation However the level of earthwork observation and
testing services provided appears to be reasonable in reducing some of the geotech
nical risks to the project It should be recognized that there are variations in the
accuracy and statistical repeatability of the tests and methods used to monitor the
earthwork construction and materials Our services were performed in accordance with
generally accepted standards of geotechnical engineering practice no warranty is
expressed or implied Provision of construction monitoring by an engineer is not
insurance nor does it guarantee construction of any type The contractor shall retain
responsibility for the quality of the work for adhering to the plans and specifications
and for repairing defects regardless of when they are found
