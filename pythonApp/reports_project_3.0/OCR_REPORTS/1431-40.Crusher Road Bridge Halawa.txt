Pile Driving Record
Crusher Road Bridge
Halawa Oahu Hawaii
In general accordance with the scope of services outlined in our
proposal we observed the predrilling and driving of fifty six 56
production piles The observations of the production pile driving were
conducted from February 28 to March 8 1989 a total of seven 7 working
days
A diesel pile hammer model DEIMAG D 16 32 with a maximm rated
energy of lbs was used to drive the precast prestressed
general depths of about 25 45 to 62 25 feet below the existing ground
surface The locations of all piles are shown on the attached pile
location plan
Based on our observations and pile load test results we believe that
the piles have been driven to sufficient depths and or resistance to
provide the design vertical compression capacity of 40 tons
Enclosed is a summary of all the driven piles and the pile driving
records for reference
