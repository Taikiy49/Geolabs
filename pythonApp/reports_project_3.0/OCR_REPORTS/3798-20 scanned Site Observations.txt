In accordance with your request we have been providing geotechnical engineering
observation and compaction testing services at the project site During the mass grading

sand and gravel Minimal fill was placed to raise the pad grades to the final elevations
Yesterday during the footing excavation for Buildings D and E moderately expansive brown
to reddish brown clay soils were noted in the lower portions of the footing excavations 12 to
24 inches below the finished grade It appears that portions of these buildings may be
underlain by the moderately expansive soils We recommend that the brown to reddish
brown clay soils be overexcavated to a minimum depth of 24 inches below the finished grade
and replaced with select fill
