REMARKS: We have reviewed the report entitled “Bi-Directional Static Load Test Report, 
Ililani Tower, Keawe & Halekauwila Parcels” dated November 19, 2019 
prepared by GRL Engineers, Inc. transmitted to our office by Swinerton via e-
mail on November 20, 2019. 
Based on the favorable results of the static load test conducted on a 
dedicated 24-inch diameter ACIP load test pile, we believe the 24-inch 
diameter ACIP production piles may be reduced in length to support the 
various design compressive load capacities. Therefore, we recommend 
some of the production pile lengths for the 24-inch diameter ACIP piles be 
revised (in bold) as shown in the following table.
