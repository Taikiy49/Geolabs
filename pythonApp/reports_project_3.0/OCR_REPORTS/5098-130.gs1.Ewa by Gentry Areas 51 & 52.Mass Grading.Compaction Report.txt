PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Areas 51 and 52 Ewa by Gentry West project site is south of Geiger Road in
the Ewa District on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is located between
Essex Road and Kapolei Parkway A Project Location Map and Site Plans are shown
on Plates 1 and 2 respectively
The project site encompasses an area of about 43 13 acres with estimated
excavation and embankment quantities of 120 410 and 139 662 cubic yards respectively
The mass grading work involved cuts up to about 7 feet and fills up to about 5 feet thick
In addition a new retaining wall about 1 800 linear feet in length will be constructed along
the west side of the project
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for
the mass grading at the project site

MASS GRADING
We observed the mass grading work for the Areas 51 and 52 Ewa by Gentry West
project from March 5 2015 to The mass grading work consisted of cuts up
to about 7 feet in height and the placement and compaction of fills on the order of up to
about 5 feet thick to achieve the finished grades for the project Royal Contracting
Company Ltd performed the mass grading work During the mass grading operations we
obtained samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials
and transported them to our laboratory for testing The maximum dry densities and
optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with
ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above the optimum
moisture content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density
as determined in accordance with ASTM D1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the mass grading operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM D2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the
compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 42 of this report
These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and
backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and
interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be
considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed as
unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained and or
approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the mass grading work for the above referenced
project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report was
performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the following
