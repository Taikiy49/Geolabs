Preliminary Pavement Design Recommendations
Proposed Pavement Repair Ewa Driveway
Rehabilitation Hospital of the Pacific
Honolulu Oahu Hawaii
Based on our field exploration performed for the above referenced project the
existing pavement structure is generally underlain by clayey subsoils with poor pavement
support characteristics at shallow depths Considering the heavy truck traffic anticipated
for the Ewa Driveway it is our opinion that the following new pavement section may be
used for the subject project
Flexible Pavement Section
3 0 Inches Asphaltic Concrete
6 0 Inches Basaltic Base Course 95 Percent Relative Compaction
12 0 Inches Select Borrow 95 Percent Relative Compaction
21 0 Inches Total Pavement Thickness on Compacted Subgrade
The pavement subgrades should be scarified to a depth of about 8 inches
moisture conditioned to at least 2 percent above the optimum moisture and compacted
to a minimum of 95 percent relative compaction If the subgrade soils are pumping during
subgrade preparation the subgrade may be stabilized by cement treatment to reduce the
potential for pumping subgrade conditions As a guide one sack of cement may be used
for approximately 25 square feet of subgrade area Alternatively the subgrade should be
