Dear Mr Creps
This letter report presents our consultation for the Individual Wastewater System
proposed for the subject quarry expansion project
Project Considerations
The project site is within the existing Grace Pacific Makakilo Quarry in the Ewa
District of the Island of Oahu Hawaii As part of the quarry expansion project we
understand an individual wastewater system IWS will be required The State of Hawaii
Department of Health requires performance of a percolation test to evaluate the
suitability of the site location for use of an individual wastewater system However the
proposed IWS location is presently at an elevation considerably below proposed grade
and it is not feasible to conduct a valid percolation test at that location
Consultation
Based on our previous experience at the Grace Pacific Makakilo Quarry the
on site residual and overburden soils have relatively low permeability For the purposes
of preliminary design of the disposal component of the IWS we recommend using an
infiltration rate of 20 minutes per inch
When the site grading approaches finish grade a field percolation test may be
performed in general accordance with the State of Hawaii Department of Health s
Administrative Rules Chapter 2 Wastewater Systems and Chapter 10 of the
Ten State s Standards to evaluate the permeability of the subsurface soils for disposal
of wastewater effluent Based on the results of that test the absorption bed design may
be modified to reflect the actual percolation rate Such modifications may include soil
replacement or changes to the size of the absorption bed
