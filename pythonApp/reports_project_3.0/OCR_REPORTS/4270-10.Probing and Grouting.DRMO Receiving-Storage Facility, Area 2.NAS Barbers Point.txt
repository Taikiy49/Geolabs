Dear Mr Castillo
This letter presents the results of the probing operation at the DRMO
Receiving Storage Facility Area 2 site which was performed on and 7 2000 The
work was performed in general accordance with our fee proposal dated
A total of 32 probeholes were drilled below the column footings of two proposed
buildings at the DRMO Receiving Storage Facility Area 2 at NAS Barbers Point on the Island
of Oahu Hawaii The proposed MHE Shed and PMB Office consists of 17 and 16 column
footings respectively The subgrade was excavated by Aczon Construction to the required
bottom of footings Materials encountered were light gray brown sandy limestone gravel
overlying coral sand formation A cavity of approximately 6 feet in depth from bottom of
footing elevation and 3 feet maximum in diameter at the footing of Probehole No 3 was
exposed during excavation of the footings No probehole was drilled on this footing A
probehole was drilled for each of the remaining 32 footings to a depth of approximately
10 feet below the bottom of the footing elevation Probing was performed using 4 inch
diameter continuous flight augers A small cavity approximately 1 foot in diameter and 6 feet
in depth radiating slightly northward from the middle of the footing at Probehole No 17 at
the proposed MHE Shed was also encountered No other cavities or clinker zones were
encountered during the course of our probing operation
Filling of the probeholes was performed after the probing operation Cement grout
1 bag of cement 6 gallons of water at 0 96 cubic foot per probehole was used to fill the
probeholes except for and 17 where cavities were encountered A total
of 2 17 cubic yards of mortar mix 1 bag cement 1 cubic foot sand 6 gallons of water was
used to fill the cavities at and 17
