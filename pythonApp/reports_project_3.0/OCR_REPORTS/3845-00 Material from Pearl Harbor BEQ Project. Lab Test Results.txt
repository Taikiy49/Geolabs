Laboratory Test Results
Material from the Pearl Harbor BEQ Project
Pearl Harbor Oahu Hawaii
In accordance with your request we have performed laboratory tests on the
materials from the Pearl Harbor BEQ project site The following is a summary of the
laboratory results performed on the bulk samples delivered to our office by your
representatives
A sample two 5 gallon buckets of the materials from the Pearl Harbor BEQ site
was delivered to our office on October 16th A California Bearing Ratio CBR test was
performed on the sample to evaluate the material for use as general fill The results of
the CBR test are summarized on the attached Summary of Laboratory Test Results
Plate 1
Based on the results of the CBR test the materials tested met the requirements for
City and County of Honolulu Borrow material We understand that the materials will be
transported to the future Kapolei High School site for placement as general fill The
materiais tested should be in general conformance with general fill specifications _ It
should be emphasized that the materials to be placed as fill should be moisture
conditioned to at least 2 percent above the optimum moisture content placed in loose
level lifts not exceeding 8 inches and compacted to not less than 90 percent relative
compaction based on ASTM D 1557
The laboratory test results and our professional opinions expressed herein are
specifically for the subject site only No warranty is expressed or implied
