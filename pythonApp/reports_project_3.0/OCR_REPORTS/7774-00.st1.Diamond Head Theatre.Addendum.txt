Soil Profile 
We understand that this project will be designed based on IBC 2012. Based on 
subsurface conditions generally consisting of hard to very hard basalt formation and the 
field penetration resistance obtained from our drilled borings, the project site may be 
classified from a seismic analysis standpoint as being a “Very Dense Soil and Soft Rock” 
site corresponding to a Site Class C soil profile type based on the Table 20.3-1 of 
ASCE/SEI 7-10. Based on Site Class C, the following seismic design parameters were 
estimated and may be used for seismic analysis of the project.

Dynamic Lateral Earth Pressures 
Dynamic lateral earth forces due to seismic loading will need to be considered in 
the design of the retaining structures based on IBC 2012. Seismic loading (PGA or 
amax = 0.302g) is used to estimate the dynamic lateral earth pressures.   
The table below summarizes the dynamic lateral earth forces acting on the 
retaining structure in the event of an earthquake versus the estimated wall displacements.
