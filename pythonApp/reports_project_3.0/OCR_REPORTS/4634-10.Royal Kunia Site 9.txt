Dear Mr Jordan
This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during house construction for the Royal Kunia Site 9 development
from August 13 2001 to May 22 2002 in accordance with our fee proposal dated
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the eastern portion of the Royal Kunia
Development Area in the District of Waipahu on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project
site is bounded by Anonui Street on the east and the future golf course on the remaining
borders The project site includes a total of 59 residential lots A Project Location Map
and a Site Plan are shown on Plates 1 and 2 respectively
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the house grading and utility installation within the house lot areas for the
project site
HOUSE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The house grading and utility installation work for the Royal Kunia Site 9 project
were observed by Geolabs Inc from August 13 2001 to May 22 2002 The house
grading work consisted of footing excavation and footing and slab on grade subgrade
preparation for the project The utility installation work consisted of trench backfill of
plumbing lines within the house and garage pad areas and the backfill of the sewer
clean out areas The house grading and utility backfilling work were performed by Reef
Development of Hawaii Inc During the house grading operations samples of the on site
and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported
to our office for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were
established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557
Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3
2006 Kalihi Street Honolulu Hawaii 96819

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557
During the house grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density
tests were performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear
Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on
Plates 4 1 through 4 13 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared
ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and
elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These
locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the
methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified
minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on
visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s backfill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the house grading and utility installation work for the
project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were
performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the following
references

2 Report by Hawaii Geotechnical Group Inc entitled Preliminary Soil Report

should be recognized that there are variations in the accuracy and siatistical repeatability
of the tests and methods used to monitor the earthwork construction and materials Our
