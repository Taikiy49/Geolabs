Project Considerations
The project site is located at an existing drainage channel off Kilauea Avenue in the
Kahala area of Honolulu on the Island of Oahu Hawaii Based on our field observations
and the information provided we understand that a section of the existing drainage
channel wall has toppled over recently The fallen wall section measures approximately
100 feet long and is located along the Diamond Head side of the channel
We understand that it is desired to evaluate the subsurface conditions at the project
site for the formulation of geotechnical engineering recommendations pertaining to the
design of the replacement wall section It is our understanding that borehole drilling to
obtain subsurface soil information will not be performed for this project
Purpose and Scope
The purpose of our evaluation is to obtain an overview of the subsurface conditions
at the project site to develop a soil rock data set for the formulation of geotechnical
engineering recommendations pertaining to the design of the replacement wall section In
order to accomplish this we propose an evaluation program consisting of the following
tasks and efforts
