REMARKS As requested we have reviewed the civil plans Drawing Nos CD C 101
through CD C 116 for the above referenced project Our review was
generally limited to the geotechnical aspects of the plans
Based on our review it is our opinion that the subject plans reviewed
generally reflect the intent of the recommendations presented in our
geotechnical engineering report prepared for the subject project However
we recommend that the attached typical trench detail be used for the water
drain and sewer lines at the project site considering the proximity of soft
soils to the inverts of the utility lines In addition the changes provided on
the attached AC Pavement Connection Detail should be considered
Based on our field exploration and the proposed invert elevations of the
sewer lines we anticipate that the sewer line inverts will be located within
soft and or loose soils We anticipate some differential settlements may
occur between the pipe segments Therefore we recommend that vitrified
clay pipes VCP NOT be used for the proposed sewer lines Consideration
should be given to the use of more flexible pipes such as PVC or HDPE
pipes
