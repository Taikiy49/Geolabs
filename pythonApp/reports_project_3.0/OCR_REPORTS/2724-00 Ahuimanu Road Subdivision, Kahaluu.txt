Dear Mr Morimatsu
As requested we have performed the observation and testing of the mass grading
operations at the above referenced project site from through February
20 1992 in accordance with our proposal dated
The grading was performed in general accordance with the recommendations
presented in Chapter 23 Grading Soil Erosion and Sediment Control of the Revised
Ordinances of Honolulu 1978 as amended Ordinance 07
The project site as shown on Plate 1 was graded for the construction of a private
roadway and its infrastructure installation Minimal fills and cuts of up to five 5 feet were
performed to accomplish the site grading
During the mass grading operations representative samples of the soils utilized as
structural fills were secured and returned to our laboratory for testing The maximum soil
density and optimum moisture content were determined in our laboratory in accordance
with the procedures described in ASTM Test Designation D 155 The results of our
laboratory tests are presented in Plate 3
Observation and testing of the earthwork in the field was performed by
representatives from our office to observe the compliance with the recommendations and
