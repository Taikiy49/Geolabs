_ From April 10 to 20 1979 the production pile driving for
the above referenced project was observed by a representative
of our office
The piles consisted of 16 inch octagonal precast prestressed
concrete piles driven with a Link Belt 520 diesel hammer
Eighteen inch diameter holes were drilled with an auger prior
to pile driving The pre drilled holes extended to a depth of
10 feet or a minimum of 1 foot into the hard coral whichever
was longer The piles were generally driven to depths of 10
to 39 feet below the existing ground surface
