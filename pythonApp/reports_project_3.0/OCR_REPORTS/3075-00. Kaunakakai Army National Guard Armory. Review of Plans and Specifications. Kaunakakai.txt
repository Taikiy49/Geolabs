TECHNICAL MEMORANDUM on Grade
CONCRETE SLABS ON GRADE
In accordance with DAGS Technical Memorandum 61 dated
a basaltic termite barrier BTB shall be
designed and installed on all new facilities This
basaltic barrier would replace the cushion fill normally _
used beneath the moisture barrier
Additionally the present building code Section 2605
requires the provision of a minimum one inch thick layer
of sand to be placed over the moisture barrier beneath
DPW Form 79
slabs on grade According to American Concrete Institute
80 the provision of a sand layer aids in
reducing the plastic and shrinkage cracking attributable
to uneven 1 drying of the slab
Ungraded sand i is not intended to prevent ground termite
intrusion It is conceivable for sand to be contaminated
with termites and transported to the project or that
termites might gain access into walls and travel through
the sand layer to other areas Therefore BTB material
shall be used in lieu of sand placed atop the moisture
barrier
Also to accommodate probable displacement of the
material during concrete placement a two inch layer of
BTB shall be provided over the vapor barrier
