In accordance with your request we have performed geotechnical engineering
analyses to evaluate the slope stability of the new embankment at the above
referenced site in support of VECP No 1 The following is a summary of our
engineering analyses performed
Based on the information provided we understand that it is proposed to utilize
the on site material to construct the new embankment in lieu of the imported borrow
material originally required in the plans Due to the height of the new embankment
and wet environment at the subject site it is desired to perform engineering analysis
to evaluate the slope stability of the new embankment constructed using on site
material In order to perform the slope stability analysis laboratory tests were
performed on remolded samples of the stockpile soils to provide the shear strength
parameters of the materials Details of the laboratory tests are provided in our letter
dated The results of the unconsolidated undrained triaxial

to perform the slope stability analyses The program PCSTABLSM is able to calculate

both undrained and drained conditions Based on our engineering analyses and
