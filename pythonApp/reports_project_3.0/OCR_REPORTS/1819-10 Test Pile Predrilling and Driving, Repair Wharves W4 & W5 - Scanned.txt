In general accordance with the scope of services outlined in our
proposal dated we have observed the test pile predrilling
and driving operations of the proposed fender pile system at Wharf W5
Records of the test pile driving observation are attached Only the
predrilling of three 3 of the test piles at Line 7 80

A total of four 4 test piles were planned at the approximate
locations shown on the attached pile location plan of Wharf W5 but only
three 3 of the piles could be driven The fourth test pile TP 7 at
Line No 69 could not be driven to the predrilled depth of about 55 feet
below the design cut off elevation approximately 10 inches above the
existing deck level of Wharf W5 due to obstruction by unknown objects or
excessive caving in of subsoils into the hole Therefore driving of the
test pile was abandoned at this depth Since the driving conditions of
the other three test piles were observed to be generally consistent we
believe that the redriving of this test pile TP 7 is not needed
immediately and may be performed later during the production pile driving
provided the hole is cleared by predrilling
