DISCUSSION AND RECOMMENDATIONS

Based on our limited field reconnaissance it is our opinion
that the site is suitable for the proposed apartment house
construction Since final plans are not available at this
time more detailed studies will be required at a later date
However it is our understanding that the structures will be
up to six stories in height and a reinforced concrete design
will be used The flood plain area may require fills up to
ten feet in height
Foundations
It is our opinion that the site may be developed for the
proposed apartment house complexes The type of foundations
will depend upon building design and foundation loads but
based on our geologic reconnaissance we anticipate the use
of spread footings

Building pads should be so designed and foundations are seated
entirely in fill or entirely in cut It is most likely that
the lower slopes will be cut area and at the flood plain will
be fill We anticipate no excessive settlements of foundations
in the cut materials or on the flood plain materials beneath
the fills
Footings and slabs on grade may have to be designed for expan
sive pressures
Due to the nature of flood plains there may exist several
soft and loose pockets of soils that will have to be removed
before any fills are placed in these areas
Slopes
We do not foresee any major construction problems relating to
slope stability that will be encountered on the site The
stream channel bank should be checked for stability and erosion
at a later date
On the lower slopes no stability problems are foreseen The
decomposed rock that will be encountered near the surface
should be rippable with conventional grading equipment and may
not require blasting The actual depth of unweathered rock
