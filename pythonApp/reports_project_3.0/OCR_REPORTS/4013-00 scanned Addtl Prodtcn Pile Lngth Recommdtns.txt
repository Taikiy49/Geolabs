Dear Mr Yung
In accordance with our recommendations presented in the above referenced
report we have observed the additional test pile driving within the 30 foot predrilling zone
on The purpose of the additional test pile installation is to evaluate the
production pile length s required in this area
The additional test pile driving operations were performed by Lahaina Pier Pile
Company Inc LPPC using the same pile hammer and setting for the previous test pile
installation A total of five additional test piles consisting of Pile 153 193
and 222 were driven to depths of about 36 to 45 feet below the existing ground surface
at the site Individual pile driving records and summary are attached for your reference
Based on our test pile driving observations it is our opinion that the additional test
piles have been driven to sufficient depths and or resistance to provide the design vertical
compressive load capacity of 65 tons per pile and may be incorporated into the actual
foundation system for the subject project In addition an estimated production pile length
of 50 feet long may be considered for the remaining production piles located within the
30 foot predrilling zone for the subject project
