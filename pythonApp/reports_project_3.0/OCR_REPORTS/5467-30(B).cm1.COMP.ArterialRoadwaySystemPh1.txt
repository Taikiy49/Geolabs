This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during site grading construction for the Mehana at Kapolei Arterial
Roadway System Phase 1 from to March 30 2009 in accordance with our
fee proposal dated
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Mehana at Kapolei development site is bordered by a drainage easement and
Roosevelt Road to the south Ft Barrette Road to the east and future James Campbell
Estate residential development to the north and west The Arterial Roadway System
Phase 1 is on the eastern portion of the development and includes extension of Kapolei
Parkway past Ft Barrette Road into the site Roads A M and N The layout of the roads
is shown on the Site Plan Plate 1 1
The mass grading consisted of fills generally less than about 2 feet The grading
plan for the site is shown on the Site Plan Plate 1 2
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for
the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING
The mass grading consisted primarily of filling the site up to about 2 feet At the
onset of grading the area was cleared and grubbed prior to filling Delta Construction
performed the site grading and backfilling work During the site grading operations
samples of the imported materials utilized as fill materials were obtained and transported
to our office for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were
established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor
and the test results are summarized on Plate 2

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method
Compaction test results are summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 34 These tests were
performed on the prepared over excavated surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills
Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site
survey stakes These locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the
degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked
until the specified minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field
representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of full time and part time field observation of the contractor s
fill placement subgrade preparation compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade
soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on our field representative observations and the referenced test results it is
our opinion the site grading and utility installation work for the Mehana at Kapolei Arterial
Roadway System Phase 1 project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered
by this report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the
recommendations in the following references
1 Chapter 14 of the Revised Ordinances of Honolulu 1990 as amended
2 Report by Geolabs Inc entitled Geotechnical Engineering Exploration
Phase 1 Mehana at Kapolei dated
