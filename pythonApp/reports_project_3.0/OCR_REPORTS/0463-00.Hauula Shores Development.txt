In accordance with your request we have completed the sub
surface borings and percolation testing for the above site
All work was performed in general accordance with our proposal
dated March 7 1973
Three 3 6 inch diameter borings were drilled utilizing
truck mounted rotary drilling equipment with solid flight
auger Coring was not used in any of the borings The
borings were from 20 to 35 feet deep and were completed on
All soil cuttings were visually classified in the field
according to the Unified Soil Classification system

PERCOLATION TESTING
The percolation tests were performed by filling the holes
with water to saturate the ground After saturation the
holes were refilled and the percolation rate observed
Boring 1
Boring 1 was 30 feet deep with the water level at 13 5
feet deep after drilling on The hole was
saturated by filling it with water and allowing it to soak
overnight Before percolation testing on
the water level was at 14 5 feet The hole was filled to
the top and the rate of drop of the water level was then
measured The results are presented in Table A
The hole had caved in to 14 feet after the percolation
test was run
Boring 2
Boring 2 was 35 feet deep with the water level at 6 feet

Boring 3
Boring 3 was drilled to 20 feet deep where hard coral or

drained away The hole was refilled and the rate of drop
