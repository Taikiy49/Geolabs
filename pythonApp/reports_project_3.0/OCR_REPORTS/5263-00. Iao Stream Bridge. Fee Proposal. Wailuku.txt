Project Consideration
It is desired to drill two boreholes for the lao Stream bridge abutment at Wailuku on
the Island of Maui Hawaii The boreholes will be drilled to depths of approximately 60 feet
below the existing ground surface using a truck mounted drill rig Drilling will be performed
using rotary wash method of drilling Rock core samples will be collected using HQ or NQ
core barrels Access to the borehole locations will be the responsibility of Island
Geotechnical Engineers Inc
Purpose and Scope
The purpose of our work on this project is to provide drilling and sampling services
In order to accomplish this we envision a program generally consisting of the following
tasks and work efforts
1 Mobilization and demobilization of a water truck truck mounted drill rig
presently on the Island of Maui and two operators from Honolulu to the site
and back
2 Drilling and sampling of two boreholes to depths of approximately 60 feet
below the existing ground surface
3 The filing of application for permits to drill toning for utilities and staking of
boring locations are not included in this scope of work
