Compaction Report
Geiger Road Culverts
From December 1990 to August 1991 we observed the earthwork and grading operations
performed for the above referenced project This report summarizes our observations and
compaction tests performed at the above referenced site
The above project site is located within the western portion of the Ewa Gentry
Development in Ewa The project comprised the installation of sixteen by 7 11
metal pipe arch culverts under Geiger Road
The grading operations at the above referenced site consisted primarily of backfill around
the culverts The backfill material consisted of No 3B Fine gravel and coralline silty sand and
gravel
Prior to fill placement the existing vegetation was removed and the existing ground
scarified and recompacted The fill was then placed in horizontal lifts and compacted to a
minimum of 90 of its maximum dry density
During the fill placement operations periodic field density tests were done in accordance
with the American Society for Testing and Material ASTM Test Designation D 1556 Sand Cone
