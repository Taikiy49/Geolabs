Purpose and Scope
The purpose of our geotechnical exploration at this time is to
evaluate the subsurface conditions at the site in order to provide soil
data for project planning preliminary costs estimate and foundation
design development for the high rise apartment and the two level
underground parking structure
Based on the geologic map amd the previous boring performed in the
general area it is anticipated that the site is underlain by a stiff
crust underlain by alluvial clays silts and sands The loose silty sands
below the water level may have low shearing strength and high
compressibilites Of particular concern would be the presence and
thickness of the loose wet sandy soils at about 27 to 36 foot depth
Truck mounted drilling equipment will be used to perform the
exploration which consist of the following borings Three 3 70 feet
borings at the apartment structure and two 2 short borings at the
two level underground parking structure
An engineer or geologist from our firm will coordinate the field work
and testing log the borings obtain the necessary soil samples and make
detailed observations of the existing site conditions Selected samples
obtained will be tested in our laboratory to determine the characteristics
of the materials
Upon completion of our work we will submit six copies of our report
summarizing our work on the project and presenting our findings and design
recommendations
