PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Ilima at Leihano project is located at the western side of the intersection of Fort
Barrette Road and Kamaaha Avenue in Kapolei on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project
will involve development of a 40 acre site that is divided into Phases 1 and 2 with each phase
approximately 20 acres in area The mass grading for the Phase 1 portion of the project have
been completed The Assisted Living Facility is located within the Phase 1 portion of the
development The location of the project site is shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
and on the Site Plan Plate 2
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services pertaining
to the site grading and building foundation work for the project site
SITE GRADING AND BUILDING FOUNDATION INSTALLATION
The site grading and building foundation installation work for the Assisted Living Facility
Ilima by Leihano Phase 1 project was observed by Geolabs Inc from to
The site grading consisted of preparation of the existing subgrade and
placement and compaction of fills and backfills up to about 2 feet in depth to achieve the
design finished grades for the project Kiewit Infrastructure West Company performed the site
grading and building foundation installation work During the site grading and backfilling

operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials
were obtained and transported to our laboratory for testing The maximum dry densities and
optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with
ASTM D1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D1557 In the pavement areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of 95 percent
relative compaction
During the site grading and backfilling operations periodic field density tests were
performed in general accordance with ASTM D6938 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the
compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 10 of this report These
tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at
the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site
survey stakes These locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the
degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed as unsatisfactory were reworked until
the specified minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative
based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of full time and part time field observation of the contractor s fill
and backfill placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures during the above
grading operations

Facility Ilima at Leihano Phase 1 project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period
