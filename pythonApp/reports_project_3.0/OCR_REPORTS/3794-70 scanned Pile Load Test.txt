A pile load test was conducted in accordance with the project specification on
Pile No 148 for the Home Depot Dole project on This report
presents the results of the pile load test program
The pile tested was a 16 5 inch octagonal precast prestressed reinforced concrete
pile The test pile was located at column designation 104 and row designation KK A
diesel hammer was used to drive the pile on
During the test pile driving a log of blow count was maintained by an engineer from our
office A log of the test pile driving record is provided on Plate1
The test pile was loaded in general accordance with ASTM D 1143 Quick Load
Test Method as specified for the project The maximum test load on the test pile was
planned at about 300 kips equal to two times the design load In general the load test
was loaded to the maximum test load of 300 kips in 15 kip load increments Each load
increment was held for at least 4 minutes or until the rate of settlement was generally
stable The 153 kip 218 kip and 159 kip load increments were held for 60 minutes
After loading the load test pile to 366 kips the test load was unloaded in approximately
four equal decrements and held for at least 5 minutes at each decrement The results
of the load test are presented on Plates 2 1 and 2 2 and are summarized as follows
