This compaction report summarizes our observations and presents the
results of the compaction tests taken during our presence at the site
The compaction test results for the in place fills are summarized on the
attached Compaction Test Summary
In general the grading operations consisted of cutting the upper
western and northern portions of the site and filling the lower areas
Prior to fill placement the existing vegetation was removed and the
existing ground scarified and recompacted The fill was then placed in
horizontal lifts and compacted to a minimm of 85 of its maximm dry
density established by ASTM D 1557 During the fill placement operations
periodic field density tests were done in accordance with the ASIM Test
Designation D 1556 Sand Cone Method
During the grading period various on site soil types were tested
prior to use in the fills The maximum soil densities and optimm
moisture contents were established in our laboratory in accordance with
ASTM D 1557 The test results are summarized as follows

RECOMMENDATIONS
House Foundations
General house foundation reconmendations for preliminary design
purposes are as follows
1 Footings located on or near slopes should penetrate down a
sufficient depth in order to develop a minimm horizontal
set back distance of 6 feet measured from the outer edge of the
footing foundation out to the slope face
Individual footings on level areas should be embedded a minimm
of 12 inches below the ground surface to minimize the effect of
subsequent soil shrinkage associated with moisture loss due to
drying under the roof area All footing backfill should be
properly compacted to obtain 85 compaction and graded to drain
water away from the footing
