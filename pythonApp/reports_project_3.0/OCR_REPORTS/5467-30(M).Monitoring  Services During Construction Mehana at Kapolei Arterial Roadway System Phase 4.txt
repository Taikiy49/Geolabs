PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Mehana at Kapolei development site is bordered by Roosevelt Road to the
south Ft Barrette Road to the east and future James Campbell Estate residential
development to the north and west Arterial Roadway System Phase 4 is situated on the
mid western portion of the Mehana at Kapolei development It includes Road E Road K
and a segment of Road A from to that runs from east to west
through the middle of Phase 4 The layout of the roads is shown on the Project Location
Map Plate 1 and on the Grading Plan Plate 2
The grading and construction of this portion of the arterial roadway system
generally consisted of trench excavations for utility installation including backfills subgrade
cuts and fills and pavement subbase and base course preparations
This report summarizes our field observations and compaction testing services for
the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project

SITE GRADING
The site grading consisted primarily of cuts and fills up to about 2 feet At the onset
of construction the area within the limits of grading was cleared and grubbed of unsuitable
surface materials Paradigm Construction performed the site grading During the site
grading operations samples of the imported fill materials were obtained and transported to
our laboratory for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents
were established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D1557 Modified
Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3 The fill and backfill materials
were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture content and compacted to a
minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as determined in accordance with
ASTM D1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill materials within 2 feet of the
finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of 95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D6938 Nuclear Gauge Method
Compaction test results are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 32 These tests were
performed on compacted fills and backfills Test locations and elevations were estimated
using on site survey stakes for reference These locations and elevations should be
considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas that did not
meet the required moisture and density conditions were reworked until the required
minimum compaction were obtained or exceeded
Our services consisted of full time and part time field observation of the contractor s
grading activity which includes subgrade preparation utilities installation fill and backfill
placement compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil conditions exposed
during the above grading operations
Based on our field representative s observations and the referenced test results it
is our opinion that the site grading and utility installation work for the Mehana at Kapolei
Arterial Roadway System Phase 4 project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period
covered by this report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the
recommendations in the following references
