This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during mass grading and construction of Ocean View Terrace Project
at the Valley of the Temples from to in accordance with

PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
Ocean View Terrace is located at the southwestern fringes of the Valley of the
Temples Memorial Park at Highway in Kaneohe Oahu Hawaii as shown
on the Project Location Map Plate 1 Mass grading for this project involved cuts fills and
backfills including construction of subsurface drains and retaining walls The project
encompasses approximately 10 35 acres as shown on the Mass Grading and Drainage
Plan Plate 2
Our monitoring services during mass grading consisted of performing field moisture
and density testing to determine adequacy of compaction of fills and backfills for utility
installations retaining wall footings paved walkways and driveways
This report summarizes our field observations and compaction testing services for
the mass grading of the project

MASS GRADING
Mass grading for the Ocean View Terrace consisted of cuts of up to 36 feet and
fills of up to about 47 feet Fills generally consisted of on site soils including materials
from a borrow operation located about 600 feet northeast of the project site
The fill and backfill materials were generally air dried near optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 85 percent of the maximum dry density MDD
as determined in accordance with ASTM D1557
SITE GRADING
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations samples of the on site
materials utilized as fill were obtained and transported to our laboratory for testing
including imported off site soils i e those used to stabilize retaining wall and driveway
foundations
The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in
general accordance with ASTM D1557 Modified Proctor The test results are
summarized on Plate 3
Periodic field density tests were performed in general accordance with
ASTM D6938 Nuclear Gauge Method The test results are summarized on
Plates 4 1 through 4 20 These tests were performed on compacted surfaces of
constructed fills and backfills The test locations were referenced from on site survey
stakes with elevations taken from global positioning system gps readings provided by
the contractor s on site surveyor These locations and elevations should be considered
accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to be
unsatisfactory were reworked until the measured relative compaction conformed to or
exceeded the minimum specified for the project
Our services consisted of full time and part time field observation of the
contractor s Kiewit Infrastructure West Co subgrade preparation fill and backfill
placement and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil conditions
exposed during the above site grading operations
Based on our field representative s observations and the referenced test results
it is our opinion that the utility installation work and site grading for the Oceanview
Terrace Valley of the Temples project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period
covered by this report were performed in accordance with the general intent of the
recommendations in the following references
