PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located in the District of Hawaii Kai on the Island of Oahu
Hawaii The location of the project site is shown on the Site Plan Plate 1 The project site
is in the vicinity of the intersection of Hawaii Kai Drive and Keahole Street in the Hawaii
Kai area in Honolulu on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project includes development of
the site into a private park The park will include concrete sidewalks and pads as part of
the proposed landscape improvements
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations for the project site
The site grading work for the Moana Kai Park project was observed by
Geolabs Inc from to The site grading work consisted of
clearing and grubbing over excavation of about 12 inches in depth and placement and
compaction of structural fills up to about 12 inches in thickness to achieve the design
finished grades for the project The site grading work was performed by Royal Contracting
Company Ltd The maximum dry density and optimum moisture content were provided
by Ameron of Hawaii s Laboratory Department in general accordance with ASTM D 1557
Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 2

The fill material were moisture conditioned to near the optimum moisture content
and compacted to a minimum of 95 percent of the maximum dry density as determined in
accordance with ASTM D 1557
During the site grading operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the
compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 3 of this report These tests were
performed on the constructed fills at the site The compaction of subgrades were verified
by using a hand probe Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and
interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be
considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to
be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
and or approved by our field representative based on visual observations and hand
probing
Our services consisted of part time field observations of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our professional opinion that the site grading work for the Moana Kai
Park Project observed and tested by Geolabs Inc in the period covered by this report
were performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the
plans and specifications for the project
LIMITATIONS
Geolabs assumes no responsibilities for alterations made to the finished grades on
the project subsequent to our observation and testing period without our knowledge and
written acknowledgment
It should be noted that it is not possible to continuously observe all activities
occurring simultaneously on a construction site therefore some work may have occurred
without our observation However we believe that the level of earthwork observation and
testing services provided is reasonable in reducing the geotechnical risks to the project It
should be recognized that there are variations in the accuracy and statistical repeatability
of the tests and methods used to monitor earthwork materials and construction Our
services were performed in accordance with generally accepted standards of geotechnical
engineering practice no warranty is expressed or implied Provision of construction
monitoring by an engineer is not insurance nor does it guarantee construction of any type
The contractor shall retain responsibility for the quality of the work for adhering to the
plans and specifications and for repairing defects regardless of when they are found
