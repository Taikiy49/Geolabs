DISCUSSION AND RECOMMENDATIONS
General
Based on our field investigation and laboratory testing
the proposed site will be able to support the proposed
CMU wall and structural backfill Conventional shallow
footings may be used
Foundations
Spread and line footings should be designed for a bearing
pressure of 2500 pounds per square foot bottom a minimum
of 18 inches below final adjacent grade and upon stiff
brown clayey silt with sand and gravel formation A one
third 1 3 increase may be used when considering wind and
seismic loadings
