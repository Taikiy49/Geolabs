This report summarizes the geotechnical engineering testing results performed
during the site grading work for the Royal Kunia Site 12 Offsite Drainage and Reservoir
project from to The earthwork monitoring and
compaction testing were performed by Hawaii Geotechnical Group Inc
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the central portion of the Royal Kunia
Development area within the Golf Course area and Future Royal Kunia Site 8 Based on
the project documents the soft soils within an existing earth reservoir were excavated and
the reservoir site was graded to match the adjacent ground elevations Also a new
retention basin was constructed above the existing earth reservoir site to accommodate
drainage water from the Royal Kunia Site 12 project The grading encompassed an area
of 14 3 acres with 45 000 cubic yards of embankment Based on the grading plans the
site grading involved fills and cuts of less than about 17 feet A Project Location Map is
shown on Plate 1
This report addresses the compaction testing performed during the site grading
operations for the project site The site grading work was observed by Hawaii
Geotechnical Group Inc HGGI
SITE GRADING
The mass grading operations which included clearing and grubbing were
observed by HGGI from to A field density
summary report by HGGI covering this work is attached to this report

The mass grading work consisted of placement and compaction of fills up to about
17 feet thick to achieve the design finished grades for the project The mass grading work
was performed by Oahu Construction Company Ltd During the mass grading operations
a sample of the on site material utilized as fill material was obtained for testing The
maximum dry density and optimum moisture content were established in general
accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results
by HGGI are attached
The fill materials were moisture conditioned to near optimum moisture content and
compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as determined in
accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557
During the mass grading operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method
Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on the attached HGGI s
summary report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills at the site
Based on our review of HGGI s field density summary report and laboratory test
results it is our opinion that the mass grading work for the above referenced project
observed and tested by HGGI in the period covered by this report were performed in
conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the following references
1 Chapter 14 of the Revised Ordinances of Honolulu 1990 as amended
2 Report by Hawaii Geotechnical Group Inc entitled Soil Reconnaissance
