Project Considerations
It is desired to continue the on going environmental assessment of the subject site
The current scope of the field work is the drilling and sampling of about four 4 borings
to depths of about 15 feet below the existing ground surface and the installation of two
2 inch monitoring wells in the drilled borings

project boundaries Utility clearance and traffic control will be required for this work in the
public right of way
