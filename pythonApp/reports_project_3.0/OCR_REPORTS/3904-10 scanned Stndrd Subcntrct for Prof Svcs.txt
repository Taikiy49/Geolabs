Dear Mr Okubo
As requested we submit our fee proposal to perform a geotechnical engineering
exploration for the redesign of the above project This proposal is based on the
information provided and our experience in the project vicinity
Project Considerations
The project site is along Hana Highway at Milepost 44 95 in the District of Hana on
the Island of Maui Hawaii The existing bridge will be replaced with a new longer
single span concrete bridge structure We prepared and issued a report entitled
Geotechnical Engineering Exploration Paihi Bridge Replacement
dated in support of the design of the replacement bridge The final
design of the bridge replacement project was submitted and the project was out to bid
However after the earthquakes which originated just offshore from the
northwestern coast of the Island of Hawaii the County of Maui inspected the bridge and
its surrounding natural features Based on the inspection a void was observed below the
north abutment of the existing Paihi Bridge
Wilson Okamoto Corporation and Geolabs Inc conducted a reconnaissance on
Based on our reconnaissance the north abutment was bearing on a
basalt rock ledge underlain by clinker material extending down to the existing streambed
The clinker was eroding from the continuous water flow within the stream creating an
overhanging condition of the basalt ledge The continuous erosion of the clinker may
create a condition where basalt ledge just below the proposed new bridge abutment is
undermined which could cause instability of the basalt ledge resulting in structural
damage to the proposed new bridge structure
Based on the current conditions at the site the County of Maui was advised to
postpone the construction of the proposed replacement bridge based on the original
design plans and to redesign the replacement bridge taking into account future erosion of

likely consist of setting back the replacement bridge further away from the centerline of the

We understand that new recommendations would be required for design of the new
replacement bridge In order to formulate these new recommendations subsurface
information at the new abutment locations is necessary therefore we recommend drilling
three additional borings at the new bridge abutment locations
Purpose and Scope
The purpose of our exploration is to obtain an overview of the subsurface
conditions at the bridge site to develop a soil and or rock data set to formulate
geotechnical recommendations for the redesign of the replacement bridge To accomplish
this we propose an exploration program consisting of the following tasks and work efforts
ts Filing of application for permits to drill and utility clearances and coordinate
toning for utilities
2 Mobilization and demobilization of a truck mounted drill rig presently on the
Island of Maui and two operators from Honolulu to and from the project site
o Drilling and sampling of three boreholes extending to depths of
approximately 50 to 100 feet below the existing ground surface
4 Coordination of the field exploration and logging of the borings by our
field engineer geologist
5 Laboratory testing of selected samples obtained during the field exploration
as an aid in classifying the materials and evaluating their engineering
properties
6 Analyses of field and laboratory data to formulate geotechnical
recommendations for the redesign
7 Preparation of six copies of a formal report summarizing our work and
presenting our findings and recommendations
8 Coordination of our overall work by our project engineer
9 Quality assurance of our work and client design team consultation by our
principal engineer
10 Miscellaneous work efforts such as drafting word processing and clerical
support
