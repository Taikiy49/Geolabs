PROJECT DESCRIPTION
In general the project involves the construction of a two level podium a
combination of 2 level internal structured parking and or 2 level tenant spaces fronting
Kawaihao and Waimanu Streets From the podium level onward there are seven floors of
housing situated in two mid rise towers The project site is situated between Kawaihao and
Waimanu Streets and about mid block between Cummins and Kamakee Streets The
general project vicinity is shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
Based on the geotechnical engineering report referenced above we recommended
supporting the new buildings on a deep foundation system consisting of 18 inch diameter
augered cast in place ACIP piles ranging from about 52 to 74 7 feet long with allowable
compressive load capacities of 300 to 500 kips 150 to 250 tons per pile The ACIP piles
would extend through the fill and lagoonal deposits and derive support primarily from the
skin friction between the pile and upper coral ledges and the underlying coralline detritus
STATIC PILE LOAD TEST
In order to evaluate the adequacy of the recommended design lengths of the
ACIP pile foundation a static compressive pile load test was conducted on one dedicated
18 inch diameter ACIP pile designated as Test Pile No 1 The load test pile was installed
to a depth of about 74 feet below the existing ground surface by Hawaiian Dredging
Construction Company HDCC using a Manitowoc 12000 series crane with a crane
mounted hollow stem auger powered by an APE75C Top Drive Drill The static pile load
test results are summarized in our report entitled ACIP Pile Load Test Report Ola
Ka Ilima Artspace Lofts Honolulu Oahu Hawaii dated
Based on our field observations and the static pile load test results from the ACIP
Pile Load Test Report the 18 inch diameter load test pile was loaded to a maximum load
of 1 125 kips Based on our field observations and the results of the static pile load test
we confirmed the 74 feet long 18 inch diameter ACIP piles would be sufficient to achieve
the design compressive load capacities of 500 kips per pile with a factor of safety of at
least 2 0
