DISCUSSION
All deleterious material such as lumber logs brush roots
and rubbish were disposed of offsite prior to placing fill
The exposed subgrade was then scarified to a depth of not
less than 6 inches moisture conditioned and compacted to not
less than 90 of maximum density determined in accordance
with ASTM D 1557 70 with sheepsfoot compactor
Field density testing was carried out in accordance with
ASTM D 1556 64 sand cone method and were performed peri
odically during the grading operations
Fill consisting of approved onsite soils was placed in 12
inches or less lifts and compacted to not less than 90 of
maximum laboratory density
Areas which were determined to be unsatisfactory were re
worked until the specified density was obtained
It will be noted that the Fill Area cross hatched on the
accompanying map was not certified The reason was that
this area was filled with unsuitable material imported from
Club View Gardens III This area is a nonstructural area
