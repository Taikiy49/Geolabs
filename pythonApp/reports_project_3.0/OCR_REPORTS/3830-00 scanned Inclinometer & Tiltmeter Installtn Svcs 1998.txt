Procedure for Installation of Inclinometer Casing
A six inch diameter PVC inclinometer outer casing is positioned on inside of the
rebar cage closest to the wall facing and securely tied to rebar cage of a drilled
shaft at maximum tie spacing of 10 feet
2 Cap the top and bottom end of the outer casing before concrete placement of the
drilled shaft
After concrete placement of a drilled shaft allow curing for about one week
Measure the depth of the casing to determine length of inclinometer inner casing
needed
Attach bottom cap of the inclinometer inner casing
Install the inclinometer inner casing to specified depth
The inner casing grooves shall be positioned perpendicular and parallel to the
drilled shaft wall within 10 degrees of the planned orientation
8 Use pipe clamps to hold the inclinometer casing at the outer casing collar while
adding next section of casing
9 Cap the top of the inner casing to prevent entry of grout
10 Grout shall be cement and water mix with ratio of 1 to 3 by volume
11 Fill the inner casing with water to balance buoyancy effect
