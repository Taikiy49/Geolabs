2 Sheet L 1 Detail A
In general we discourage the use of gravel strips directly adjacent to the
perimeter of the house to reduce the potential for ponding water around the
house foundations Considering the expansive nature of the on site soils and
the severe dry weather conditions in the Kapolei area we recommend that a
walkway and or concrete apron be provided around the house structure to
reduce the potential for surface water infiltration into the subsoils directly
adjacent to the perimeter foundations
3 Sheet A 6 1 Wall Sections
As recommended in our report gutter systems should be installed around the
houses and the discharge should be diverted away from the foundation and
driveway areas This drainage requirement is essential for the proper
performance of the house foundations since ponding water adjacent to the
house foundations could cause subsurface soil saturation and subsequent
heaving or loss of strength
A Sheet A 6 1 Wall Sections
As stated in our report positive drainage away from the perimeter of the house
is critical to the performance of the house slab and foundation system at the
project site Therefore the finished grades outside the house structure should
be sloped minimum 1 percent to provide positive drainage away from the
house structures Based on the plans reviewed we did not notice grade
controls on the plans for the fine grading operations during the house
construction

3 04 Protection
A
Weathered subgrade Should the prepared subgrade become soft
spongy or yielding due to the weather or excessive sprinkling the
contractor shall at his own expense remove and replace the soft
material or let it dry out sufficiently and then recompact the material to
the required density and grade
Where shrinkage cracks are noted after compaction of the subgrade
the soil shall be re scarified to a minimum depth of 8 inches
moisture conditioned to at least 2 percent above the optimum moisture
and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent relative compaction
Saturation and subsequent yielding of the exposed subgrade due to
inclement weather and poor drainage shall require over excavation of
the soft areas and replacement with well compacted engineered fill at
no additional cost to the Department
