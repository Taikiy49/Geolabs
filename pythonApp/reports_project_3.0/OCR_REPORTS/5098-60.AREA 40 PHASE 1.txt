PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Ewa by Gentry Area 40 site is on the western side of Fort Weaver Road
south of Geiger Road in the Ewa District on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site
is bounded by Area 12 to the north Fort Weaver Road to the east future Keaunui Drive
to the south and Kapolei Parkway to the west The Area 40 site is divided into three
phases with Phase 1 at the eastern portion of the site adjacent to Fort Weaver Road A
Project Location Map and Site Plans are shown on Plates 1 and 2 1 and 2 2
respectively
The project involves site grading 145 house pads for a residentiai development
consisting of house lots and related access roads The site grading work involved fills up to
about 2 feet thick In addition the project includes utility and roadway construction

PAD GRADING AND UTILITY ROADWAY CONSTRUCTION
We observed the pad grading and utility and roadway construction work for the
portion of the Area 40 Phase 1 project west of Road B from to June
24 2008 The site grading work consisted of placement and compaction of fills on the
order of up to about 2 feet thick to achieve the finished grades for the project
Albert C Kobayashi Inc performed the site grading and utility and roadway construction
work During the site grading operations we obtained samples of the on site and imported
materials utilized as fill and backfill materials and transported them to our laboratory for
testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in
our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test
results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above the optimum
moisture content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density
as determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility and roadway construction operations periodic
field density tests were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 2922
Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on
Plates 4 1 through 4 35 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground
surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations
were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations
and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods
used Areas assessed as unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum
compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on visual
observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility and roadway construction
work for the above referenced project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period
covered by this report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the
recommendations in the following references
