Summary Report
Subsurface Probe Drilling and Grouting Services
Laie Water Reclamation Facility Expansion
Laie Wastewater Treatment Plant
Laie Oahu Hawaii
In accordance with our Master Agreement for drilling services dated January 26th
and our Probe Drilling and Grouting Fee Estimate dated we have
completed the subsurface cavity probe drilling and grouting operations for the above
referenced project from March 20th to May 6th
A cavity probe drilling and grouting program was conducted for the following
structures at the subject site and the data is summarized on the following Plates of this
report
Headworks Building Plates 2 and 3
Secondary Clarifier No 1 North Plates 4 thru 6
Secondary Clarifier No 2 South Plates 7 thru 10
Sludge Storage Tank Plates 11 and 12
Dewatering Building Plates 13 thru 15

Pump Station Nos 1 and 2 Plates 18 and 19
The cavity probe drilling was performed with a Mobile B 53 rotary drilling rig
equipped with 4 inch diameter augers A total of 220 probe holes totalling 2 531 lineal
feet of probe drilling was performed during the cavity probe drilling operation The drilled
probe holes were subsequently backfilled with approximately 10 96 cubic yards of
2 1 sand cement grout mixes The probing and grouting work was observed and
recorded by a representative of our firm Results of our probe drilling and grouting
observations are summarized on Plates 1 through 19
