Gentlemen
The grading operations and our field inspections of the fill
embankment for Roadway A are complete
We hereby certify that the grading was done properly and in
accordance with our specifications and recommendations and
City and County of Honolulu Ordinance No 3968
The natural vegetation and other deleterious materials were
properly removed and disposed of prior to fill placement
OFFICES Van Washington e Tacoma Washington Honolulu Hawaii
W 2 May 12 1975
The proposed fill area was proof rolled using a vibratory
compactor Several soft areas were revealed during the
proof rolling in the vicinity of the former streambed These
areas were undercut to depths of two feet and backfilled
with compacted soil
The fill was properly keyed and benched into natural ground
The fill was placed in horizontal lifts and compacted with
a sheepsfoot or vibratory compactor The compacted lifts
were approximately ten inches in thickness The vibratory
compactor was used at the beginning and at the end of the
job The majority of the compaction was by sheepsfoot
The fill material was tested in our laboratory and approved
for use Maximum densities and optimum moisture contents
were determined in our laboratory according to Soil Density
Test ASTM D 1557 72T

W 3 May 12 1975
The grading operations were continuously supervised and
tested by our field personnel from to
May 9 1975
The fill was compacted to at least 90 percent of the maximum
density The in place densities were determined in the field
using Field Density Test ASTM D 1556 sand cone method
The results of the tests are presented in the enclosed Com
paction Summary
Operations at the project site were occasionally suspended
due to wet weather Each time work was resumed the fill
area was inspected prior to placing any new fill The sur
face was proof rolled and any remaining wet areas were
removed
The backfilling of the excavation for the 54 inch reinforced
concrete pipe drain was also inspected and approved
It should be noted that our approval and certification is
contingent upon the understanding that no further grading or
alteration is to be performed on the completed fill
We are not responsible for fill which is subsequently removed
and replaced in utilities excavations unless inspected by our
office
