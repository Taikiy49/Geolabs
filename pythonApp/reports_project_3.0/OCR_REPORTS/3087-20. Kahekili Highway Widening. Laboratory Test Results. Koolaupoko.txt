Laboratory Test Results
Select Granular Backfill for MSERW
Kahekili Highway Widening
Likelike Highway to Vicinity of Haiku Road
In accordance with your request we have performed the laboratory tests on the
select granular backfill material for the MSERW to evaluate the gradation and friction
angle of the materials The following is a summary of the laboratory test results
performed on the samples
Three bag samples of the select granular backfill were retrieved from the
stockpile near MSERW No 6 at the project site and transported to our office A sieve
analysis was performed on overall sample in general accordance with AASHTO T 88
ASTM D 422 Based on the results of the sieve analysis we noted that the gradation
of the select granular backfill material is in general conformance with the gradation
requirements of the backfill for the MSERW in the Special Provisions for the project
The oversize portion of the sample retained on the No 4 sieve was then screened
and another sieve analysis was performed on the portion of the sample passing the No
4 sieve Graphic presentation of the grain size distribution of the two samples is
presented on Plate 1 1
In order to prepare the sample for the direct shear test a Modified Proctor test
was performed in general accordance with AASHTO T 180 ASTM D 1557 on the
portion of the sample passing the No 4 sieve to evaluate the moisture and dry density
relationship Results of the Modified Proctor test are presented on Plate 2 1 Based

on the results of the Modified Proctor test four direct shear test specimens were

shear test
Based on the results of the direct shear test the portion of the select granular
material passing No 4 sieve has a friction angle of about 43 and cohesion value of
about 320 pounds per square foot p s f Results of the direct shear test are
