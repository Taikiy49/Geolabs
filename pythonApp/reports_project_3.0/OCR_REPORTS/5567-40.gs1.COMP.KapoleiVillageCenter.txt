SITE GRADING UTILITY INSTALLATION
Geolabs Inc observed the site grading and utility installation work for the Kapolei
Village Center project from to The site grading and
utility installation work consisted of placement and compaction of fills and backfills on the order
of up to about 9 feet thick to achieve the design finished grades for the project
Jayar Construction Inc performed the site grading and utility installation During the site
grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill
materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum dry densities
and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with
ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the roadway and parking areas the fill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of 95 percent
relative density
During the site grading and utility installation operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 6938 Nuclear Gauge Method Results
of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 17 of this report
These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and
backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating
from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be considered accurate
only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were
reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field
representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the referenced
tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility installation work for the above referenced
project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were performed in
conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the following references
