This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing 
services performed during the grading and infrastructure installation for the Anahola 
Reservoirs Improvement Project from July 31, 2020 to October 13, 2020 in accordance 
with our fee proposal dated July 30, 2020. 
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS 
The Anahola Reservoirs Improvement Project is located at two sites, Lower 
Anahola (TMK No.: [4] 4-7-004: 002) and Kealia Field 2 (TMK No.: [4] 4-7-002: 004) 
within the district of Anahola on the Island of Kauai, Hawaii. The project involves the 
decommissioning of the reservoirs located at the above-mentioned sites by removing 
and remediating portions of the existing earthen dam structures. The project location 
sites and general vicinity are shown on the Project Location Map, Plate 1. A layout of 
the project sites is shown on the Site Plans, Plates 2.1 and 2.2. 
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services 
pertaining to the mass grading work for the Lower Anahola and Kealia Field 2 project sites. 
MASS GRADING 
We observed the mass grading work for the Anahola Reservoirs Improvement 
Project from July 31, 2020 to October 13, 2020. The mass grading work consisted of 
clearing and grubbing, excavation of materials from the existing dam structures and 
placement and compaction of fills up to about 10 feet thick to achieve the finished grades. 
The mass grading work was performed by Goodfellow Bros. During the grading 
operations, we obtained samples of the on-site materials utilized as fill and backfill

representative based on visual observation and hand probing.
