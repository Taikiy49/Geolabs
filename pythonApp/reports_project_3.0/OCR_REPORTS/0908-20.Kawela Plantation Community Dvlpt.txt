CONCLUSIONS AND RECOMMENDATIONS
Foundation
Spread or continuous footing are recommended for the proposed
community development Footings should be embedded a minimum
of eighteen 18 inches deep below the finish ground surface
An allowable bearing value up to 3 500 p s f is recommended
for the footings The above value is for total dead plus live
loads and may be increased by one third when temporary loads
are considered Footing settlements should be minimum for the
above designed footing bearing in the dense surface boulderly
silt soil
Slab on Grade
For slab on grade bearing on the soil it is recommended that
a four 4 inch cushion be provided under the four 4 inch
concrete slab to evenly distribute the floor load Slab per
imeter footing should be embedded in a minimum of 12 inches
below the outside grade
