In summary the foundation probing and grouting operations have been conducted
in general conformance with the project specifications Based on our field observations it
is our professional opinion that the cavities encountered below the building subgrade
have been probed and grouted and that the subgrade area should provide the
recommended foundation support for the pump station building
Limitations
It should be noted that it is not possible to detect and remediate cavities and or
voids present beneath the pump station building Therefore some cavities and or voids
may not have been detected even with the probing and grouting program undertaken for
the project However the level of foundation preparation undertaken for the boost pump
station and the subgrade bearing on the on site basalt formation appears to be reasonable
in reducing the geotechnical risks to the project Performing probing and grouting of the
foundations bearing on the on site materials provides the owner with a higher degree of
assurance that substantial cavities and or voids are not located directly below the booster
pump station
