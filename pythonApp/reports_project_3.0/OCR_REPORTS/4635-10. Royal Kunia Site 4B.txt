PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the western portion of the Royal Kunia
Development Area in the District of Waipahu on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project
site is bounded by Kunia Road on the west Royal Kunia Site 4A on the east and the
future golf course on the north The location of the project site is shown on the Project
Location Map Plate1 The project site includes a total of 61 residential lots and
two roadways The site grading work generally consisted of cuts and fills of less than
10 feet The grading plan for the site is shown on the Site Plan Plate 2
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The site grading and utility installation work for the Royal Kunia Site 4B project was
observed by Geolabs Inc from to The site grading work
consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills up to about
10 feet in thickness to achieve the design finished grades for the project The site grading
and utility backfilling work was performed by Delta Construction Corporation During the
site grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and
backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum
dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general
accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results
are summarized on Plate 3

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill
and backfill materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum
of 95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear
Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates
4 1 through 4 20 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground
surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations
were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations
and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods
used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum
compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on visual
observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of full time and part time field observation of the contractor s
fill placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility installation work for the
Royal Kunia Site 4B project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this
report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in
the following references

2 Report by Geolabs Inc entitled Geotechnical Engineering Exploration
Royal Kunia Site 4B Waipahu Oahu Hawaii dated March 1 2001
