Dear Mr Eckman
This field quality control testing report summarizes our geotechnical engineering
observations and presents the field density test results performed during construction of
the Kahuku Wastewater Treatment Plant project in Kahuku from July 1997 through
The services were provided in general accordance with our fee
proposal dated March 31 1997
During the earthwork operations for the project various on site and imported soils
were sampled and tested prior to their use in the field The maximum dry density and
optimum moisture content relationships were established in our laboratory in general
accordance with American Society of Testing and Materials ASTM Test Designation D
1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 1 of this report
Our services during the earthwork operations consisted of part time field
observation of the contractor s fill placement and compaction procedures and
performance of field density testing Our services were on an on call basis and were
requested by personnel of Bodell Construction During the fill and backfill placement
operations periodic field density and field moisture content tests were performed in
general accordance with the ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method
and ASTM D 3017 Results of the compaction tests are summarized on Plates 2 1
through 2 5 of this report
Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from
on site survey stakes where available and or other visible features These locations and
elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods
used Where areas with low test results were noted the areas were reworked until the
specified minimum compaction was obtained and in most cases re tested
