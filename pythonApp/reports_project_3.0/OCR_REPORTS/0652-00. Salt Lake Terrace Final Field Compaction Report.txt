From to periodic inspections
and density tests were made at the subject site This report
summarizes our inspection and testing
As of this date the site has been constructed to final grade
and all areas under our concern has been filled and compacted
adequately Compaction test results for the in place fills
are enclosed in the Compaction Test Summary
During the fill placement operations periodic field density
tests were made in accordance with the American Society for
Testing and Material ASTM Test D 1556 Sand Cone Method
During the course of fill construction various types of on
site and imported soils were utilized These materials were
tested prior to accepting them as fill materials The maximum
soil density and optimum moisture contents were established on
the soil types in accordance with the ASTM D 1557 designation
and the test results are as follows

The embankment fill construction for this site was accomplished
by utilizing on site material cut from the upper western slope
areas and imported material from other Royal Contractor s job
sites These materials were wetted prior to being compacted by
a sheepsfoot roller or rubber tired equipment Prior to fill
placement the original ground surface was under cut to remove
existing vegetation and other deleterious material The fill was
then placed in horizontal lifts and compacted to a minimum of 90
of the maximum density determined in accordance with the above
test methods
Fill placed against the steeper slopes of the western portion of
the project site incorporated key and bench construction into
firm natural mudrock material to provide a bond between the
original ground and the new fill
Utilities were placed primarily in the roadway system of the site
after road areas were brought up to subgrade elevations under
general site grading Utility trench backfill in the road was
under the jurisdiction of the different utility divisions of the
City County of Honolulu and inspection and testing of such
backfill were performed by them Final road pavement section
testing was performed by the City County of Honolulu
Subsequent to the completion of lot grading utility trench back
fill operations within the building pad should be properly back
filled and compacted to match the adjacent compacted subgrade
Such backfilling operations should be performed under the soils
engineer s inspection and tested for required compaction
This office assumes no responsibility for any alterations made
_ to slopes or pads on the subject tract subsequent to the issuance
of this report without our knowledge and written approval
Contractors involved with utility trench retaining wall and footing
backfill should be made aware of the necessity for compaction and
testing
Should you have any questions concerning the above _please do not
hesitate to call us
