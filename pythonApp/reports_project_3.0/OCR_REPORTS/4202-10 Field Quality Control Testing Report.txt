This field quality control testing report summarizes our geotechnical engineering
observations and presents the field density test results performed during the backfilling of
an underground storage tank pit and trench excavations from January 29 through
The pit and excavations were backfilled with import material from
Ameron and Hawaiian Cement The services were provided in general accordance with
our fee proposal dated
During the excavation and backfilling operations for the project various on site and
imported soils were sampled and tested prior to their use in the field The maximum dry
density and optimum moisture content relationships were established in our laboratory in
general accordance with American Society of Testing and Materials ASTM Test
Designation D 1557 Test Method for Laboratory Compaction Characteristics of Soil using
Modified Effort and the test results are summarized on Plate 1 of this report
Our services during the backfilling operations consisted of part time field
observation of the contractor s fill placement and compaction procedures and
performance of field density testing During the backfilling operation periodic field density
g tests were performed in general accordance with the ASTM Test Designation D 2922
Standard Methods for Density and Soil Aggregate In Place by Nuclear Method and
ASTM D 3017 Standard Test Method for Water Content of Soil and Rock In Place by
Nuclear Method Results of the compaction tests are summarized on Plate 3 of this
report
It should be noted that it is not possible to continuously observe all activities
occurring on a construction site especially since our services were provided on an on call
basis Therefore some of the work may have occurred without our observation It should
be recognized that there are variations in the accuracy and statistical repeatability of the
