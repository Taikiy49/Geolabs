PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
Koa Ridge is a master planned development project located in Central Oahu
between Waipio and Mililani Town on the Island of Oahu Hawaii We understand the
development will encompass roughly 770 acres and is planned for residential commercial
educational and recreational uses As part of the off site improvements for the
development a new drainage system will be constructed at the southern tip of the
development The Project Location Map and Site Plan are provided as Plates 1 and 2
respectively
The new drainage system will consist of a 10 foot wide by 8 foot high concrete
box drain measuring about 970 feet in length connecting to about 57 linear feet of open
channel and terminating in a 25 foot long outlet structure at Kipapa Stream

SITE GRADING AND DRAIN LINE INSTALLATION
We observed the site grading and drain line installation work for the Koa Ridge
Off Site Drain Line No 1 project from to The site
grading work consisted of placement and compaction of fills on the order of up to about
20 foot thick to achieve the finished grades for the project Royal Contracting Company
Ltd performed the site grading and drain line installation work During the site grading
operations we obtained samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and
backfill materials and transported them to our laboratory for testing The maximum dry
densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general
accordance with ASTM D1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on
Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above the optimum
moisture content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density
as determined in accordance with ASTM D1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and drain line installation operations periodic field density
tests were performed in general accordance with ASTM D6938 Nuclear Gauge Method
Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 19 of
this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should
be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed
as unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and drain line installation work for
the above project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report
were performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the
following references
