Dear Mr Leong
In accordance with our Blanket Purchase Agreement Soil Rock Testing with the
laboratory tests requested in Call No 6 with the exception of one consolidation test One
of the samples for the consolidation test was unsuitable for the test and your office was
notified Based on the request we have completed the following laboratory tests and are
transmitting the test results to you for your use Preliminary test results have been previously
transmitted to your office
The laboratory tests performed included determination of natural moisture content
modified swell tests Atterberg Limits consolidation unconsolidated undrained triaxial
compression California Bearing Ratio CBR and Modified Proctor compaction tests The
results of the tests performed are summarized in the Summary of Laboratory Test Results
Tables 1 1 and 1 2
Twenty moisture content ASTM D 2216 determinations were performed on selected
soil samples as an aid in the classification and evaluation of soil properties The results of
these tests are presented on the Summary of Laboratory Test Results
Six modified swell tests one inch rings were performed on relatively undisturbed
samples to evaluate the swelling potential of the soils under a surcharge pressure of about
55 pounds per square foot psf These tests were performed on non air dried samples and
one sample was air dried before inundation with water Results of these swell tests are
presented on the Summary of Laboratory Test Results Tables 1 1 and 1 2
Two Atterberg Limits tests ASTM D 4318 were performed on selected samples
of the soils to evaluate the liquid and plastic limits and to aid in soil classification
Results of these tests are summarized on the Summary of Laboratory Test Results
Tables 1 1 and 1 2 Graphic presentation of the test results is provided on Plate A 1

Three consolidation tests ASTM D 2435 were performed on selected soil samples
to evaluate the compressibility characteristics of the soils Two tests were performed on the
natural on site soils and one test was performed on a remolded soil sample from sample
B 5 It should be noted that the two consolidation tests performed on the natural on site soils
swelled upon inundation with water see Plates A 2 and A 4 The test results and
consolidation curves are presented on Plates A 2 through A 4
Eight Unconsolidated Undrained Triaxial Compression tests ASTM D 2850 were
performed to evaluate the undrained shear strength of the clayey soils The tests were
performed on both relatively undisturbed and remolded soil samples The approximate in
situ effective overburden pressure was used as the applied confining pressure for the
relatively undisturbed samples For the remolded sample the applied confining pressure
requested was approximately 0 1 kips per square foot ksf The test results and stress strain
curves are presented on Plates A 5 through A 12
Three Modified Proctor tests ASTM D 1557 were performed on bulk samples to
evaluate the relationship between the moisture content and the dry density of the soils
Results of the tests are presented on Plates A 13 through A 15
Three California Bearing Ratio CBR tests ASTM D 1883 were performed on three
bulk samples to evaluate the strength characteristics for pavement subgrade support
Results of the CBR test are presented on Plates A 16 through A 18
We appreciate the opportunity to provide our laboratory testing services to you on this
project If you have questions or need additional information please contact our office
