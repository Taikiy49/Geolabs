A visit to the site was made on November 17 to observe the progress of the
footing excavations for the loading dock walls at the above referenced project The
following is a summary of our field observations
The footing excavations for the loading dock walls were cut down to near the
proposed finished elevation Hand probing below the excavation disclosed 1 to 2 feet
of soft soils at the areas indicated on the attached plan We recommend that the soft
soils be removed for a depth of 24 inches below the bottom of the footing and replaced
with a layer of geotextile and 3B Fine gravel The geotextile and gravel may be placed
below the water level therefore dewatering is not necessary for this work
The excavated soft soil should be evaluated by the environmental consultant to
determine the proper method of disposal
