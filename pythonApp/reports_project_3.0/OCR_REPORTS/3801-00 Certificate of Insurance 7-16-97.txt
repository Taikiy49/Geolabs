Purpose and Scope
The purpose of our exploration is to obtain an overview of the subsurface
conditions at the project site to develop a soil rock data set for the formulation of
geotechnical engineering recommendations pertinent to the proposed development of the
site In addition our efforts will include attendance of the design charette as well as
providing consultation services during the course of the master plan development
process In order to accomplish this we propose an exploration program consisting of
the following tasks and efforts
1 Background research and review of available in house soils and boring data
in the vicinity of the project site
2 Attendance of the design charette and consultation services during the
master plan development process
3 Mobilization and demobilization of a truck mounted drill rig and operators to
and from the project site
4 Drilling and sampling of about 20 borings at the building locations to depths
from about 20 to 30 feet below the existing ground surface for a total of
about 500 lineal feet of exploration In general approximately 2 to
3 borings will be drilled at each building location depending on the size of
the building footprint
