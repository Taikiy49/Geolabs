Dear Mr Erskine
As requested by R M Towill Corporation this letter presents our recommended
design percolation rate for the leach fields
It is our understanding that to accommodate proper flow of sewage effluent
toward the leach field the leach field is planned to be at an elevation of about 5 feet
Mean Sea Level MSL This will result in a depth of about 7 5 feet Since this is
deeper than our recommended minimum leach field depth of 5 feet and we anticipate
more permeable material with depth we recommend that an average percolation rate of
2 3 minutes per inch be utilized for design
