This technical memorandum provides the results of the settlement monitoring
program performed for the above project As requested we reviewed the surface
settlement gauge readings obtained from the settlement gauges installed at the deep fill
areas of the Kukui ula Parcel project Approximate locations of the settlement monitoring
gauges are provided on the attached Settlement Monitoring Location Plan Plate 1
Based on the settlement gauge readings submitted it appears that the fill
settlements have generally stabilized under the new fills placed within the deeper fill areas
except for Settlement Point Number S 6 on Lot No 20 At Settlement Point Number S 6
we believe that the gauge was installed at a low point where water collected on the pad
where shrinkage cracks were noted Probe depths surrounding the gauge utilizing a steel
T probe measured up to 10 inches of penetration indicated the soils surrounding the
settlement gauge were not compacted to prevent water infiltration around the gauge See
Plate 3 for photographs taken of Settlement Point S 6 on August 7 2023 Based on the
previous observations we believe water has infiltrated the soils surrounding the gauge and
has caused excessive shrinking and swelling of the near surface soils Therefore the
gauge readings should not be relied upon for evaluation of the overall settlement for the
project
Results summarizing each settlement monitoring gauge and associated plots are
presented on the attached Settlement Point Reading Summary Plates 2 1 through 2 14
The following table summarizes the results of the settlement gauge readings
