In accordance with our field inspection and consultations with
we are pleased to submit our comments and pro
posal for earthwork and compaction controls at the existing sub
ject subdivision development
The proposed earthwork will consist of approximately eight feet
of 111 on the lower portion of the development to extend Apuakea
Street The higher portions east of Apuakea Street will be cut
to create several lots
A series of utility trenches will traverse the site and will in
clude a deep trench excavation along Road A
At the time of our visit the major portion of
the earthwork was completed and the contractor was nearing com
pletion of the trench excavations Areas of stockpiled materials
existed throughout the site and several wet soft zones were ob
served in the roadway
We have been requested to perform the necessary field inspections
and testing to certify that the fill was properly placed and com
pacted Since there has been no previous soil investigations for
the present fill it will be necessary to verify that the under
lying materials will provide adequate support for the new fill
The new fill should be keyed into competent soils and compacted
in horizontal lifts to a minimum of 90 of the maximum density
Page 1974
The utility trenches should be backfilled in a similar manner and
compacted to 90 of the maximum dry density
It will therefore be necessary to remove the present fill so
that the underlying soils can be inspected and the filling opera
tions monitored
The following information is an estimate of the soil engineering
inspection costs for earthwork control at the subject subdivision
SOILS ENGINEERING INSPECTION AND TESTING DURING CONSTRUCTION

follows
1 Provide a soils technician to

grubbing of the Apuapu Street Extension and Lots 14
and 15 in accordance with generally accepted earthwork
specifications
b inspect the placement and compaction of future fills
and the removal of any unsuitable materials
c perform the necessary field density tests and labora
tory compaction tests
2 Provide the necessary engineering supervisory inspections
3 Provide your office with a certification report on the com
paction of the fill with the necessary field density test
results
