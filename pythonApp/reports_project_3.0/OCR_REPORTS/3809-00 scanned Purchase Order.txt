2 4 Decontamination
Decontaminate all drill string tools that directly contact subsurface soils prior to breaking ground at each
monitoring well location by steam cleaning tools over a containment trailer
Decontaminate all soil sampler parts and soil sampler liners prior to each sample interval by scraping off any
residual soil washing in a solution of Liquinox or other suitable detergent and tap water followed by three
serial rinses with clean tap water The final rinse water will not contain visible turbidity at any time Final rinse
water will be changed at the first sign of turbidity carry over from previous washes

7 0 Definitions
Standby Time
Standby time is any portion of a normal work day when conditions at the site cause shutdown as approved by
CH2M HILL provided that CH2M HILL has not issued verbally or in writing a Stop Work Order a Notice of
Unsafe Conditions CH2M HILL Form 309 or a Nonconformance Report CH2M HILL Form 316
Down Time
Down time is that time other than standby time during which work could occur but does not Examples of
down time include but are not limited to time when
1 machinery is broken
2 materials or equipment are not available
3 Subcontractor elects not to work
4 CH2M HILL has issued and not withdrawn a Stop Work Order A Notice of Unsafe Conditions or
a Nonconformance Report after the conditions eliciting the Order have been corrected
