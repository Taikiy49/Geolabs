PROJECT CONSIDERATION
The Medical Office Building is located adjacent to the Castle Medical Center at
640 Ulukahiki Street in the Kailua area on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The general
location and vicinity of the project site are shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
We understand that the Medical Office Building is part of the Five Year Master Plan for
the Castle Medical Center
The Medical Office Building MOB is located at the southeast corner of the existing
castle medical center building The MOB is a steel framed structure consisting of three
stories The MOB has plan dimensions of approximately 109 feet wide by 151 feet long
The finished floor elevation is set at approximately 110 feet Mean Sea Level MSL

EARTHWORK OBSERVATION AND TESTING
The site grading operations and utility construction for the Castle Medical Center
Medical Office Building project were performed by Jayar Construction Inc The site
grading consisted of the demolition of existing structures and pavements excavation of the
existing ground subgrade preparation placement and compaction of fills and backfills
utility trench excavation and backfill and pavement construction
During the site grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials
utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing
The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our
laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor
and the test results are summarized on Plate 2 of this report
Periodic field density tests were performed during the subgrade preparation and fill
and backfill operations to confirm our field observations as variations in construction
procedures and soil conditions were noted A total of 47 field density tests including
retests of failed areas were performed using the nuclear probe method in general
accordance with ASTM Test Designations D 2922 and D 3017 In general fill and backfill
materials and the base course material required for the project were generally placed in
near level lifts brought to above the optimum moisture and compacted to a minimum of
90 or 95 percent of the maximum dry density determined in accordance with ASTM Test
Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor
Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 3 1 through
3 6 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations
should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas
assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was
obtained and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and
hand probing
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our professional opinion that the earthwork operations during the site
grading foundation excavation utility installation and pavement construction for the
subject project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were
performed in general accordance with the intent of the project plans and specifications and
our geotechnical engineering recommendations presented in the above referenced report
and subsequent recommendations
