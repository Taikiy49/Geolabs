FOOTING SUBGRADE PREPARATION AND COMPACTION TESTING REPORT 
KAHALA MARKET BY FOODLAND 
KUONO MARKET PLACE 
HONOLULU, OAHU, HAWAII 
Dear Mr. George: 
This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing 
services performed during building footing subgrade preparation for the Kahala Market by 
Foodland, Kuono Market Place project from June 30, 2020 to July 15, 2020 in accordance 
with our fee proposal dated June 19, 2020. 
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS 
The project site is located on the mauka side of Waialae Avenue, east of Hunakai 
Street, and south of Keanu Street in the Honolulu area on the Island of Oahu, Hawaii. The 
project is part of the new commercial development at the site. The new Foodland will be 
constructed within an existing building. New spread footings and slabs-on-grade will be 
installed for the project. The location of the project site is shown on the Project Location Map, 
Plate 1 and the new building structure is shown on the Site Plan, Plate 2. 
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services 
pertaining to the building foundation subgrade preparation for the project site. 
BUILDING FOUNDATION PREPARATION 
The building foundation preparation work for the Kahala Market by Foodland was 
observed by Geolabs, Inc. from June 30, 2020 to July 15, 2020. The foundation preparation 
work was performed by Allied Builders System. Samples of the on-site and imported materials 
utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing.  
The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our 
laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D1557 (Modified Proctor), and the test results 
are summarized on Plate 3.

The fill and backfill materials were moisture-conditioned to above optimum moisture 
content and compacted to a minimum of 95 percent of the maximum dry density as 
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D1557. 
During the foundation preparation work, periodic field density tests were performed in 
general accordance with ASTM D6938 (Nuclear Gauge Method). Results of the compaction 
tests performed are summarized on Plate 4 of this report. These tests were performed on the 
prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site. Test locations and 
elevations were estimated using the foundation plan for the project.  These locations and 
elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used.  
Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction 
was obtained and/or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and 
hand probing. 
Our services consisted of part-time field observation of the contractor’s fill and backfill 
placement, subgrade preparation, and compaction procedures during the above foundation 
subgrade preparation operations. 
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the referenced 
tests, it is our opinion that the foundation subgrade preparation work for the Kahala Market by 
Foodland, Kuono Market Place project, observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered 
by this report, were performed in conformance with the general intent of the following 
references. 
1. 
Chapter 14 of the Revised Ordinances of Honolulu 1990, as amended. 
2. 
Report by Geolabs, Inc. entitled, “Geotechnical Engineering Exploration, 
Waialae Commercial Development, Honolulu, Oahu, Hawaii”, dated 
June 5, 2014. 
LIMITATIONS

project subsequent to our observation and testing period without our knowledge and written 
acknowledgment.
