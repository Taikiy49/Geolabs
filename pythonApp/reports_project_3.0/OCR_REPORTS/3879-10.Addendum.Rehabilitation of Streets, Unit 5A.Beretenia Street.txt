Based on the additional information provided we understand that it is desired to
revise the pavement design recommendations to reflect the updated traffic data
pertaining to the pavement reconstruction areas along the portion of Beretania Street
extending from North King Street to Alapai Street
Based on the updated Pavement Design Manual dated March 2002 prepared by
the State of Hawaii Department of Transportation Highways Division Material Testing
and Research Branch we understand that new pavements for the subject roadway may
be classified as High Volume Urban and Tunnels Therefore the new pavements will
need to be designed for a pavement life of 50 years However a shorter design life of
10 years may be adopted for new pavements used to replace localized and or spot areas
of structural failures The design traffic parameters for Year 2003 provided for our
analyses is presented on Plate 1 of this addendum In addition the projected traffic data
for Year 2013 10 year design life and Year 2053 50 year design life by the traffic
consultant is also presented on Plates 1 and 2 respectively
Based on the two design periods traffic volume and truck traffic distribution
information provided a Traffic Index Tl of 10 and 12 5 have been determined for the
10 year design life and 50 year design life as discussed above Detailed analyses on the
Traffic Index Determination are presented on Plates 3 and 4 of this addendum

Based on our pavement analyses it is our opinion that the following pavement
section may be used for the majority of the pavement reconstruction areas at the subject
roadway
Pavement Reconstruction based on 50 Year Design Life
5 0 Inch Asphaltic Concrete
6 5 Inch Asphalt Concrete Base
6 0 Inch Untreated Permeable Base
6 0 Inch Aggregate Subbase
23 5 Inch Total Pavement Thickness on Compacted Subgrade
In general we understand that the City streets are always subjected to frequent
excavations for the installation of underground utilities and laterals Therefore we believe
that the effectiveness of the permeable base layer could be severely impacted due to the
frequent excavation and replacement of the pavement section due to these future
activities In addition it should be noted that Beretania Street is a heavily traveled
thoroughfare Therefore it is critical that the reconstruction work be performed in a timely
manner and the roadway be re opened for traffic at the end of each work day
Based on the above we believe that a pavement section consisting only of AC over
ACB is highly desirable to facilitate the reconstruction work As a result we have also
provided the following alternate pavement section in case a design exception could be
obtained to eliminate the pavement drainage layer for the subject project
5 0 Inch Asphaltic Concrete
12 0 Inch Asphalt Concrete Base
17 0 Inch Total Pavement Thickness on Compacted Subgrade
In areas where localized spot pavement reconstruction is required it is our
opinion that the following pavement section may be used
Pavement Reconstruction based on 10 Year Design Life
5 0 Inch Asphaltic Concrete
8 5 Inch Asphalt Concrete Base
13 5 Inch Total Pavement Thickness on Compacted Subgrade
Detailed calculations and analyses for the above recommended pavement
sections are presented on Plates 5 through 7 of this addendum
