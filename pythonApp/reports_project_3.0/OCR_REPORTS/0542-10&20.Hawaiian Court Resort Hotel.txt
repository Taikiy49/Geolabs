This report presents a summary of production pile driving
inspections for the above project
A total of 316 16 inch octagonal concrete piles were driven
The enclosed Table 1 summarizes the driving record for each
of the pile installed and any irregularities occurred
All the piles were driven under our inspection and in accordance
with the recommendations contained in the above referenced reports
The Pile No reference system shown in the summary conforms
with the locations indicated on the attached Foundation Plan
for this project
Because of localized loose pockets and thin end bearing layer
some of the piles did not achieve the designed capacity at
the recommended length therefore the following three alter
nate solutions were proposed ce

P 2

until the required capacity was obtained
2 Splice can added with additional section of
pile and continued on with driving until
the required blow count had developed
3 Accepted the reduced pile capacity of the
already driven in place pile and then added
an extra pile tn the pile group to make up
for the deficient bearing capacity
An additional pile No 208A at Column Line E 2 was driven
in accordance with the Structural Engineer s new location
to compensate for the deviation in the pile group
For the tower crane
