PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is at the intersection of Kolowaka Drive and Fort Weaver Road in the
District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The new water line consists of a 24 inch
Concrete Cylinder Pipe CCP about 361 linear feet in length The majority of the new water
line will be encased with a reinforced concrete jacket A Project Location Map and a Site
Plan are shown on Plates 1 and 2 respectively
This report addresses our field observations made during the water line installation for
the project site
WATER LINE INSTALLATION
The water line installation work for the Kolowaka Drive and Fort Weaver Road Water
System Improvements project was observed by Geolabs Inc from to
March 25 2009 The water line work consisted of the installation of about 361 linear feet of
24 inch CCP The trench backfill consisted of Controlled Low Strength Material CLSM
Therefore compaction testing of the trench backfill was not performed The 50 to
150 pounds per square inch psi CLSM was provided by Hawaiian Cement The water line
installation work was performed by Hawaiian Dredging Construction Company The
condition of the trench subgrade was observed by our field representatives Loose soft
subgrade soils encountered were removed to stiff natural ground
