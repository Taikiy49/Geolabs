PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is within the Ewa by Gentry West Development area on the Island of
Oahu Hawaii The proposed sewage pump station site is about 2 000 feet south of the
Sun Terra Subdivision project and near the northern boundary of the Ocean Pointe
Residential Development The pump station building consists of a three level reinforced
concrete and concrete masonry unit structure The ground floor was built at grade at about
Elevation 22 feet Mean Sea Level MSL and the wetwell invert was at about
Elevation 4 68 feet MSL An asphaltic concrete paved parking area is located at the
ground floor level The project location is shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1 and
the new pump station structure is shown on the Site Plan Plate 2
The project site was excavated for the new sewage pump station structure that
extended below the existing ground water level Continuous steel sheet piling were installed
at the perimeter of the structure A tremie plug was constructed below the structure slab
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading and backfill operations for the project site

SITE GRADING AND BACKFILL INSTALLATION
Geolabs Inc observed the site grading and backfill installation work from
to The site grading and backfilling consisted of clearing
and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills up to about 27 feet in thickness to
achieve the design finished grades for the project Hawaiian Dredging Construction
Company HDCC performed the site grading and backfilling work During the site grading
operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials
were obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum dry densities and
optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with
ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the pavement areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of 95 percent
relative density
During the site grading and backfilling operations periodic field density tests were
performed in general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of
the compaction tests performed are summarized on the attached Plates 4 1 through 4 7
These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and
backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating
from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should be considered accurate
only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed as unsatisfactory were
reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field
representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill and backfill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures during the above grading
operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and backfill installation work for the
Ewa Gentry 2 Wastewater Pump Station project observed and tested by Geolabs in the
period covered by this report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the
following references
