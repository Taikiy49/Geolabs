Dear Mr Chun
This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing services
performed during mass grading for the Ewa by Gentry Area 43 project from June 17 to July 22
2010 in accordance with our fee proposal dated June16 2010
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Ewa by Gentry Area 43 site is located on the western side of Fort Weaver Road
and south of Geiger Road in the Ewa District on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project
site is bounded by Area 40 to the east Keaunui Drive to the south and Kapolei Parkway to
the west A Project Location Map and a Site Plan are shown on Plates 1 and 2
respectively
The project involves mass grading approximately 3 acres for a new church
development The mass grading work involved fills up to about 3 feet thick
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services pertaining
to the mass grading operations for the project site
MASS GRADING
The mass grading work for the Area 43 project was observed by Geolabs Inc from
June 17 to The mass grading work consisted of clearing and grubbing and
placement and compaction of fills on the order of up to about 3 feet thick to achieve the design
finished grades for the project Royal Contracting Company Ltd performed the mass grading

work During the mass grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials
utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The
maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in
general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are
summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557
During the mass grading operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM D 6938 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the compaction
tests performed are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 3 These tests were performed on
the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations
and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These
locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the
methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified
minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on
visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the referenced
tests it is our opinion that the mass grading work for the above project observed and tested by
Geolabs in the period covered by this report were performed in conformance with the general
intent of the recommendations in the following references
