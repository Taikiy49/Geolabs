Based on our on going compaction testing services rendered at the
above referenced project site we understand that the Contractor has experienced some
difficulties in meeting the pavement subgrade compaction requirements due to the high
moisture contents of the on site clayey subgrade soils In order to facilitate the pavement
construction for the subject project we have recommended various remedial measures
such as the placement of additional select borrow and geotextile in the pavement section
and or stabilization of the subgrade soils with cement or lime treatment
However we understand that these additional measures would not be
implemented for the subject project due to budgetary constraints In addition it is desired
to evaluate a reduced compaction requirement for the subgrade soils to between 80 and
85 percent relative compaction to facilitate construction Therefore it is desired to
re evaluate the adequacy of the planned pavement section under the existing site
conditions Based on our further review and assessment we believe that the following
planned pavement section may be adequate for the subject project provided a somewhat
reduced pavement life is acceptable
6 0 Inch Aggregate Base Course
12 0 Inch Select Borrow Subbase
24 0 Inch Total Pavement Thickness on Moist Subgrade Baipaiod
