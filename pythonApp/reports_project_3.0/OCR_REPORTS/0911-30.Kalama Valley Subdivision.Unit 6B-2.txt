Subject Results of Adobe Soil Thickness
Exploration
Kalama Valley Subdivision Unit 6B 2
Gentlemen
As requested 3 borings were drilled in the head of the valley
area near the proposed fill daylight area to determine the
existing thickness of the adobe clay soil The boring logs
and boring location plan are attached
The thickness of the adobe Clay directly affects the
required set back distance for lot development We have
previously recommended a lot set back distance from the fill
daylight line of 100 feet in order to maintain a minimum
factor of safety against Slope failure of 1 5 This was
based on the assumption that the adobe thickness is 25
feet The factor of safety decreases with increased adobe
thickness
The purpose of the borings was to verify the adobe thickness
to determine the required lot setback distance
Pindings
The borings encountered cobbles and boulders in an adobe
clay matrix The amount of adobe clay present decreased
with depth and appears to be relatively small below 20 feet
Based on the results of the borings it appears that the
100 foot set back requirement previously recommended is
Suitable
