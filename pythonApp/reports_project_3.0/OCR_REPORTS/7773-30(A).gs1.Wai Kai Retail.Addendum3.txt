Dear Mr. Irvine: 
This addendum to our report entitled “Geotechnical Engineering Report, Wai Kai 
Retail, Ewa Beach, Oahu, Hawaii,” dated July 15, 2019, provides corrosion potential 
information of the coralline soils and groundwater at the project site. 
The coralline materials and groundwater due to the close proximity to the ocean at 
the project site should be considered extremely corrosive.  Corrosion protection should be 
provided to the new structures and foundations.  The project designs should provide 
comprehensive long-term protection against corrosion.  It may be appropriate to consult 
with a professional corrosion engineer to provide recommendations for corrosion 
protection.
