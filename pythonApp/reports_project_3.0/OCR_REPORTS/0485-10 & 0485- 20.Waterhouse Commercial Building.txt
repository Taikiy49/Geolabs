This is a summary of test pile driving and the results of pile
load test of one of the test piles for the above project The
above work was done between December 19 and 23 1975
A total of eight 12 inch square concrete piles were driven at
the project site Table 1 summarizes the driving record for
each test pile Pile driving refusal was attained at about
10 to 14 feet below existing ground surface
The pile at P 10 location was selected for load testing At a
160 ton loading 200 of 80 ton design load the pile settled
0 15 inch After rebound a net settlement of 0 02 inch was
recorded
The load test indicated that the test pile can adequately
support the 80 tons designed load The pile load test results
was tabulated in Table 2
Based on the result of pile load test and additional engineering
analysis it is our opinion that the rest of the production pile
can be casted to the following lengths
Ten 16 feet long piles for the south eastern
portion between column lints N Q
