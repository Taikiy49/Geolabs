ASPHALT PAVING OBSERVATION REPORT
DOLE PLANTATION ASPHALT PAVING REPAIRS
Dear Mr Gaston
This report summarizes the asphalt paving repair observations for the Dole Plantation
Improvement project in Wahiawa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii
The project included the repair of fractured pavement areas placement of asphalt
concrete overlay and placement of asphalt emulsion seal coating The asphalt repairs were
observed by Geolabs Inc at the Dole Plantation project site from
through
The subject site is located on Kamehameha Highway near Plantation Road in
Wahiawa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii as shown on the Site Plan Plate 1
The asphalt paving repair work was performed by Rons Construction Corporation
The asphalt concrete consisted of State Mix V and was provided by Hawaiian Bitumuls
Paving The asphalt concrete was compacted to a minimum relative compaction of 91
percent based on AASHTO T 209 The maximum densities of the asphalt concrete were
provided by Hawaiian Bitumuls Paving and are summarized on Plate 2 of this report
During the overlay paving and backfilling operations periodic field density tests were
performed using a nuclear gauge equipment Results of the compaction tests are
summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 4 of this report
Wet subgrade soils were encountered during the excavation for the repair of a
fractured pavement area within the Handicap Parking Lot area The wet soils encountered
in the Handicap Parking Lot area were over excavated about 20 inches below the existing
ground surface and backfilled with asphalt concrete

At ihe south entrance of the project site the pavement repair area was over
excavated about 40 inches below the existing ground surface and backfilled with asphalt
concrete The relative compaction of the backfill material were less than the minimum
relative compaction of 91 percent We understand that the repair area will be monitored for
settlement and if settlement does occur the contractor will re construct the repair area
Our services consisted of full time field observation of the contractor s asphalt
placement and compaction procedures during the above pavement repair operations
Our test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from
on site features and structures These locations and elevations should be considered
accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Where low test results were
noted the areas were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained except
for the south entrance repair area
Based on the observations of our field representative and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the asphalt paving and backfilling repair work
observed and tested by Geolabs Inc in the period covered by this report were performed
in accordance with the general intent of the recommendations in the project documents
