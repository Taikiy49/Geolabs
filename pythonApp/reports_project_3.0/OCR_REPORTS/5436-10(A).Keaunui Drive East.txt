PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site involves site grading and utility installation for the project The site is
east of Fort Weaver Road south of Area 39 and north of the Hawaii Price Golf Course in the
District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The roadway extension covers a distance of
about 975 linear feet A Project Location Map and a Site Plan are shown on Plates 1 and 2
respectively
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
Geolabs observed the site grading and utility installation work from to
The site grading work consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement
and compaction of fills on the order of about 7 feet thick to achieve the design finished
grades Albert C Kobayashi Inc performed the site grading and utility installation work
During the site grading operations we obtained samples of the on site and imported
materials utilized as fill and backfill materials and transported them to our laboratory for
testing We established the maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents in our

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of 95 percent
relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations we performed periodic field
density tests in general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of
the compaction tests are summarized on the attached Plates 4 1 through 4 12 These tests
were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the
site Test locations and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site
survey stakes These locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the
degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked
until the specified minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field
representative based on visual observation and hand probing
We encountered soft subgrade soils consisting of brown silty clay between about
Station to 11 25 The soft subgrade soils were removed and the excavation was
backfilled with compacted fill material
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests our opinion is that the site grading and utility installation work for the
above referenced project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this
report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the
following references
