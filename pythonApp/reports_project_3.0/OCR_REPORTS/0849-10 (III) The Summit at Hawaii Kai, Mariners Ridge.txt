SITE CONDITIONS
The development is located within the Kaluanui Mariners
Ridge on rising ground above Hawaii Kai The lots under
study are shown on Plate 1 Site Plan The lots were graded
or rough graded to their present conditions between the period
of 1972 and 1974 The majority of the lots were located within
a series of former ravines or drainage channels and were graded
to their present grades and elevations by filling operations
Some of the house foundations in Phase I were partially com
pleted Work on the lots has been halted
Various stockpiles of earth were noted in the vicinity of
Lots 56 thru 62 and Lots 79 thru 81 Most of the lots were
generally graded as shown on the original grading plan Most
of the lots are covered with weeds and small haole koa trees
