Field Report 
Makakilo Quarry Conveyor 5A/6A 
Ewa District, Oahu, Hawaii 
Dear Mr. Nobriga: 
At the request of Mr. Benjamin Rasa of Tapestry Partners Ltd. (TPL), a site visit 
was made on April 8, 2013 to observe the work performed to stabilize the excavation 
and sidewalls for the bottom end of Conveyor 5A/6A. Based on our observations at the 
site, it is our opinion that the work generally conforms to the recommendations 
presented in our letter report dated February 1, 2013. 
The following items were implemented: 
1. 
Loose material present on the existing rock faces was removed and the 
rock faces were covered with shotcrete.  
2. 
The soil slopes were graded to a maximum slope ratio of two horizontal to 
one vertical (2H:1V). 
3. 
The excavation floor was concreted and various foundations were 
stabilized with concrete.
