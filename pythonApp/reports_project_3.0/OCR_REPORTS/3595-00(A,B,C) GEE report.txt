In accordance with your request we are transmitting the proposed boring locations
and a brief description of the work to be performed in connection with the realignments of
portions of Packages A B and C of the Palau Compact Road We will be performing
field exploration for the realignments in the months of April and May
Attached are five sets of portions of available drawings showing the proposed
boring and access trail locations The boring and trail locations will be cleared with UXO
and archaeologist field personnel prior to commencement of the trail clearing We are
planning to drill approximately 13 to 15 borings ranging in depths from 20 to 100 feet with
both truck mounted equipment and portable drilling machinery
The clearing of trails in swamp land conditions and remote areas will be performed
by manual clearing to permit access with the portable drilling machinery A bulldozer will
be utilized where appropriate to permit access from the existing roadway to the boring
sites with truck mounted equipment The trail locations will be coordinated with the
