This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations performed
during the mass grading pad grading utility installation and road construction operations

Project Considerations
The project site is located to the southwest of the end of Elele Street in the
Makakilo area on the Island of Oahu Hawaii as shown on the Project Location Map
Plate 1 The project site is located along the top of a ridge generally extending in a west
to east direction and is bordered by existing residential developments to the east The
location of the project site is shown on the Site Plan Plate 1 The project involves site
grading for 14 multi family building complexes
Deep fills were placed along the southeastern and southwestern areas of the
project site The majority of the northern and center portions of the site were cut Site
grading involved cuts up to 7 feet deep and fills up to 18 feet high relative to the original
existing ground surface to achieve the design grades for the development The tallest
constructed slope has a slope inclination of two horizontal to one vertical 2H 1V and is
about 38 feet in height
Site Grading
The earthwork operations for the project were performed by Royal Contracting
Company At the on set of earthwork areas within the contract grading limits were
generally cleared and grubbed of vegetation and deleterious materials Materials
generated from the clearing operations were disposed of off site Following the clearing
and grubbing operations the areas to be cut were excavated Finish grades were
compacted using a sheepsfoot and or smooth drum vibratory roller

In general on site soil materials generated from the excavation were primarily used
in the deeper fill area The on site fill materials generally consisted of silty and clayey soils
with some gravel The expansion of the on site fill material ranged from low to high
Samples of the fill and backfill materials utilized during construction were obtained and
transported to our office for laboratory testing The maximum dry densities and optimum
moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with American
Society of Testing and Material ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor The
test results are summarized on Plate 3 of this report
In general fill materials consisting of on site material less than 6 inches in size were
placed for the fills and utility trench backfills Field density tests were performed with the
nuclear density gauge ASTM Test Designations D 2922 and D 3017 During the fill and
backfill operations the materials were generally placed in near level lifts brought to near
the optimum moisture and compacted to obtain the specified degree of compaction based
on visual observations and or field density tests Periodic field density tests were
performed during the fill and backfill operations to confirm our field observations as
variations in construction procedures and soil conditions were noted
Imported structural fill was used for the top 3 feet of fill lots The structural fill
material consisted of crushed basalt boulders
