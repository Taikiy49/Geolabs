PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Ewa by Gentry Area 49 project site is south of Geiger Road in the Ewa
District on the Island of Oahu Hawaii A Project Location Map and Site Plan are shown
on Plates 1 and 2 respectively
The project involves the basin excavation and installation of a maintenance ramp
and pedestrian bike path at the Kaloi Gulch basin This report addresses our field
observations and compaction testing services for the basin excavation and maintenance
ramp and pedestrian bike path installation at the project site

BASIN EXCAVATION AND MAINTENANCE RAMP
AND PEDESTRIAN BIKE PATH INSTALLATION
We observed the basin excavation and maintenance ramp and pedestrian bike
path installation work for the Area 49 project from to Royal
Contracting Company performed the basin excavation and ramp and path installation
work During the work we obtained samples of the on site and imported materials utilized
as fill materials and transported them to our laboratory for testing The maximum dry
densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general
accordance with ASTM D1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on
Plate 3
The fill materials were moisture conditioned to above the optimum moisture content
and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as determined in
accordance with ASTM D1557 The aggregate base course fill material was compacted to
a minimum of 95 percent relative density
During the basin excavation and basin excavation and ramp and path installation
work periodic field density tests were performed in general accordance with ASTM D6938
Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on
Plates 4 1 and 4 2 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground
surfaces and on constructed fills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated
by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations
should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas
assessed as unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was
obtained and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and
hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading work for the above referenced
project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were
performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the following
references

grades on the above project subsequent to our observation and testing period without our
knowledge and written acknowledgment
