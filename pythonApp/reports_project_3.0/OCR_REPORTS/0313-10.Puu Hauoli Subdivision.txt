This report presents the findings and conclusions of a preliminary
soils investigation performed on the subject property The pur
pose of this investigation was to evaluate the soils underlying
the site with respect to the planned development to define their
engineering characteristics and to provide preliminary recommenda
tions for grading pavement design and compaction control
The investigation included a geologic reconnaissance of the area
digging test pits identification logging and sampling of the
soils encountered laboratory testing and engineering analysis

PROPOSED DEVELOPMENT
According to the information received from Warren S Unemori
Engineer Land Surveyor the property is to be divided into
91 building pads for single family residences Fill grading will
create a road network and the building sites No cutting is
planned and fills will generally be less than 5 feet The fill
material will be imported from a cinder pit
SITE DESCRIPTION
The proposed housing site and subdivision is located just west
of Kaunakakai Stream and 200 to 400 feet north of Manila Place
in the town of Kaunakakai on Molokai Hawaii See Vicinity Map

The site covered by this investigation consists of approximately

GEOLOGY AND SOILS DESCRIPTION
_ The area of the proposed Puu Hauoli subdivision is located near
the lower edge of a bedrock ridge composed of gently dipping lava
flows These flows are well exposed in a nearly vertical slope
immediately east of the site next to the Kaunakakai Stream
The basalt is weathered at the surface into a residual red and
brown soil mixed with boulders of hard basalt The soil is
very irregular in distribution and varies from 6 to 60 inches
thick

W O 313 10
The soil generally classifies as Clayey SILT or Silty CLAY with
a Unified Soil Classification of CL It is slightly expansive
when remolded The soils are medium stiff and will be capable
of supporting the proposed fills and footings

that the subject site is suited to the proposed construction
insofar as the following recommendations are incorporated in
the design considerations project plans and job specifications
