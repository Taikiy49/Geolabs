Gentlemen
As requested we performed observation and testing of the mass grading
operations at the above referenced project site on a periodic basis from
through This report summarizes our observations and compaction
tests performed at the project site
The subject site was graded for the development of eighteen single family
residences as shown on the attached Site Plan Plate 5 Fills consisting of on site clayey
silts were placed and compacted to a maximum depth of eight 8 feet in consecutive lifts
Periodic observation and testing of the grading operations were performed to check with
compliance with the recommendations and specifications pertaining to the development
The fills were placed in horizontal lifts and compacted to a minimum of 90 of the
maximum dry density as determined by ASTM D 1557 Periodic field density tests were
done in accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear Method and D 1556
Sand Core Method The results of the in place density testing are presented in the
attached Summary of Density Tests Plates 3 through 5
