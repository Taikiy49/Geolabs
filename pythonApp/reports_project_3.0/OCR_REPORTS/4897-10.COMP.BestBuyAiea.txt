PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the Pearl City area on the Island of Oahu Hawaii
The site is located south of Kamehameha Highway and near the intersection of
Kamehameha Highway and Kanuku Street The new retail facility will consist of a
two story main building an adjacent parking area and other related infrastructures In
addition off site improvements will be performed on Kamehameha Highway The location
of the project site is shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1 The site grading work
generally consisted of cuts and fills of up to about 6 feet The grading plan for the site is
shown on the Site Plan Plates 2 1 and 2 2
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The site grading and utility installation work for the Best Buy Aiea project was
observed by Geolabs Inc from March 18 2005 to The site grading
work consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills up to
about 6 feet in thickness to achieve the design finished grades for the project The site
grading and utility backfilling work was performed by Koga Engineering and Construction
Inc During the site grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials
utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing

Hawaii California

The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our
laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test
results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method
Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 14 of
this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations
should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas
assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was
obtained and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and
hand probing
Soft subgrade conditions from previously placed fiber optic trench backfill were
encountered at the deceleration lane area The soft subgrade material was removed and
replaced with compacted fill material
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
