Alternative Recommendation for Foundation Design
for the Proposed New Towers
We believe that deeper spread or continuous wall footings
extending below the fill and bearing on the in situ stiff to very stiff
clayey silt or highly weathered rock can also be used for the
foundation support of the proposed new towers Maximum allowable
bearing pressure of 2000 p s f is recommended
Based on the boring data the footings for the eastern tower should
extend about 4 feet below the existing ground while the footings for the
western one should extend about 3 feet below the existing ground
