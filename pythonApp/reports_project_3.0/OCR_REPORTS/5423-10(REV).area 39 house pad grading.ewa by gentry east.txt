PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the eastern portion of the Ewa by Gentry Residential
Development Area in the District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is
east of Fort Weaver Road south of Area 19 and north of the Hawaii Prince Golf Course
The project involved the site grading of about 9 34 acres of future house pads for a
residential development The site grading work involved fills of generally less than 2 feet
thick A Project Location Map and a Site Plans are shown on Plates 1 and 2 1 thru 2 4
respectively
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for the
house pad grading operations
SITE GRADING
We observed the site grading work for the Area 39 project from to
The site grading work consisted of placement and compaction of fills on
the order of about 2 feet thick to achieve the design finished house pad grades
Albert C Kobayashi Inc performed the site grading During the site grading operations we
obtained samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials
and transported them to our laboratory for testing We established the maximum dry

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture

determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557
During the site grading operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the compaction
tests are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 15 These tests were performed on the
prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations
and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These
locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the
methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified
minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on
visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the

observed and tested in the period covered by this report were performed in conformance with
