This letter report presents the results of our percolation tests performed for the
Individual Wastewater Systems at the project
Project Considerations
The project site is at Lot No 12 of Fairways North subdivision within the Mauna
Kea Beach Resort in the District of South Kohala on the Island of Hawaii As part of the
project we understand individual wastewater systems will be required for the new
residence The State of Hawaii Department of Health requires performance of
percolation tests to evaluate the suitability of the site location for use of individual
wastewater systems Therefore field percolation tests were performed at the proposed
leaching field locations
Percolation Test
Two field percolation tests designated as PT 1 and PT 2 were performed at the
proposed absorption bed areas for the residence A site plan showing the percolation
test locations are provided as Plate 1 The field percolation tests were performed in
general accordance with the State of Hawaii Department of Health s Administrative
Standards to evaluate the permeability of the subsurface soils for disposal of
wastewater effluent
The percolation tests were performed in excavated pit of about 5 feet in depth
from the existing ground surface The tested depth roughly corresponds to about
16 inches below the invert of leach field drain pipes Approximately 1 inch of gravel was

During testing the test pits were filled with water to a depth of about 6 inches

were then recorded to provide data upon which the calculations of the percolation test
was based
Based on the percolation test conducted the percolation rates at PT 1 and PT 2
are about 0 8 and 1 3 minutes per inch respectively at the test elevations The
percolation test results are provided on Plates 2 1 and 2 2 In accordance with the
Manual of Septic Tank Practice prepared by the U S Department of Health
Education and Welfare the selected leach field locations at PT 1 and PT 2 with the
percolation rate obtained at the site would be suitable for the siting of the absorption
system at these locations
