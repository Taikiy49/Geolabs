This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during site grading and utility and roadway construction for the Ewa by
Gentry Area 36 Phase 4 Subdivision project from to August 14 2015 in
accordance with our fee proposal dated May 5 2015
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Ewa by Gentry Area 36 Phase 4 site is east of Fort Weaver Road in the Ewa
District on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site consists of the last phase of the
Area 36 project A Project Location Map and a Site Plan are shown on Plates 1 and 2
respectively
The project site has been previously graded to finished grade for the house pads
A new retaining wall roadway and utilities will be installed for the project
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for
the site grading and utility and roadway construction at the project site

SITE GRADING UTILITY ROADWAY CONSTRUCTION
We observed the site grading and utility and roadway construction work for the
Area 39 Phase 4 project from to August 14 2015 The site grading work
consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills on the order of
up to about 2 feet thick to achieve the finished grades for the project Royal Contracting
performed the site grading utility and roadway construction work During the site grading
operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill
materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum dry
densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general
accordance with ASTM D1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on
Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above the optimum
moisture content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density
as determined in accordance with ASTM D1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility and roadway construction operations periodic
field density tests were performed in general accordance with ASTM D6938 Nuclear
Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 4 1
through 4 4 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces
and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were
estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and
elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used
Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum
compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on visual
observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility and roadway construction
work for the above referenced project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period
covered by this report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the
recommendations in the following references

Geolabs assumes no responsibility for any alterations made to slopes or finished
grades on the subject project subsequent to our observation and testing period without our
knowledge and written acknowledgment
