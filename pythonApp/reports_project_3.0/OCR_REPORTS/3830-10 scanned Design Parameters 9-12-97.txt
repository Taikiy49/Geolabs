to approximately 30 to 35 feet will be needed to install the new drain line Boring B 24
drilled by Ernest K Hirata and Associates Inc on in the vicinity of the
site indicates that the area is underlain by about 5 feet of fill consisting of stiff silty clay

surcharge loads due to traffic etc Please note that the minimum depth of embedment
below excavation should be at least 10 feet deep
