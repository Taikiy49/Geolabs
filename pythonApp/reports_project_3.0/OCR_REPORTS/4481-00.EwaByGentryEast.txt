This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during site grading and roadway and utility construction for the Ewa by
Gentry Area 19 Phase A development from May 10 2000 to May 16 2001 in accordance
with our fee proposal dated May 3 2000
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the eastern portion of the Ewa by Gentry Residential
Development Area in the District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is
located at the corner of Iroqouis Point Road and Road A A Makai as shown on the Project
Location Map Plate 1 The project site encompasses an area of approximately 11 4 acres
and includes a total of 55 residential lots and related access roads The initial 56 lots for the
project was changed to 55 lots in order to accommodate changes to some of the lot sizes
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The site grading and utility installation work for the Area 19 Phase A project was
observed by Geolabs Inc from May 10 2000 to May 16 2001 The site grading work
consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills on the order of
about 2 to 5 feet thick to achieve the design finished grades for the project The site grading
was performed by RHS Lee Inc at Lot Nos 1 to 19 Lot Nos 37 to 56 Roads 19A 19B
19C and 19E by Delta Construction Corporation at Lot Nos 20 to 36 and Road 19D
The utility installation work was performed by Delta Construction Corporation During the site
grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill
materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The maximum dry densities
