PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located at the eastern side of the intersection of Iroquois Point Road
and the future Road AA in the District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii A Project
Location Map is shown on Plate 1 The project site encompasses an area of approximately
4 6 acres and includes a total of 53 residential lots
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
The initial portions of the site grading and utility installation work were observed by Fewell
Geotechnical Engineering Ltd FGE
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The mass grading operations which included clearing and grubbing and the initial
fine grading and utility installation work were observed by FGE from to
A partial grading report by FGE covering this work is attached to this
report The remaining fine grading and utility installation work for the Area 21 Phase A
project were observed by Geolabs Inc from to
The fine grading work consisted of placement and compaction of fills on the order of
about 3 to 4 feet thick to achieve the design finished grades for the project The fine grading
and utility backfilling work were performed by Delta Construction Company Ltd During the

fine grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and
backfill materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the fine grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge
Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 3 1 through
3 10 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should
be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed
to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Subsequent to fine grading Lot Nos 13 through 22 numerous surface cracks were
observed on the pads for these lots These lots were filled with borrow material from
Schofield and generally consisted of brown silty clay The compacted fill below the house
and garage pads and driveways were over excavated to a depth of about 2 feet from
finished subgrade and replaced with compacted backfill consisting of sand and coral gravel
material from Area 28 and Area 13 Phase 9 through 12 projects
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations

In accordance with your request during the period between and December
30 1999 we provided geotechnical monitoring services for the fine grading and utility
installation for the Area 21 Phase A of the Ewa by Gentry East Development in Ewa
Oahu Hawaii Our observations and test results were reported in the previously
transmitted Daily Field Reports 1 through 44 and are summarized in the attached Tables I
and I The general area of Phase A is shown on the attached copy of the Grading Plan
Prior to the start of the grading operations the 4 5 acre site including Lots 1 through 53
and Roads 21A through 21F was cleared and grubbed of the existing surface vegetation
The then exposed subgrade was then recompacted to at least 90 percent relative compaction
as determined by ASTM D1557
Mass grading then commenced using on site low expansion clayey silts and coral gravels
imported coral gravel obtained from the Area 22 Parksite and imported low expansion
clayey silt obtained from Schofield Barracks Lot Nos 1 through 12 were filled to their
approximate finish subgrade FSG levels Approximately 1 to 2 feet of fill was placed
within Lot Nos 13 through 28 raising the existing grade to about 3 feet below the FSG
level Approximately 0 5 to 1 foot of fill was placed and compacted on Lot Nos 29
through 53 raising the natural grade to about 2 5 feet below the FSG level
Sewer drainage and water lines have been installed and backfilled with excavated on site
soils within Roads 21C and 21D and between and in Road 21B The
installed and backfilled The electrical lines within Roads 21A 21B and 21C were also
installed but had not been backfilled as of

to about 1 5 feet below their FSG levels The remainder of Road 21B from about Sta
2 70 to and all of Road 21D had been graded to about 4 0 feet below their FSG
levels No road grading had occurred in Roads 21E and 21F
