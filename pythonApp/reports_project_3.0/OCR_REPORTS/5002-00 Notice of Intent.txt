Dear Director
We wish to inform the Department of our intent to perform trail clearing for the
purpose of conducting subsurface geotechnical soils exploration at the subject site The
project site is located in the Hawaii Kai area on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The site is to
the north of the Kamilonui Agricultural Subdivision 19 44
We wish to emphasize that the trail clearing is to provide access for our personnel
and excavation equipment to the site and is not part of any project grubbing or grading
work Our work will consist of excavating and backfilling test pits at 20 selected locations
to obtain subsurface data pertaining to buried soil conditions
Our soils exploration will occur between March and May 2003 The actual work
trail clearing and test pits is estimated to take about 14 days We estimate up to about
9 000 lineal feet of trail may be cleared The trail will be cleared to allow access for a
track mounted excavator machine only Vehicular traffic is not anticipated Therefore
the trail will be limited to crude construction for excavator entry only
