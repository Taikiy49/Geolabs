We are aware that our CME 45 drill rig has experienced frequent mechanical
problems resulting in delays in the progress of the exploration it appears that the overall
mechanical condition of this rig warrants extensive repair work in our shop While this drill
rig is somewhat undersized compared to the drill rigs specified in Ogden s RFP we have
successfully utilized this rig in the recent past to penetrate and sample to a depth of 150
feet in unconsolidated formations Our operators have informed us that they are
encountering metal objects in the borings at the site This has resulted in our crew having
to abandon several borings then having to start up again at new locations We did not
anticipate encountering buried metal objects in the borings

for this casing utilizing 12 inch diameter solid flight augers with extensions to create 14
inch holes The CME 45 has a cable winch rated for lifting up to 12 000 pounds Even
if the auger flights were completely encased in soil or mud such capacity should be
