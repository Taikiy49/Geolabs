PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Kamakana Street Increment 2 project site is south of Geiger Road in the
Ewa District on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is located between
Essex Road and Kapolei Parkway as shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
General Site Plan Plate 2 and Site Plans Plates 3 1 through 3 4
The new roadway is about 1 892 linear feet long and about 48 feet wide including
concrete curb and gutter on each sides of the roadway A 15 foot wide shoulder that
includes a 10 foot wide planter area and 5 foot wide sidewalk are located on both sides of
the roadway In addition the project includes utility installation along the roadway The
roadway grading work involved fills up to about 3 feet thick
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for
the site grading and utility and roadway construction at the project site

SITE GRADING AND UTILITY ROADWAY CONSTRUCTION
We observed the site grading and utility and roadway construction work for the
Kamakana Street Increment 2 project from March 1 2016 to March 10 2017 The site
grading work consisted of placement and compaction of fills on the order of up to about
3 feet thick to achieve the finished grades for the project Paradigm Construction LLC
performed the site grading and utility and roadway construction work During the site
grading operations we obtained samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as
fill and backfill materials and transported them to our laboratory for testing The maximum
dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general
accordance with ASTM D1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on
Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above the optimum
moisture content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density
as determined in accordance with ASTM D1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility and roadway construction operations periodic
field density tests were performed in general accordance with ASTM D2922
Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on
Plates 5 1 through 5 26 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground
surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations
were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations
and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods
used Areas assessed as unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum
compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on visual
observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility and roadway construction
work for the above referenced project observed and tested by Geolabs Inc in the period
covered by this report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the
recommendations in the following references
