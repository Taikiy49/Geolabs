Dear Mr Wakida
This report presents the results of our geotechnical engineering services
performed during the earthwork and grading operations for the above subject project
from June to November 1994
The subject site is located in the district of Hoolehua on the island of Molokai
Hawaii The project site is bounded by Lihi Pali Avenue to the northeast Molokai High
School to the east Farrington Avenue to the south and Ala Ekolu to the west
The site grading operations at the above referenced site was performed by
Fletcher Pacific Construction and consisted of cuts up to about 6 feet fills up to about
14 feet and installation of utilities The mass grading work consisted of scarifying and
compacting the existing subgrade prior to the placement of fills The available on site
fill materials generally consisted of reddish brown clayey silt and brown clayey silt with
decomposed basalt gravel
During the grading period various on site and borrow soils were tested prior to
use in the field The maximum dry density and optimum moisture content relationships
were established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation
D 1557 Modified Proctor The test results are summarized on Plate 1

Prior to fill placement the existing vegetation was removed and the exposed
subgrades were scarified to a depth of approximately 6 inches brought to near
optimum moisture and compacted to 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 Modified Proctor The fill
material was then placed in near level lifts and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent
of its maximum dry density
During the fill placement operations periodic field density tests were performed
in general accordance with the American Society for Testing and Material ASTM Test
Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests are
summarized on the attachments Plates 2 1 through 2 15 of this report
Our services consisted of periodic field observation of the contractor s fill
placement and compaction procedures and observation of subgrade soil conditions
exposed during the above grading operations
Field density test locations were selected to confirm field observations as
variations in construction procedures and soil conditions were noted Test locations
and elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes
These locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree
implied by the methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until
the specified minimum compaction was obtained
Observations by our field engineer during the mass grading work for the project
indicate that this work was performed in general accordance with the intent of the
recommendations presented in the above referenced soils report
Additional Recommendations
Any future fill placement should be properly compacted under the observation
of a soils engineer This office assumes no responsibility for any alterations made to
slopes or finished grades on the subject project subsequent to our observation and
testing period without our knowledge and written acknowledgement
Lot owners should be advised not to block the surface drainage and to obtain
professional consultation in the planning design and construction of any future
improvements

Limitations
It should be noted that it is not possible to continuously observe all activities
occurring simultaneously on a construction site Therefore there is a possibility that
some work may have occurred without our observation However we believe that the
level of earthwork observation and testing services provided is reasonable in reducing
the geotechnical risks to the project It should also be recognized that there are
variations in the accuracy and statistical repeatability of the tests and methods used to
monitor the earthwork construction and materials Our services were performed in
accordance with generally accepted standards of geotechnical engineering practice no
warranty is expressed or implied Provision of construction monitoring by an engineer
is not insurance nor does it guarantee construction of any type The contractor shall
retain responsibility for the quality of the work for adhering to the plans and
specifications and for repairing defects regardless of when they are found
Closure
We appreciate the opportunity to be of continued service to you on this project
If you have questions or need additional information please contact our office
