PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project is located within the existing Star of the Sea church and school
premises between Malia Street and Kalanianaole Highway in the Kahala area of Honolulu
on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The senior living community site is situated on the Ewa
side of the premises approximately bounded by Malia Street to the north Waialae Nui
Drainage Canal and Kilauea Avenue to the west Kalanianaole Highway to the south and
the existing church and school facility and Kapakahi Drainage Canal to the east The site
is approximately rectangular in shape and encompasses approximately 13 9 acres in area
The senior living community project consists of 358 independent living and assisted
living units comprised of one and two bedroom unit floor plans contained in five and
six story building wings on approximately 7 9 acres in land area A three level
below grade parking structure was built on a portion of the remaining six acres The
approximate location and layout of the new construction are shown on the Site Plan

EARTHWORK AND FOUNDATION PREPARATION
The general contractor for the Kahala Nui Senior Living Community project is
Nordic PCL A Hawaiian Joint Venture The site grading operations foundation
excavation pavement construction and utility installation for the project were performed
by Kiewit Pacific Company The site grading consisted of the demolition of existing
structures and pavements excavation of the existing ground for the underground parking
footing excavation subgrade preparation placement and compaction of fills and backfills
pavement construction and utility trench excavation and backfill
Following the demolition and clearing operations the existing ground was generally
over excavated where necessary to approximately 12 inches below the slab cushion
within the building limits to provide the recommended 12 inch thick non expansive select
granular fill layer The over excavated subgrades were moisture conditioned to at least
2 percent above the optimum moisture content and compacted to at least 90 percent of
the maximum dry density as determined in accordance with ASTM Test Designation
D 1557 Modified Proctor
Based on additional swell test results performed during construction the
non expansive select granular fill layer was increased from 12 inches to 18 inches
underneath some of the building slabs for the project In addition a minimum 18 inch thick
layer of non expansive select granular capping fill was provided under the swimming pool
slab swimming pool deck and the adjoining sidewalks to reduce the potential for slab
cracking due to the swelling of the expansive subgrade soils
During placement of the non expansive select granular fill layer the fill materials
were generally placed in near level lifts brought to above the optimum moisture conteni
and compacted to a minimum of 95 percent of the maximum dry density determined in
accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor During the
foundation excavation and preparation a 6 inch thick layer of non expansive select
