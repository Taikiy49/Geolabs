Cavity Probe Drilling Report
Proposed Child Development Center
Barbers Point Oahu Hawaii
In accordance with our fee proposal dated May 16th we have recently
completed the cavity probe drilling and observation services for the above referenced
project In accordance with your facsimile transmittal dated May 4th the probe holes
were laid out at each column footings and at 10 foot intervals along the perimeter
footings
The cavity probe drilling was performed using a truck mounted drill rig with 4 inch
diameter continuous flight augers A total of 87 probe holes including 17 additional
probes totaling lineal feet of probe drilling was conducted during our cavity
probe drilling program Based on our probe drilling results it appears that cavities
and or loose soils were generally encountered along gridline A at the southwestern
side of the proposed building Where cavities and or voids were encountered or
suspected during the probing operation additional probes were drilled at closer
spacings to help delineate the vertical and lateral extent of the cavity and or void The
approximate locations of the probe holes are shown on the attached Foundation Plan
Plate 1 Results of our cavity probe drilling operations are summarized on Plates 2
and 3
