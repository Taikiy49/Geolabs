During the period between March and July 1976 our soil techni
cian was called in to perform compaction tests on compacted fill
layers This was coordinated with the Maui County job site in
spector Throughout this fill placement period your inspector
was at the job site on a full time basis Therefore it was
under his monitoring that additional fill placement and recom
paction tests were performed whenever test results indicated
inadequate compaction
This office assumes no responsibility for any alterations made
to the slopes or pads on the subject tract subsequent to the
issuance of this report without our knowledge and written appro
val All ramps made through slopes and pads and other areas of
disturbance require the placement of compacted fill to restore

certified unless operations are performed under our inspection
and supervision and tested for required compaction Loose ma
terial cast over certified compacted slopes shall negate our
certification of slope face compaction unless material is removed
