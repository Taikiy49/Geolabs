In accordance with the project specifications one micropile proof test was
conducted on a specified micropile designated as MP15 1 for the Piers 12 and 15
Improvements project at Pier 15 on This letter report presents the results
of our field observations and the test data obtained during the micropile proof test
Based on information provided by Yogi Kwong Engineers 1
was installed to a depth of 88 4 feet below the existing ground surface which corresponds
to a pile tip elevation of about 81 1 feet Mean Lower Low Water MLLW Prior to testing
the top 2 2 feet of the micropile rebar was exposed providing load testing access The
micropile was installed with a bonded length of 64 0 feet and an unbounded length of
22 2 feet The approximate micropile locations are shown on the Micropile Test Location
Plan Plate 1
A pullout test proof test was conducted on the selected on June
18 2015 In general the micropile was loaded to a maximum test load of 38 kips
200 percent of the design load of 19 kips in approximately 4 75 kip load increments The
micropile was held at each load increment for 5 minutes and the maximum test load of
38 kips for 10 minutes to evaluate the creep effect The results of testing are presented on
Plate 2 Micropile Proof Test Data Sheet The testing load versus micropile displacement
is presented on Plate 3 while the creep movement is presented on Plate 4
The micropile was loaded gradually to the design load of 19 kips and the maximum
test load of 38 kips with about 0 049 and 0 114 inches of vertical displacement
respectively Subsequently the micropile was unloaded to zero load in five decrements At
the maximum test load of 38 kips the creep movement between the 1 and 10 minutes
holding period was less than 0 04 inches and the total movement exceeded 80 percent of
