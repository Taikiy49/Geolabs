SUMMARY OF FINDINGS AND RECOMMENDATIONS
Our field exploration generally encountered reddish brown to
brown silty clay to depths of 1 to 6 feet below existing grade
underlain by hard tannish white coral rock
Groundwater was not encountered in the test pits at the time
of our field exploration However the water levels at the
project site may be influenced by tidal influences seasonal
precipitation and storm surge conditions
Our laboratory testing on the silty clay and clay material
revealed a moderate expansion potential To reduce the
potential for distress to slabs on grade resulting from swelling
of the clayey materials we recommend that 12 inches of
non expansive select granular material be used below interior
slabs on grade and 9 inches of non expansive select
granular material be used below exterior slabs on grade The
on site silty coral sand and silty coral gravel may be used as
non expansive select granular fill
In general the proposed structures may be supported on a
shallow foundation system bearing on properly compacted fill
stiff to very stiff silty clay or clay or the underlying hard coral
rock
The text of this report should be referred to for detailed
discussion and grading recommendations

Fill material shall be free of organics and have a maximum particle size of
