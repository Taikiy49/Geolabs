limitations noted at the end of this letter report
Project Considerations
The Kauai Lagoons Resort project site is within the existing Marriott Kauai Lagoons
Resort in the Nawiliwili area of Lihue on the Island of Kauai Hawaii The Subdivision 4
portion of the project generally consisted of site grading for 22 residential lots and
associated infrastructure A General Site Plan is provided on Plate 1
Our observation and testing services during the site work construction of the
project occurred between January 2009 and September 2009 Earthworks Pacific Inc
was the site work contractor for the project

Discussion and Recommendations
Earthwork at the Subdivision 4 site generally consisted of cuts and fills of up to
about 6 to 8 feet in thickness However the majority of the site grading consisted of less
than about 3 to 4 feet of cuts and fills A site plan is provided on Plate 2
Fill and backfill materials generally consisted of excavated materials from
Subdivision 4 and other project sites within Kauai Lagoons In general fill materials
consisted of materials less than 3 inches in largest dimension However rock fragments
of up to about 12 inches in size were placed at least 2 feet below the finished grades
Samples of the on site and imported materials were obtained and transported to our
office for testing where appropriate The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture
contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557
Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were generally moisture conditioned to above
optimum moisture content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum
dry density as determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 Compaction requirement
was generally increased to a minimum of 95 percent of the maximum dry density for the
last lift of fills under pavements
Subgrades in the cut areas were generally scarified and recompacted to a
minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as determined in accordance with
ASTM D 1557
During the earthwork operations periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with ASTM D 2922 and D 3017 Nuclear Gauge Method where
appropriate The compaction test results are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 37
presented herein These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations
should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas
assessed to be unsatisfactory were generally reworked until the specified minimum
compaction was obtained and or accepted by our field representative based on visual
observation and hand probing
Based on the observations of our field representatives it is our professional
opinion that the site work operations for the above mentioned site observed by Geolabs
in the period covered in this report were performed in general conformance with the intent
of the project plans and_ specifications and our geotechnical engineering
recommendations presented in the referenced reports and subsequent
recommendations
It should be noted the above statement of professional opinion is based on the
grades existing at the completion of the site work operations of the project in
