Dear Mr Kanna
This letter provides a summary of work performed to obtain samples of the
marina sediments for testing modeling by JJR marina consultant for the project
Seven borings were drilled in the marina from March 22 to 24 2006 to obtain
samples of the marina sediments The borings were drilled with a skid mounted drill on
a wooden raft Near continuous samples were obtained with modified 2 0 inch standard
split spoon samplers connected to form a 5 foot long sampler The samples were
sealed and sent to JJR via FedEx The logs of the borings are attached along with the
approximate boring locations Additionally a 5 gallon bucket of sediment was obtained
by dragging the bucket along the marina bottom and sent to JJR At the request of JJR
additional two buckets of sediments were obtained and sent via FedEx in June 2006
If you have questions or need additional information please contact our office
Respectfully submitted
