This report summarizes the results of our geotechnical engineering services
performed during the earthwork and grading operations at the above referenced site
from about October 1992 through January 1993 in general accordance with our
proposal dated
The site grading work was performed by Hercules Construction Company Inc
A general layout of the project site is presented on the Site Plan Plate 1 The site
grading operations required consisted of the preparation and stabilization of the lowlying
wetland areas Prior to fill placement the area within the grading limits was cleared
and grubbed which also included scraping of soft and wet materials In some lowlying
areas where equipment clearing and grubbing of soft soils was not feasible due to
water ponding conditions a working platform was first constructed The working
platform was constructed over the soft wet soil deposit by using a geotextile fabric
overlying about one foot of No 3B Fine gravel layer In other areas only a gravel
blanket or coralline surge rock layer of about 2 to 3 feet thick was used to stabilize the
soft ground conditions Imported brown clayey silts with some gravels were
subsequently placed and compacted in lifts by using a sheepsfoot roller and bulldozers
The fills were placed up to near the finish grades at approximately 6 feet Mean Sea
