PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is in Kaneohe within the District of Koolaupoko on the Island of Oahu
Hawaii The development involves the construction of 18 single family homes on a parcel that
is approximately 3 65 acres The location of the project site is shown on the Project Location
Map and Site Plans Plates 1 and 2 1 and 2 2
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services pertaining
to the house grading driveway and utility installation within the house lot areas for the project
site
HOUSE GRADING DRIVEWAYS AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The house grading driveways and utility installation work for the Mahinui by Gentry
project were observed by Geolabs Inc from to The house
grading work consisted of footing excavation and footing and slab on grade subgrade
preparation for the project The driveway and utility installation work consisted of driveway
subgrade preparation and trench backfill of plumbing lines within the house and garage pad
areas respectively The house grading driveway subgrade preparation and utility backfilling
work were performed by Gentry Homes Ltd Samples of the on site and imported materials
utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The
maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory in
general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are
summarized on Plate 3

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 The aggregate base course material below the
driveways was compacted to a minimum of 95 percent relative compaction
During the house grading driveway subgrade preparation and utility backfilling
operations periodic field density tests were performed in general accordance with
ASTM D 6938 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are
summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 3 of this report These tests were performed on the
prepared ground surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and
elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These
locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the
methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified
minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on
visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s backfill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade
soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the referenced
tests it is our opinion that the house grading driveway subgrade preparation and utility
installation work for the above project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered
by this report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations
in the following references

HOUSE CONSTRUCTION CONSIDERATIONS
The finished grades adjacent to the house structures should be sloped to shed water
away from the foundations and to reduce the potential for ponding It is also advised that
gutter systems be installed around the house structures and that the discharge be diverted
away from the foundation and pavement areas Excessive landscape watering within 3 feet of
the foundations and slabs should be avoided Gravel strips adjacent to slabs and foundations
should be avoided Planters next to foundations should be avoided or have concrete bottoms
and drains to reduce water infiltration into the subsoils These drainage requirements are
essential for the proper performance of the house foundations since ponded water could
cause subsurface soil saturation and subsequent loss of strength of the near surface soils
