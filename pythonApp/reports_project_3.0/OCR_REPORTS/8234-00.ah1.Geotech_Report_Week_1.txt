This technical memorandum summarizes our field observations for the
excavation, shoring, sheeting, and dewatering plans for the Kaneohe Bay WWPS No. 3
and Paokano Loop Sewer Improvements project located at Kaneohe Bay WWPS No. 3
between July 12" and 16", 2021, and our recommendations.
Based on our field observations, the trench walls exposed consisted of firm,
stable soil-cement mixture (result of the jet grout column installation). At the time of our
site visits, the trench excavation appeared to be stable with no observable distress in
the trench walls and ground surface. Soldier piles with steel plates and lagging were
also installed along the trench excavation for additional trench support. Based on the
materials excavated within the trench, it appears the subsurface conditions are relatively
consistent to what was presented in the RFP documents at the time this memorandum
was written. We believe that the current construction progress is generally in
conformance with the plans and specifications requirements.
