Dear Mr Chiswick
In accordance with your request we are submitting the following fee proposal to
perform a geotechnical engineering exploration pertaining to the nitrogen stripping water
tank project proposed at the project site This proposal is based on the information
provided and our experience in the vicinity of the project site
Project Considerations
The project site is located within the existing Sea Life Park in the Waimanalo area
on the Island of Oahu Hawaii Based on the information provided it is desired to
construct a nitrogen stripping water tank with a reinforced concrete mat slab foundation
One boring extending to a minimum depth of 20 feet below the existing ground surface
will be drilled at the water tank location for the evaluation of soil bearing pressure and soil
profile for seismic design consideration We have assumed that the proposed boring
location will be accessible by our truck mounted drill rig without the need for site clearing
In addition we understand that a water source will be available for our use at no cost to
us
Purpose and Scope
The purpose of our field exploration program is to obtain an overview of the
surface and subsurface conditions at the site to develop a soil rock data set for the
formulation of geotechnical engineering recommendations pertaining to the foundation
design of the nitrogen stripping water tank In order to accomplish this we propose an
exploration program consisting of the following tasks and work efforts
1 Research and review of available in house soils and boring data in the
vicinity of the project site
2 Coordination of boring stake out and utility clearance at the proposed boring
location by a field engineer geologist from our firm
