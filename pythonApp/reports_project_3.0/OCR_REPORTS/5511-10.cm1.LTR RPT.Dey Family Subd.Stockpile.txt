Geotechnical Engineering Consultation Report 
Stockpile Plan for Lots 14, 19 & 20 
Dey Family Subdivision 
Kailua, Oahu, Hawaii 
Dear Mr. Waters: 
This letter report presents our recommendations for the proposed temporary 
stockpile on Lots 14, 19 and 20 at the above subdivision. This is a supplement to our 
Geotechnical Engineering Exploration report dated September 30, 2005.  
Project Considerations 
The project site is located along Old Kalanianaole Highway on the southern side of 
Kalanianaole Highway in the Kailua/Waimanalo area on the Island of Oahu, Hawaii.  The 
project site is bounded by Waimanalo Ridge towards the south and Mount Olomana 
towards the west.  We understand that the project will involve subdividing the undeveloped 
property (TMK: 4-2-05: 06) into 24 residential agricultural lots ranging from 2 to 8 acres in 
size.  In addition, the initial site development will include mass grading of residential lots, 
installation of roadways, and installation of sewer, water, and electrical utilities.  
The mass grading and infrastructure installation is currently in progress. We 
understand that lot grading is currently being designed. We also understand that excess 
soil from the mass grading and infrastructure installation will be stockpiled for future lot 
grading. 
Stockpile Recommendations 
Prior to placement of the stockpile materials, the stockpile limits should be 
thoroughly cleared and grubbed.  Vegetation, debris, deleterious materials, and other 
unsuitable materials, should be removed and disposed of properly off-site or stockpiled in a 
designated area to reduce the potential for contamination of the stockpile materials.
