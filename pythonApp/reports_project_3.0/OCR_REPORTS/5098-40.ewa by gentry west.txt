PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is on the western side of Fort Weaver Road and south of
Geiger Road in the Ewa District on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is
bounded by Area 12 to the north Fort Weaver Road to the east the future
Keaunui Drive to the south and Kapolei Parkway to the west A Project Location Map
and a Site Plan are shown on Plates 1 and 2 respectively
The project involves site grading twelve house pads for the model home lots The
site grading involved fills of generally less than 2 feet thick In addition the project included
utility and roadway construction
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for
the site grading operations
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
We observed the site grading and utility installation work for the Area 40 Model
Homes from to May 1 2007 The site grading work consisted of
clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills on the order of up to about
2 feet thick to achieve the design finished grades Albert C Kobayashi Inc performed the
site grading and utility installation work During the site grading operations we obtained
samples of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials and
transported them to our laboratory for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum
moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with
ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 3

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility installation operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method
Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 7 of
this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should
be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed
as unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility installation work for the
above project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were
performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the following
references
