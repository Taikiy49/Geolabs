Dear Mr Ogawa
In accordance with our revised fee proposal dated March 31st we have
performed additional laboratory testing and evaluation for the above referenced project
Our previous field exploration at the project site encountered near surface soils ranging
from slightly to highly expansive when subjected to moisture fluctuations In order to
reduce the cost of over excavation of the potentially expansive soils and replacement
with non expansive materials throughout the project site we have recommended that
additional near surface soil samples be obtained to perform swell tests to delineate
potential areas that may be underlain by the highly expansive clayey soils
Our supplemental field exploration consisted of sampling a total of 29 soil
samples from approximately 12 inches below the existing ground surface at the
locations shown on the Site Plans Plates 1 through 3 Bulk samples were also
obtained to evaluate the maximum dry density and California Bearing Ratio values of
the on site soils Results of the laboratory tests are presented on Plates 4 through 10
Based on the laboratory test results and engineering analyses it is our opinion
that the 24 inch non expansive select capping fill recommended below the slabs on
