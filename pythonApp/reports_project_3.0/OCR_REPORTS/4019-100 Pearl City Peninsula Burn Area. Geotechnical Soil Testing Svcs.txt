Dear Mr Ortelli
In accordance with our subcontract for laboratory testing services with IT Corporation
we performed the laboratory tests requested for the above referenced project Transmitted
herewith are the test results to date for your use
IT Corporation transmitted 22 soil samples to our laboratory Laboratory tests
performed to date include the determination of natural Moisture Content Atterberg Limits
Sieve Analysis and Particle Size Distribution
Eleven Grain Size Distribution analyses ASTM D 422 were performed on the
sediment soil samples to evaluate the gradation characteristics and to aid in soil
classification Graphic presentation of the test results is provided on Plates 1 through 3 The
organic content for sample E SD01 was too high for performing the test requested
Therefore this test was not done
Five Moisture Content ASTM D 2216 determinations were performed on the soil
samples as an aid in the classification and evaluation of soil properties The results of these
tests are presented on the Water Content and Unit Weight Work Sheet Plate 4
Three Atterberg Limits tests ASTM D 4318 were performed on the soil samples to
evaluate the liquid and plastic limits and to aid in Unified Soil Classification Graphic
presentation of the test results is provided on Plate 5
Three Sieve analyses ASTM C 117 and C 136 were performed on the soil samples
to evaluate the gradation characteristics and to aid in Unified Soil Classification Graphic
presentation of the test results is provided on Plate 6
