Project Considerations
The project site is generally located on the western side of Poipu Road just south
of Lopaka Paipa Boulevard in Koloa on the Island of Kauai Hawaii Based on the
information provided the project generally consists of mass grading about 20 acres of
vacant land for the future development of a Community Park with parking areas comfort
station and other related infrastructure We understand this project will be constructed in
conjunction with the County of Kauai s planned Koa e Workforce Housing project located
east of the project site across Poipu Road
Based on a conceptual mass grading plan provided we anticipate site grading
consisting of cuts and fills on the order of about 4 feet or less will be required to achieve
the design grades In addition an approximate 3 acre detention basin is planned at the
southern portion of the site for the on site management of stormwater runoff We
anticipate that the site is underlain by residual soils consisting of stiff silty clay underlain
by basalt rock formation
Based on the above we recommend drilling a total of six boreholes to a depth of
about 15 to 20 feet below the existing ground surface Drilling will be performed using a
