A subsurface drainage system such as weepholes and or perforated pipe

The 6 inch base course should be underlain by 12 inches of non expansive

The mat slab foundation should be supported on 12 inches of select borrow
subbase material compacted to at least 95 percent relative compaction

The shallow exterior footings should be embedded below a 1H 1V
imaginary plane extending upward from the bottom of the adjacent retaining
wall footing to avoid surcharging the retaining wall or the retaining wall
should be designed to resist the additional structural loads
