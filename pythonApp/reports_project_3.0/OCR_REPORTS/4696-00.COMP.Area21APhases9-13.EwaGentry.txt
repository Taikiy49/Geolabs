This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during site grading and roadway and utility construction for the Ewa by
Gentry Area 21A Phases 9 to 13 developments from May 30 2001 to in
accordance with our fee proposal dated
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the eastern portion of the Ewa by Gentry
Residential Development Area within the District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii
The project site is located between Iroquois Point Road and Area 21B and includes a total
of 122 residential lots and related access roads A Project Location Map and a Site Plan
are shown on Plates 1 and 2 respectively
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The site grading and utility installation work for the Area 21A Phases 9 to 13
projects were observed by Geolabs Inc from May 30 2001 to The site
grading work consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills on
the order of about 2 to 5 feet thick to achieve the design finished grades for the project
The site grading work was performed by RHS Lee Inc and the utility installation work was
performed by Delta Construction Corporation During the site grading operations samples
of the on site and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained
and transported to our office for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum
moisture contents were established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM
Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on
Plate 3

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill
and backfill materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum
of 95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear
Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates
4 1 through 4 28 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground
surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations
were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations
and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods
used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum
compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on visual
observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility installation work for the
above project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this report were
performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the following
references
