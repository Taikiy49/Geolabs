The piles consisted of 16 inch octagonal precast pre
stressed concrete piles in casting lengths varying from
Eighteen inch diameter holes were drilled with a hydraulic
rotary auger with a Vulcan 010 hammer delivering about
Lbs of energy with each blow The predrilied
holes extended down to a depth of 9 to 15 feet below grade
with a minimum embedment into coral of 1 foot but generally
averaging 1 5 to 3 0 feet The range of penetration below
existing ground surface was 9 0 to 35 0 feet with the major
ity of piles meeting the specification of refusal at a depth
of about 11 0 to Termination of each driven pile
was based on meeting specifications of nefusal 25 blows per
11 inch or 150 blows per 12 i nenes
In addition to the 752 pile locations shown on the plan
three 3 additional piles numbering 137A 91A and 659A were
added Pile No 137A was the location given by the struc
tural engineer to replace Pile No 137 which was broken while
driving Piles 91A and 659A were to replace test piles 20
and 10 respectively which had been driven in the wrong loca
tions
