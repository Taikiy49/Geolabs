PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is located within the eastern portion of the Ewa by Gentry Residential
Development Area in the District of Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project site is
bounded by Area 21 Phase A project on the north and Road AA Makai on the west A
Project Location Map is shown on Plate 1 The project site encompasses an area of
approximately 3 6 acres and includes a total of 49 residential lots and related access roads
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services
pertaining to the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project site
SITE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The site grading and utility installation work for the Area 21 Phase B project was
observed by Geolabs Inc from to The site grading
work consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills on the order
of about 2 to 4 feet thick to achieve the design finished grades for the project The site
grading was performed by RHS Lee Inc and the utility installation work was performed by
Delta Construction Company Ltd During the site grading operations samples of the on site
and imported materials utilized as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported
to our office for testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were
established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557
Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized on Plate 2

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and
backfill materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge
Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on Plates 3 1 through
3 29 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground surfaces and on
constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations were estimated by
pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations and elevations should
be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods used Areas assessed
to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum compaction was obtained
and or approved by our field representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Loose fill and grub materials were encountered within portions of Road 21A and in
the electrical trench excavation from approximately Station 4 25 to Station 5 70 The loose
fill and grub materials were removed by RHS Lee Inc and replaced with compacted fill
material Loose fill material was also encountered within portions of Road 21A from about
Station 2 75 to Station 4 25 The loose fill material was removed down to existing ground
by Delta Construction Co Ltd and replaced with compacted fill material
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil
conditions exposed during the above grading operations
Based on the observations of our field representatives and the results of the
referenced tests it is our opinion that the site grading and utility installation work for the
above referenced project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered by this
report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the recommendations in the
following references
