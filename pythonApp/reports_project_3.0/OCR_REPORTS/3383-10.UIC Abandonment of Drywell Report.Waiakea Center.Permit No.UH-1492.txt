7 Description of injection well abandonment procedures
On January 30th the contractor removed the top covers to Drywells B and D
and prepared the drywells for backfill by cleaning the loose rock and other
unacceptable material from the well bottom The drywells were measured to obtain the
depth and diameter data
On January 30th the specified grout mix was placed in the lower portions of
Drywells B and D extending up to the existing hollow tile well linings A total volume
of about 38 cubic yards of grout was placed The drywell top covers were reinstalled
to protect the work accomplished The grout was allowed to set before continuing with
the backfill procedure
On January 31st the contractor removed the concrete cover and pre existing
hollow tile lining located above the grout backfill from Drywell B The remainder of
the drywell excavation was then backfilled with six inch minus on site rock materials
and compacted in lifts to the existing ground level This completed the backfill of
Drywell B
On February 2nd the contractor removed the concrete cover and pre existing
hollow tile lining located above the grout backfill from Drywell D The remainder of
the drywell excavation was then backfilled with six inch minus on site rock materials
and compacted in lifts to an elevation of 69 feet MSL The drywell backfill was
completed on February 3rd
Geolabs Hawaii was present to observe and document the backfilling procedures
as they progressed The backfilling work was performed by the site contractor
