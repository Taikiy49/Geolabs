The Kaneohe Marine Corps Air Station is located on the
windward side of Oahu in Kaneohe Bay see Location Plan
Plate 1
The site is located in the central portion of the Marine
base at the top of Puu Hawaiiloa an ancient Cinder cone

northeastern portion of the hilltop where the structures
are planned There is an existing TACAN tower and a two
story control building located in the south central portion
of the hill top

crest of the hill at elevation 412 to 420 feet This area
is relatively flat with grass and asphaltic pavement on
the surface The initial Proposed site of the TACAN tower
is along the northeastern Slope at an approximate elevation

approximately 2 to 6 feet thick consisting of medium stiff

ground surface
