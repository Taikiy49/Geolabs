PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The project site is within the Ewa by Gentry Laulani Development area on the
Island of Oahu Hawaii The site is bounded by the Ocean Pointe Development area to the
south and Ewa by Gentry Area 14 and Coral Creek Golf Course to the north A Project
Location Map and a Site Plan are shown on Plates 1 and 2 respectively
The project involves mass grading of the future school site installation of about
1 500 linear feet of 78 inch and 84 inch diameter reinforced concrete drain lines and
about 300 linear feet of 12 inch diameter polyvinyl chloride pvc sewer line In addition
manhole and outlet structures were installed for the project The site grading work will
generally consist of cuts and fills less than 3 feet in height
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for
the mass grading and utility installation operations for the project site
MASS GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
Geolabs Inc observed the mass grading and utility installation work for the
Area 47 Drain and Sewer Lines project from to May 7 2008 The site
grading work consisted of clearing and grubbing and placement and compaction of fills on
the order of up to about 3 feet thick to achieve the design finished grades for the project
Royal Contracting Company Ltd performed the site grading and utility installation work
During the site grading and utility installation operations samples of the on site and

methods used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified
minimum compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on
visual observation and hand probing
The installation of the 12 inch diameter sewer line was stopped at Station No
10 57 Sewer Manhole No 4 instead of Station No 16 70 as shown on the project
drawings We understand the sewer line installation from Station No 10 57 to 16 70 will
be performed at a later date
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations

grades on the project subsequent to our observation and testing period without our
