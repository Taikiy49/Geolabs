Project Considerations
It is desired to abandon two existing off site monitoring wells located at Sheridan
and Makaloa Streets within Keeaumoku Superblock in Honolulu on the island of Oahu
Hawaii The two existing 2 inch diameter monitoring wells were installed in boreholes
approximately 15 feet below the existing ground surface
We understand that traffic control will be required for this work in the public right
of way
Purpose and Scope
The purpose of our work on this project is to provide the well abandonment
services The surface completion must be first removed from around the top of the well
casing The borehole shall then be completely backfilled with an annular seal of portland
cement and a minimum of 3 to 5 percent bentonite The borehole seal shall extend from
the total depth of the borehole to a depth of approximately 1 foot below ground surface
The former well locations of the well shall then be repaired to match the surrounding land
surface
