RECOMMENDATIONS
Building Foundations
Foundations and slabs should be setback a minimum of 6 feet
away from the top of cut or fill slopes to reduce the detri
mental effects of slope creep and erosion
Footings located on slopes should penetrate down to firm soil
in order to develop a minimum horizontal setback distance of
6 feet measured from thé outer edge of the footing foundation
out to the slope face All footing backfill should be properly
compacted to obtain 85 compaction and graded to drain water
away from the footing The soils engineer should inspect the
bottom of all footing excavations prior to concrete placement
