Earthwork Observation and Compaction Testing Report
Ewa Town Center
Dear Mr Agcaoili
This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations performed during
the site grading operations for the buildings and parking lot of the Ewa Town Center from
September 1998 through September 1999
The subject site is located at the northwestern corner of Geiger Road and Fort Weaver
Road in Ewa on the Island of Oahu Hawaii A copy of the site plan is attached for your
reference
The site grading work for the buildings and parking lot was performed by Jayar
Construction Inc The site grading work consisted of minor fills and utility backfills The fill
materials were generally imported from Grace Pacific Quarry The available fill materials
generally consisted of reddish brown silty sand The on site subgrade materials generally
consisted of tannish white silty coral sand and brown clay
The fill and backfill materials were brought to near optimum moisture content and
compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as determined in
accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 The fill and backfill materials within 2 feet from
finish grade was compacted to a minimum of 95 percent of the maximum dry density
During the grading period various on site soils were tested prior to use in the field The
maximum dry density and optimum moisture content relationships were established in our
laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and
the test results are summarized on Plate 2 of this report
During the fill placement operation periodic field density tests were performed in
general accordance with the American Society of Testing and Materials ASTM Test
Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests are
summarized on Plates 3 1 through 3 13 of this report
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil conditions exposed during the
above grading operation
