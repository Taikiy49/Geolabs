Drilled Pier Foundation Installation Observation Report
Proposed Hauula Playground Lighting Project
Hauula Oahu Hawaii
In accordance with our fee proposal dated and our additional
budget request dated we have observed the drilled pier foundation
installation at the above referenced site from April 23rd through May 16th
A total of six drilled piers with a diameter of 36 inches were drilled by Foundation
International Inc at the subject site Because of the presence of loose coralline materials
underlying the site temporary casing was installed to reduce the potential for caving in of
the drilled holes The drilling operations were observed by our field representatives and
the measured drilled depths below the existing ground surface and concrete used
estimated volumes are presented in the following table
