We present this report summarizing our geotechnical engineering observations
performed during the rockfall hazard mitigation work for the Ka iwakiloumoku Hawaiian
Cultural Center project The purpose of our observation services during the mitigation work
was to observe that the project is constructed in general conformance with the contract
documents and to provide engineering consultation to assist in problem resolution during
the rockfall hazard mitigation work
Project Considerations
The project site is located in the area at and near the vicinity of the parking lot for
the Bernice Pauahi Bishop Memorial Chapel at the Kamehameha Schools Kapalama
Campus in the Honolulu area on the Island of Oahu Hawaii The project location and
general vicinity are shown on the Project Location Map Plate 1
The proposed building will be situated on the slope located above the existing
Chapel parking lot Since the project is on sloping ground relatively high cut slopes are
required to level the site for the new building structure Based on the proposed finished
floor elevation the cut slopes may range from about 25 to 40 feet high

This report describes our field observations during the rockfall hazard mitigation

Rockfall Mitigation Work Summary
We observed the rockfall hazard mitigation work along the upper slope of the
Ka iwakiloumoku Hawaiian Cultural Center project on October 12 and 13 2010 Delta
Construction Corporation performed the rockfall hazard mitigation work The major work
tasks observed by Geolabs Inc included the following activities
Boulder sweep
Rock demolition
Boulders were removed using manual labor with the assistance of pry bars and
hand picks The boulders appeared to be about 1 to 2 feet in average diameter with
occasional 3 foot diameter boulders In addition a large excavator with hoe ram
attachment was used to remove fractured rock outcrops that appeared to be unstable
Based on the observations of our field representative it is our opinion that the
rockfall hazard mitigation work for the Ka iwakiloumoku Hawaiian Cultural Center project in
the period covered by this report were performed in conformance with the general intent of
the plans and specifications and our recommendations contained in the above referenced
report
Limitations
It should be noted that it is not possible to continuously observe all activities
occurring simultaneously on a construction site therefore some work may have occurred
without our observation However we believe that the level of construction observation
services provided is reasonable in reducing the geotechnical risks to the project Our
services were performed in accordance with generally accepted standards of geotechnical
engineering practice no warranty is expressed or implied Provision of construction
monitoring by an engineer is not insurance nor does it guarantee construction of any type
The contractor shall retain responsibility for the quality of the work for adhering to the
plans and specifications and for repairing defects regardless of when they are found
Geolabs Inc assumes no responsibility for alterations made to slopes or finished
grades on the project subsequent to our observation period without our knowledge and
written acknowledgment
Rockfall activity may be classified as a natural event which cannot be calculated or
predicted precisely Therefore it is impossible for Geolabs Inc to guarantee absolute
safety for persons and property with scientific engineering methods
