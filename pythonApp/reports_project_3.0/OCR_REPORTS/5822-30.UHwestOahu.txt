Dear Mr Sumida
This letter is in regards to the use of the 3 inch Minus Structural Fill from Grace Pacific
for non expansive select granular fill material for the above project This letter presents the
results of our laboratory testing of the borrow material and recommendations based on our
review of the laboratory test results for the borrow material
Geolabs Inc obtained a soil sample from the stockpile material of 3 inch Minus
Structural Fill at the Grace Pacific Quarry The soil sample obtained from the borrow material
from Grace Pacific generally consisted of reddish brown sandy gravel with some clay
California Bearing Ratio CBR and Atterberg Limits tests were performed on the recovered
sample to obtain an indication on the soil s swelling potential Also proctor test and
gradation were performed to obtain the moisture density relationship and grain size
distribution of the soil The results of the CBR Atterberg Limits gradation and proctor tests
are presented on Plate 1
Based on our review of the laboratory test results we believe the 3 inch Structural Fill
material from Grace Pacific Quarry may be used as non expansive select granular fill
material for the project provided the borrow material do not differ significantly from the
sample tested
