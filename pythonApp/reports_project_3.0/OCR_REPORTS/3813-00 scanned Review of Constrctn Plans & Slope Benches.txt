Subject Slope Benches
Hawaiian Memorial Park Cemetery Expansion
Kaneohe Oahu Hawaii
Reference Report by Geolabs Hawaii dated
entitled Geotechnical Engineering Exploration
Proposed Hawaiian Memorial Park Expansion
Kaneohe Oahu Hawaii
Dear Mr Inouye
An embankment fill slope up to about 40 feet high is planned for the development It
is our opinion that a slope bench is not required for this slope based on the following reasons
Surface runoff at the top of the slope will be contained by the pavement and
carried down the slope by a lined swale Therefore only the water that falls
directly on the slope face will flow down the slope
Installation of a slope bench would increase the area of the slope face thereby
increasing the amount of water that falls on the slope face
An erosion mat will be installed on the slope face to reduce water velocity and
erosion
