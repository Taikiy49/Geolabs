FINE GRADING AND UTILITY INSTALLATION
The fine grading and utility installation work for the reconfigured lots of the Area 13
project were observed by Geolabs Inc from to The fine
grading work consisted of placement and compaction of fills on the order of about 1 foot
thick to achieve the design finished grades for the project The fine grading and utility
backfilling work were performed by Delta Construction Company Ltd and Gentry Builders
During the fine grading operations samples of the on site and imported materials utilized
as fill and backfill materials were obtained and transported to our office for testing The
maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in our laboratory
in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor and the
test results are summarized on Plate 3
The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM Test Method D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill
and backfill materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum
of 95 percent relative density
During the fine grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear
Gauge Method Results of the compaction tests performed are summarized on
Plates 4 1 through 4 5 of this report These tests were performed on the prepared ground
surfaces and on constructed fills and backfills at the site Test locations and elevations
were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations
and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the methods
used Areas assessed to be unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified minimum
compaction was obtained and or approved by our field representative based on visual
observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of part time field observation of the contractor s fill
placement subgrade preparation and compaction procedures and confirmation of
subgrade soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations
In July 2002 Lot Nos 33 and 34 were reconfigured again Additional compaction
tests were taken on subgrade fill material
