This report summarizes our geotechnical engineering observations and testing
services performed during construction for the Mehana at Kapolei Arterial Roadway
System Phase 2 Kapolei Oahu Hawaii from May 17 2010 to in
accordance with our fee proposal dated March 16 2010
PROJECT CONSIDERATIONS
The Mehana at Kapolei development site is bordered by a drainage easement and
Roosevelt Avenue to the south Ft Barrette Road to the east and the future
James Campbell Estate residential development to the north and west The Arterial
Roadway System Phase 2 is on the eastern portion of the Mehana at Kapolei
development and includes Roads B M and N The layout of the roads is shown on the
Phasing Plan Plate 1 and on the Grading and Erosion Control Plan Plate 2
The mass grading of this site was implemented from October 2006 to
November 2007 and generally consisted of fills up to about 8 feet
This report addresses our field observations and compaction testing services for
the site grading operations and infrastructure installation for the project
SITE GRADING
The site grading consisted primarily of cuts and fills up to about 1 foot At the onset
of grading the area was cleared and grubbed prior to filling Paradigm Construction
performed the site grading During the site grading operations samples of the imported
materials utilized as fill materials were obtained and transported to our laboratory for
testing The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were established in
our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM D 1557 Modified Proctor and the test
results are summarized on Plate 3

The fill and backfill materials were moisture conditioned to above optimum moisture
content and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with ASTM D 1557 In the roadway areas the fill and backfill
materials within 2 feet of the finished subgrade were compacted to a minimum of
95 percent relative density
During the site grading and utility backfilling operations periodic field density tests
were performed in general accordance with ASTM D 6938 Nuclear Gauge Method
Compaction test results are summarized on Plates 4 1 through 4 22 These tests were
performed on compacted surfaces of constructed fills and backfills Test locations and
elevations were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These
locations and elevations should be considered accurate only to the degree implied by the
methods used Areas assessed as unsatisfactory were reworked until the specified
minimum compaction requirement were obtained and or approved by our field
representative based on visual observation and hand probing
Our services consisted of full time and part time field observation of the contractor s
fill placement subgrade preparation compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade
soil conditions exposed during the above grading operations

is our opinion the site grading and utility installation work for the Mehana at Kapolei Arterial
Roadway System Phase 2 project observed and tested by Geolabs in the period covered
by this report were performed in conformance with the general intent of the
