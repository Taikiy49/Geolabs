Prior to receiving fill the existing ground surface was
grubbed to remove the surface vegetation top soil and
previously placed loose stockpile fills The exposed
scarified ground surface was then moisture conditioned
and compacted to a minimum of 85 of its maximum density

the densities from wet to dry

RECOMMENDATIONS
Building Foundations
Because of the downhill creep effect of soils on a slope
building should generally be placed a minimum of 5 feet from
the tops of slopes
Slab on grade should have deep perimeter footings extending
down about 12 inches below the final adjacent finish grade
due to slightly expansive soil conditions at this project area
Major retaining walls on fill slope should be avoided unless
designed by an engineer and inspected by a soils engineer
to be sure that adequate bearing has been obtained
Site Regrading
Site regrading to accommodate house construction particularly
along the upper western slope area Lots
and 33 should be done under the guidance of a soils engineer
This is to avoid undermining the general stability of the
upper steep slope section
This office assumes no responsibility for any alterations made
to slopes or building pads on the subject tract subsequent to
the issuance of this report without our knowledge and written
approval
