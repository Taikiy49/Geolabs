This report summarizes the results of our geotechnical engineering services
performed during the earthwork and grading operations for the road subgrade utilities
and lot pads at the above referenced project from August 1991 through March 1993
The subject site is located between Fort Weaver Road and the proposed North South
Road to the south of Geiger Road and to the north of a sugar cane field within the Ewa
by Gentry Development in the district of Ewa on the island of Oahu Hawaii The project
site consists of Area F 30 through F 33 as designated in the mass grading plans for the
development
The mass grading work was performed by RHS Lee iInc and the infrastructure
grading work was performed by Oahu Construction The mass grading work generally
consisted of scarifying and compacting the existing subgrade and placing up to about two
2 to six 6 feet of new fills The on site fill materials generally consisted of tannish

Prior to fill placement the existing vegetation was removed and the exposed
subgrades were scarified to a depth of approximately six inches 6 brought to near
optimum moisture content and compacted to 90 percent of the maximum dry density as
determined in accordance with American Society of Testing and Material ASTM Test
Designation D 1557 Modified Proctor The new fill material was then placed in near level
lifts and compacted to a minimum of 90 percent of its maximum dry density
During the fill placement operations periodic field density tests were taken in
general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 2922 Nuclear Gauge Method The
results of the compaction tests are summarized in the attachments of this report
Our services consisted of full time field observation of the contractor s fill placement
and compaction procedures and confirmation of subgrade soil conditions exposed during
the above grading operations
During the grading period various on site and borrow soils were tested prior to
their use in the field The maximum dry densities and optimum moisture contents were
established in our laboratory in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1557
Modified Proctor and the test results are summarized in the attachments of this report
Field density test locations were selected to confirm field observations as variations
in construction procedures and soil conditions were noted Test locations and elevations
were estimated by pacing and interpolating from on site survey stakes These locations
