In accordance with the project specification proof tests were conducted on
production micropiles designated as Pile and 3 for the Waikapoki WWPS
Force Main Replacement project
The micropiles tested consisted of 5 5 inch outside diameter OD steel micropiles
with wall thicknesses of 0 476 inches We understand that the bonded sections of the
production micropiles were extended below the bottom of the permanent casings into the
underlying cobble and boulder layer Based on information provided by your office the
unbonded length of each micropile is presented in the attached Final Pull Out Load Test
Progress Summary presented on Plate A
The station number or location of each
micropile is presented in the attached Pull Out Load Test Data Sheets Plates 1 1 through
3 1
As required by the project specifications each micropile except Pile No 9A was
loaded in tension in general accordance with Section 209 Paragraph 3 11 of the
specifications
A compressive pile load test was performed on Pile No 9A as a
pre production micropile in general accordance with ASTM Test Designation D 1143
Standard Load Test Method as described in Paragraph 5 1 of the test method presented
in the specifications for the project The other pile load test results were presented in our
previous reports and addendum
Based on the project requirements the maximum test load on the micropile was
58 5 kips In general the micropile was loaded to the maximum test load of 58 5 kips in
accordance with the following load schedule 2 2 kips 11 kips 22 kips 33 kips 44 kips
52 8 kips and 58 5 kips Each load increment was held just long enough to measure the
micropile head displacement but not longer than 1 minute
The maximum load of
58 5 kips was held for 10 minutes for each micropile After holding the micropile at the
maximum test load the micropile was unloaded
In accordance with specifications Section 209 Paragraph 3 11 theoretical elastic
elongation of unbonded length of the test pile was estimated in each Proof Test as follows
